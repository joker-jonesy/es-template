The Challenge - UKIT Beginner: Walk the Line

The Challenge

Your challenge is to build a robotic car that is able to move forward using the UKIT Beginner kit. You will use your measuring skills to measure the distances your robot car travels and then you will create a line plot of your data.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN measure whole, half and quarter numbers.
  • I CAN share data by creating a line plot.
  • I CAN build a robotic car using the UKIT Beginner Kit.
  • I CAN use programming language to code a robotic car to drive forward.

Stuff I'll Need

  • UKIT Beginner Kit 
  • Computer or Tablet  
  • UKIT EDU App (downloaded)
  • Ruler
