Comic Life: Storytelling

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to pick a super power and create a comic strip about your heroic adventures using Comic Life.

As super heroes, you will use your talents and powers to help people or to solve a problem.

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about the elements of a story.
  • Students will learn about the importance of image sequencing in a comic story.

Stuff You'll Need

  • Comic Life software
  • Mac: In your dock
  • PC: All Applications folder
