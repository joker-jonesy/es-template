Do It!

Getting Started 

The Skoog has 5 colors on each side of the cube except for the bottom. Each color represents a different note. 

To turn on the Skoog hold down the small black button on the bottom of the Skoog until a green battery light illuminates and the Bluetooth icon flashes in the display on the red side.

     

Download and Open the Skoog App

Skoog requires an iPad or an Apple device to operate. Open the Skoog app in Digital Communications folder on your iPad or download the free Skoog app onto your Apple device. In the app click the Skoog icon in the top left corner and search for the Skoog device.

Connect to the Skoog Device 

  • Step 1: Open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad.
  • Step 2: Click on Bluetooth.
  • Step 3: Find your Skoog on the list of “My Devices.”
  • Step 4: Click the (i) logo for more information.
  • Step 5: Click “Forget This Device.”
  • Step 6: Reconnect your Skoog using the Skoog app.
  • When prompted to "Pair" the device click "Cancel" instead of "Pair." Do this every time you connect.
  • Select the Skoog to connect. The Skoog will calibrate then you are ready to play music.

         

To change the musical instrument, click the flute on the menu bar on the top.

To change the notes and scales clink the music note. The default is the C-Major pentatonic scale. If you want to learn more about pentatonic scales, go back to the What You Should Know section. 

             

Play a Song

To play a song click the Songbook icon in the menu bar at the top of the app. Scroll down and select Old MacDonald from the list. Follow the colored pattern using your Skoog to play the notes. What do you notice about the music notes? How are the 5 notes represented? Try to sing along while you play. Pick a different song to play. Try changing the instrument. 

           

Turn Off Your Skoog

It's important to turn off your Skoog when you are finished to save battery life. To turn off your Skoog, hold down the button on the bottom of the Skoog until a red battery light flashes then let go of the button.
