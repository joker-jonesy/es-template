Extend Yourself - Snap Circuits Electricity: Electrical Circuits and Number Patterns

  • Pick another project from the Snap Circuits book.
  • Do you have any lights in your house that are controlled by more than one switch? How do those circuits work? Explore “three-way switches” online to learn more.
  • Go online and learn more about electricity. Is there a difference between the electricity that comes out of a wall outlet and the electricity that comes out of a battery?
  • Check out the Snap Circuits Showcase to see what other kids have done. Design and submit your own circuit.
  • Use different-sized snaps to find new number patterns. For example, what pattern can you identify if you add a 3-snap wire and a 2-snap wire each time? Are the numbers all odd, all even, or do they alternate? Make a table to show the multiplication pattern. Note that you may need to change your table’s headers. For example, you can use the following headers for the example table: Number of 3-Snap and 2-Snap Pairs; X 5; Total Snaps.
