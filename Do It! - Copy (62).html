Do It!

The city has some serious problems to solve, and it needs your help!

  1. The river has been overflowing and flooding homes. We need to control the water level.
  2. A hurricane damaged part of the city. We need to deliver supplies to the residents.
  3. Some people are not recycling because it takes too much time to sort. We need a way to make recycling quicker and easier so more people will do it.

Your challenge is to come up with a solution to one of these problems, and make a model to show how your solution works.

Lego WeDo 2.0

Open the WeDo 2.0 app. Watch the video to learn how to use WeDo 2.0.

Helping Nature with Lego WeDo 2.0

Follow the steps below to start a new project. 

1. Click on the book to start a new project. 

2. Click on the books in the toolbar and select 6,7, or 8 under Guided Projects. 

3. Move forward or back through the activity using the arrows on the top of the page. 

4. Do your best to answer the questions using the documentation tool or your ePortfolio. If you do not know the answers, write how you could find the answer. 

5. When it is time to build, use the arrows at the bottom to go step-by-step.
