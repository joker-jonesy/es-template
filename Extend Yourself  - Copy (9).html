Extend Yourself

Explore Gears Some More

  • Build one of the other gear machines in your K'NEX collection. How does this gear machine work differently than the one you built? 
  • Try building a different type of simple machine like a wheel and axle, inclined plane, pulley, or lever. (You may need to use one of the other K'NEX STEM kits.) How could you combine two simple machines into a complex machine that can do even more?
  • Go on a gear scavenger hunt. Pick a room in your house like the kitchen or garage. How many gears can you find? Can you recreate one of those machines with your K'NEX STEM Gear kit?
