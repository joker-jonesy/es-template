Get Started

Start by doing the 7 Direct Starters activities. Once you complete them all, design your own 3D object. If you have a 3D printer, print the object you design. 

Complete the Direct Starters:

STEP 1: Sign in to Tinkercad.com

STEP 2: Click on Learn

STEP 3: Complete the Direct Starters to learn the basics of Tinkercad.

Tinker Tips

STEP 1: You can rename your design by clicking on the title at the top left.

STEP 2: Double check sizes.

Tinkercad measures in millimeters.

In Tinkercad, the cube below is only 20 mm on each side. One side of this cube will be smaller than a quarter.

STEP 3: Check all sides. Use the navigation tool to pan, zoom, and spin your objects. Make sure your objects are where you want them to be. What would happen if you tried to print the object below?
