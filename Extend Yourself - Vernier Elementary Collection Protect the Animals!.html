How Quickly Can Temperature Change?

Many people wonder how quickly ice melts or how long it takes boiling water to cool. While these have many factors, like the material the ice or water is in, the temperature around the cups, and even where the cups are sitting, it can be a fun experiment to try.

  1. You need two thermometers placed in two different cups.
  2. Fill one cup with boiling water and one with an ice cube.
  3. Take the initial temperature, then check the temperature again every 20 minutes. Or, you can use the probe the entire time to continuously track the temperatures.
  4. Once the ice has completely melted, stop collecting data.
  5. Analyze your data.
    • What was the highest temperature of each?
    • What was the lowest temperature of each?
    • What surprised you the most about the data you collected?
    • How long did it take you to complete the experiment?
    • What was your environment like surrounding the cups?
