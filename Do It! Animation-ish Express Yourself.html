Do It!  Animation-ish: Animate Word Problems

 The Challenge

Your challenge is to use Animation-ish (Flipbook-ish) to solve and animate two-step word problems.

 Project Steps

  1. Select a Two-Step Word Problem

  2. Solve Two-Step Word Problem

  3. Animate the Two-Step Word Problem

Select a Two-Step Word Problem

You will use Flipbook-ish to animate the solution (answer) to a two-step word problem. Start by selecting one of the word problems below to solve and animate. 

  • You are reading a great book. The book has 378 pages. You read 125 pages on Saturday and 96 pages on Sunday. How many pages do you have left to read?
  • Your family purchased 6 cartons of eggs. Each carton has 12 eggs. Your family decided to give 3 cartons to a family in need of food. How many eggs does your family have after sharing with another family?
  • Carmen made cupcakes for her friends. She baked 4 different flavors, and she made 6 of each flavor. She shared the cupcakes equally with 3 friends. How many cupcakes did each friend get?

Solve the 2-Step Word Problem

  1. Read the number story. What is the number story asking you to find?
  2. Find the hidden question. What information do you need before you can find what the number story is asking you to find?
  3. Write an equation to represent the hidden question. What numbers do you need? What operation symbol do you need (+, -, ×, ÷)? Why?
  4. Find the answer to the hidden question. That answer is the first step in the two-step word problem.
  5. Use the answer from the hidden question to write an equation that represents step 2 of the word problem.
  6. Use the answer from the hidden questions to solve step 2 of the word problem. What numbers do you need? What operation symbol do you need (+, -, ×, ÷)? Why?

Animate the Two-Step Word Problem

  1. Start the Animation-ish software. If you don't have the program installed on your computer, go to the web version and use your username and class code provided by your facilitator to sign into Animation-ish.
  2. Go to: FableVision Learning to watch the lessons.
  3. Watch Lesson 2 to learn how to make frames in FlipBook-ish.
  4. Go back to the Animation-ish application and select FlipBook-ish and the workspace will open.
  5. Use what you learned in the video to illustrate the solution to the problem and the process you used to find the solution.
