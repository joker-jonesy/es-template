The Challenge - Makey Makey: Music and the Coordinate Plane

The Challenge

Your challenge is to make a programmable circuit that produces sound or music using Makey Makey software and objects that conduct electricity.

This is your opportunity to play the piano on a banana or to make the computer ring a cowbell. There is a project idea for everyone. You just have to use your creativity to find materials and program your software to understand your commands.

Then, explore how you can use your sounds to create patterns, and how these patterns make ordered pairs on a coordinate plane.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN test objects for their ability to conduct electricity.
  • I CAN construct simple circuits.
  • I CAN program a circuit using Scratch block coding.
  • I CAN make patterns that follow a rule.
  • I CAN identify relationships between corresponding terms from patterns.
  • I CAN form ordered pairs from the terms of the two patterns.
  • I CAN graph ordered pairs on a coordinate plane.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Makey Makey Classic or Deluxe Collection
  • Makey Makey Accessory Collection 
  • Graphing paper for coordinate plane OR online coordinate plane using PDF Filler
