Do It! Lux Blox: Build and Measure 3D Shapes

 The Challenge

Your challenge is to build a 2D shape and measure its surface area by counting unit squares using Lux Blox building blocks.

 Project Steps

  1. Build Your Polygon

  2. Measure the Perimeter of Your 2D Shape

  3. Measure the Surface Area of Your 2D Shape

Build Your Polygon

Start by building a regular polygon or 2D shape using the Lux Squares.

Measure the Perimeter of Your 2D Shape

The perimeter is the outline of a 2D shape.

When you measure the perimeter of a polygon or 2D shape, you have to calculate how long (length) and tall (height) all the sides of the shape are. If you add all the sides of a regular polygon together, you get the perimeter.

Measure the Surface Area of Your 2D Shape

You will need to model the area of your shape.

Steps:

  1. Determine how many unit squares are in the shape. To do this, you can count how many unit squares or Lux Blox are in your shape.
  2. Use repeated addition to check the area you found in the step.
  3. Use multiplication to find the area of your shape.
  4. Identify the non-standard measurement used to determine the area of your shape.

When you have determined your area, make sure you also label the units. Area is always squared in a 2D shape. For example, the area of the shape below is 20 units².
