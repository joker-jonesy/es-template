Do It!

Your challenge is to create a public service announcement video using Pixie. 

Safety Video Challenge

The first step is to decide what your video will be about. Click on the link to visit the Safe Kids Worldwide  website. Safe Kids Worldwide has lots of great ideas for staying safe. Browse the different risks (see left hand column) and pick a topic that interests you.

What to Include

Tips

The Hard Facts tell you why the topic is important.
Top Tips has great safety ideas for your video.
Learn More has links to lots of other great information on the topic. 

Explain how you chose your topic and why it is important to you in your presentation.  

Write a Script

Once you have selected your topic, the next step is to write your script. Think carefully about what you want to say. Be sure to think about your audience; your message will probably be different if you are talking to kids or grown-ups. Be sure your script clearly communicates your topic – why it is important and how to be safe. You can write your script on the computer or on paper.

Edit Your Video

See the What You Should Know section to find out how to get help with any of these features.

Create Images

Launch Pixie and create the images you will need for your safety video. As you create your images, you may find that you need to change your script a little as you go.

Record Sound

Record the sound for each slide in your video. Remember you can download instructions on recording sounds.

Add Transitions and Export Video

When you have finished recording your script, choose your slide transitions. Then click the Project button and select Export to create your video. You can insert your video into your slide presentation to show your classmates.
