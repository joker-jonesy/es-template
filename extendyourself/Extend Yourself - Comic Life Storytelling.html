Extend Yourself - Comic Life: Illustrating Problem and Solution

Communicating with Comics

  • Learn more about a comic author like Dav Pilkey the author of Captain Underpants. Research the author. You can read more about how Dav Pilkey became an author by reading his comic strip biography.  
  • Use Comic Life to complete a homework assignment. (E.g. Show the steps for solving a math problem.)
  • Use Comic Life to write the biography (life story) of a famous person or a member of your family.
  • Are you running for class president, secretary, or treasurer? Use Comic Life to create a poster to win the election.
  • Show your ability to learn a new language. Write a comic strip using a language other than English.

Mass Production

Think of an area of mass production you are interested in: automobiles, food, etc. Set up a multi-step math word problem that relates to the industry and create a comic for your multi-step math word problem. Look up details about that industry to help you create an interesting word problem. For example, maybe to find the total number of cars produced in a month. Or you may want to know how many parts can be manufactured in a week.
