Extend Yourself - Animation-ish: Animate Word Problems

 Real World Animation

Think of something that people do everyday that takes more than one step. Some examples are washing the dishes, brushing teeth, or locking the door. Can you teach or animate daily activities? In this extension you can choose. Do you want to teach someone how to do a daily activity or animate it using Flipbookish? It's your choice: teach, animate, or do both.

 Use What You Learned to Improve

You learned a lot while animating a two-step word problem. How can you use what you learned about animation and problem solving to make an even better animation? Here is your chance. Make up your own two-step word problem and animate it using Animation-ish.
