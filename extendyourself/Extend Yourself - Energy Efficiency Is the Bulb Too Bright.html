Extend Yourself - Vernier Elementary Energy Efficiency: Save Energy at School

Research

Decide if it is important to light classrooms with specific lights. Do you think lighting that is too bright or too dim can affect students' learning? What kind of light helps you focus best?

Measure More Light Levels

Measure the lighting in other areas of the school. Design an additional bar graph to share with a school leader. Think about whether or not there needs to be a change in lighting within the school.

Create a Survey

One way that people collect data is by using a survey. Watch the following YouTube video to learn more about surveys, called "Taking a Survey".  Make a survey about the type of light bulbs your friends and family use. Make a bar graph using the results of your survey.
