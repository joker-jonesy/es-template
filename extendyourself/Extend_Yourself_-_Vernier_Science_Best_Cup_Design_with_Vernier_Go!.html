 My Favorite Activities - How Much Time?

It is helpful to know how long it will take you to do certain activities. We need to know when to get up in the morning so we have time to take a shower, brush our teeth, get dressed, and eat breakfast. We can time different activities to help us plan better. 

  1. Select an activity or series of related activities. The activity should last longer than an hour but not more than eight hours (example: getting ready in the morning).

  2. The next time you do the activity, look at a clock or a watch and write down the start time. 

  3. At the end of the activity, use a clock or a watch to check the time. Write down the end time. You will need this information during the challenge.

  4. Open the My Favorite Activity T-Chart Template.

  5. Go to slide 2. Slide 1 is an example.

  6. Use the start time and end time from the activity that you timed yourself completing.

  7. Complete the fill-in-the blank at the top of the template.

  8. Fill in the start time.

  9. Fill in the hours/minutes column.

  10. Complete the time column. Stop when you get to your end time.

  11. Add up all of the hours and minutes in the hours/minutes column. The total is the elapsed time.

  12. Complete the two fill-in-the blanks at the bottom of the Template.
