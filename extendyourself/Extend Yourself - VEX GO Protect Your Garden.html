Extend Yourself - VEX GO: Protect Your Garden

Flip the Eye Sensor

Try flipping the eye sensor. Point it up.

  • What happens when you turn the sensor the other way?
  • How could the flipped eye sensor be helpful?
  • When would you want it pointed up?

Color Detection

Use your farmer to detect the different colors in your garden. For example: If you have a patch of blueberry bushes growing, program your farmer to secure the blueberry perimeter.

  • How can you program your farmer to only walk around the blueberry patch?
  • How do you think the eye sensor can detect color?

Solve a Community Problem

Your robot can do more than protect a garden. Have you noticed a problem in your community? How can you use your robot to solve a community problem?

Create a model, like the garden you created, of the place in your community you want to help. Program your robot to help. For example, people leave trash in a local park.

  • How can you program the robot to patrol the park and find trash?
  • What obstacles might be in its way?
  • How can you program the robot to move around playground equipment, trash cans, and benches?
