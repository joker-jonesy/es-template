Extend Yourself 

  • Research why certain materials are hotter than others. How does color and density affect how materials hold on to heat? What is the hottest material you can find with your IR sensor? What is the coolest? 
  • Urban heat islands can be dangerous for vulnerable populations like old, young, and sick people and pets. Are there people or animals in your community that you can help during heat and cold swings in weather? What can you do to help keep them safe? Make a list of ways these people and animals could combat extreme heat and cold and share it with their caregivers.
