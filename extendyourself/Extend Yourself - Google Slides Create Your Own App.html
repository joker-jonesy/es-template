Choose Your Own Adventure Story

You are in charge of creating a story, but the best part is your readers will also have a say in what happens in your story, too. Your challenge is to create a short story told through multiple slides in a presentation. The fun part is that at certain parts of the story, you will give the readers two options of what can happen next. Each choice will lead to a different slide. That means there is more than one way the story can be told. Before you start, it would be best to get all of your ideas down on paper and even create a flowchart to stay organized.

The United States of America

Let’s learn all about our country! The United States has a total of 50 states.

  • On the cover slide, include a map or image of the entire country. 
  • Choose at least five states to label and link them from the cover slide to another slide.  Once your five states have their own slides, research that state’s capital, as well as that state’s bird, flower, tree, nickname, and an interesting fact.
  • Add images to increase the appearance of each slide.
  • BONUS: Instead of adding an image right next to the word, link the word to take you to another side that shows the image.

Example: The state flower of Florida is an orange blossom. Instead of just putting the picture on the slide, I can link the word orange blossom to another slide where I can have a variety of pictures of them.
