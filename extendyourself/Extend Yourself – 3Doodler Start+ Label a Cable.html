Extend Yourself – 3Doodler Start+: Label a Cable 

More Cables

What other cables need organizing? Design a new cable organizer to work with the system you developed. Consider the qualities of a different cable than the one you focused on earlier. Is the cable longer? Do the ends of the cable need special protection? Create your new cable organizer and label(s) to meet the new design criteria. 

Cable Security 

Have you seen a public charging station at a bus station, airport, or shopping center? Cables in a public charging station need to be secured or locked to prevent theft. Design a solution to keep cables in the SmartLab secure while plugged into a charger or computer. Use the 3D pen to create a system to lock the cables in place but allows them to be removed with a key or some other tool.
