Plan a Trip

Plan a trip to some of the interesting places you added to your map. Figure out the distance and estimate the time. Convert both the distance and the time to different units. Google Maps is a resource that you can use to find the distance between points. To use Google Maps:

  1. Type in the name of one interesting place in the Google Maps search bar.
  2. Click on the Directions button.
  3. Add your second destination.
  4. Google maps will give you different routes, show the distance, and the estimated travel time.
