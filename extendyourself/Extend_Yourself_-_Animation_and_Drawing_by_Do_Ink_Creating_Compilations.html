Extend Yourself - Animation and Drawing by Do Ink: Frames and Fractions

Trace It!

Use the trace tool and layers to create a drawing based on a picture you like. Watch the How to Use Trace Feature on this page to learn how.

Make it More Complicated

Make your animation complex by adding several more drawings. You can add static drawings, which means they consist of one frame and don't move, and animated drawings that consist of multiple frames. Add these drawings in at different times. Make them each have their own unique motion path.

Model a Process

Create an animation of a process or event that causes something to change, like how erosion changes the way land is formed. Add text to explain the process. Use the #3-How to Add Text tutorial on this page to learn how.

Show What You Know

Do you have any upcoming projects that are due? A book summary? A science or social studies presentation? Want to explain how to do a math problem in a way that is memorable and fun? Consider using the Do Ink app to “show what you know” when creating a presentation to share your learning.

Mix It Up!

Fractions are all around you, even at the grocery store. The local grocer has a new display where customers can make their own trail mix. Customers are charged by the weight of the mix, in kilograms. Each ingredient below shows how much a scoop of each ingredient weighs in fractions of a kilogram.

If you have enough money to purchase 80/100 kg of mix, what are some possible combinations of ingredients you could select? Make an organized list or fill in a chart to record three to five combinations that could be made; an example has been done for you.  Show your math work to support your chosen combinations.

Use the Do Ink app to animate the creation of your trail mix. As you add the ingredients to your bag or container, add the weight of each ingredient to your total weight.
