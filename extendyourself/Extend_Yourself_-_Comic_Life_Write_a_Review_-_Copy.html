Extend Yourself - Comic Life: Write a Review

Review, Math and You!

Create two short versions of a comic of your choosing. The comics should have few differences between them, like a change in dialogue, event, ending, or overall style. Then, gather data based on what your friends and family think about each one. Analyze data and create one final copy that uses the feedback given by your friends and family.

Same Story, Different Reviews

In today’s day and age, there are many versions of the same thing, and everyone has their own opinion on which version is better. There are remakes of songs, movies, books, toys, and almost everything.

Use your comic review from the primary project and write it from another person’s perspective.  For example, if you wrote a review about a restaurant and said the food was bland, write from the perspective of someone who went on another day when the food was full of flavor.
