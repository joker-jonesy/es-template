Extend Yourself - K'NEX STEM Simple Machines: Build and Measure Inclined Planes

 Create Your Own Machine

Use your imagination and what you now know about inclined planes to build your own inclined plane machine.

What is it used for? What type(s) of inclined plane does it use (a regular inclined plane? A wedge? A screw?)?

 Build More Models

Build a different type of simple machine like a wheel and axle, lever, pulley, or gear.

You can use any of the K’NEX activity books for design ideas. Compare each design to see what they all have in common. Compare the common characteristics using a coordinate plane. You can compare height, width, incline, or anything else. Draw each design’s height, width, etc., on the coordinate plane. How do they compare to one another?
