Extend Yourself

  • Add voices or sound effects to your comic. You can record sound in Pixie and save your file as a movie or you can add your narration in PowerPoint.
  • Play the Recycle City games.
  • Explore the EPA Student website to learn more and play fun games.
  • Explore other buttons and tools in Pixie.
