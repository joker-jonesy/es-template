 Calculate Average Energy Usage in the US

In 2020, the average energy usage in the US was 10,715 kilowatt hours (kWh). Estimate the amount of energy the United States uses daily, weekly and monthly. 

Need Help?  You can do this by dividing 10,715 by 365 (for daily usage since there are 365 days in a year), 52 (for weekly usage, since there are 52 weeks in a year), and 12 (for monthly usage). With each calculation, round the decimals to the nearest ones place.

Conservation scientists use this type of information to compare usage from year to year. The hope is to achieve a daily decrease in energy used by all. You can include these calculations on your poster, brochure, flyer, or presentation.
