Extend Yourself - Geometric Shapes: Exploring 2D Shapes and Area

 Explore Rectangles

Build two rectangles with sides of different lengths that have the same area. Measure the sides and find the area of each rectangle to make sure they are actually  the same. Why can different rectangles have the same area?

 Measure and Explore Angles

There are many ways to organize and explore 2D shapes. In this challenge, you measured the length and width of your shapes. You can also measure the angles between the edges or sides of shapes.

To do this you need a protractor. Click here to download a protractor.

 Build 3D Shapes

Now it is time to turn your 2D shape into a 3D shape. A 3D shape has length (or height), width, and depth.

Pick several of your 2D shapes and use them as a starting point for a new 3D shape. For example, you can use a 2D triangle and build a 3D pyramid.
