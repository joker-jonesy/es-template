Modify Your “Metal Collector” 

Now that you have created your “Metal Collector,” try building a different one. For example, if you built an adaptive claw for your project, try building something different to pick up your metal. If you didn’t incorporate the electromagnet into your robot, try building it in somehow.

Solve a Problem in Your Community

Come up with a different problem in your community. An example is cleaning up trash, helping provide food to people in need, etc. Use VEX GO Robotics to create a solution.
