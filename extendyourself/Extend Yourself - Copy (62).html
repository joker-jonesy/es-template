Extend Yourself

  • Check out the Snap Circuits Kid Creations website to see what other kids have done. Maybe you can design and submit your own circuit.
  • Explore holiday light strings. Holiday light strings can use series circuits, or a combination of both series and parallel. If you have a holiday light string at home, remove a bulb and see what happens. Can you figure out what kind of circuit it is? How do blinking light strings work?
  • While struggling to create the long-lasting light bulb, Thomas Edison once said, "I have not failed. I’ve just found 1000 ways that won’t work!" What do you think he meant by that? Discuss it with your partner, friend or parents.
  • Go online to learn more about Thomas Edison or Humphrey Davy. What else did they invent? Who else made important contributions to the invention of the light bulb?
  • How do compact fluorescent lamps (CFL bulbs) work? How are they different than incandescent bulbs? How do they save energy? Go online to learn more.
  • What are LED bulbs? Do they have advantages over incandescent bulbs? Find out online.
