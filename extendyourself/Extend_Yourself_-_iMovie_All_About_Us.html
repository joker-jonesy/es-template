Extend Yourself - iMovie: All About Us

Interviews

Interview someone in your school. Remember to complete each step of the video production process.

Pre-Production

  • Decide who you want to interview and get their permission.
  • Set up a day and time that works best for both.
  • Have questions ready to go.
  • Decide where you want the interview to be. Will it be in more than one place? Will you and the person be sitting or standing? 
  • Are you filming alone or with someone else?

Production

  • Film the interview.

Post-Production:

  • Add the video clips into iMovie.
  • Edit the clips.

Nature

Take several images or videos of the nature and environment around you. Create a movie that includes the images or clips. Add music or voice overs to describe what is seen in the pictures. Remember to use strong adjectives!
