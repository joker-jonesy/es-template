Screen Recording

Did you know you can record what you are doing on an iPad without using another device?

  1. Swipe down in the top right corner of the iPad. The control center will appear.
  2. Click on the dot for screen recording. A countdown will appear.
  3. Click out of it to begin recording.
  4. Record yourself editing extra video clips you have available.
  5. You can share the recording with others that do not know how to use iMovie.
