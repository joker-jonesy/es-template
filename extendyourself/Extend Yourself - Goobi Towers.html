Extend Yourself - Goobi: Tower Math

 Design Your Own Tower

Now that you have recreated a famous tower, consider your own original tower design. What would you call your tower? Where would your tower be located? What would your tower look like? What would be the purpose of your tower? What natural forces would the tower have to withstand?

  1. Plan your tower.
  2. Design the structure.
  3. Use multiplication and division to figure out how many of each piece you will need.
  4. Revise your plan if you do not have enough of any one piece.
  5. Build your tower.

Share your design with friends. Challenge them to build the tower from your design.

 Build With Triangles

Design and build a structure made only of triangles.

  1. Start by making the base and 5 or 6 more triangles. Explore how these triangles can fit together and stand upright.
  2. Plan how you will build the structure. Use grid paper or an online drawing tool.
  3. Use division to figure out how many triangles you can make. Is this enough for the building you planned? If not, revise your design.
  4. Build the structure. Did it stand or fall down? If it falls, try again!
