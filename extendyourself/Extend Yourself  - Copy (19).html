Extend Yourself 

  • Explore and share your work with the Kodu Community. Find out the latest in Kodu news and contests. Play games other kids have made and upload your own game.
  • Kodu for PC is a free program you can download to your home computer. There is also a version of Kodu for XBox. Look for it in the XBox MarketPlace.
  • Explore the many Worlds that come with Kodu. Select Load World on the home menu and choose your world.
