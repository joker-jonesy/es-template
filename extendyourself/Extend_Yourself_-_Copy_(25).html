Extend Yourself

  • Create a longer and more complicated animation. You can find idea starters under step 4 of the activity BOLT: Matrix in the Sphero Edu app. 
  • Go through more Activities to learn more skills. Choose any that interest you. You can use the filter icon at the top of the Activities screen to find activities that interest you for Sphero BOLT.
  • Set up obstacles and then program Sphero BOLT to navigate around and through the obstacles.
  • Trade code algorithms with another group and see if you can recreate their design or challenge them to recreate your design.
