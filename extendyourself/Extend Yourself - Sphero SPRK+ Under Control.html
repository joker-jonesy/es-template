Solve Word Problems

Solve these word problems. Use estimation or another type of mental math to help you figure out the answers. Then write an algorithm so Sphero could follow a similar path.

  1. We hiked 15 kilometers. First, we walked 3 kilometers to a farm. We walked 5 kilometers around the farm. We then turned left to reach our campsite. What was the distance from the farm to the campsite?
  2. A team of four are in a swimming relay race. Each swimmer travels 50 meters, and then the next swimmer dives in. They repeat this two times, so each swimmer swims twice. What is the total distance of the race?
  3. A soccer team warms up by running around the field three times. The soccer field is 25 meters long and 15 meters wide. How far does each player run?
