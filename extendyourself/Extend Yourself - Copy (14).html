Extend Yourself

  • Take inventory of your switches. Do any of your electronics or appliances have more than one kind of switch on them? Can you find one of each of the different kinds of switches?
  • Research the diagrams for the new switches you learned about here. Create a schematic circuit diagram for one of the circuits you in your classroom or home using a new switch diagram. For example, if there are two switches that turn like doorknobs that control the one light in your kitchen, what might the schematic look like for that closed circuit?
  • Use another Squishy Circuits kit to create a parallel and series circuit that interact with each other.
