Extend Yourself – CMPSR: Remix a Song   

New Song, Same Time Signature  

Use the same time signature from your remixed song, but compose a new song. Use a different major or pentatonic scale of your choice. Try changing the instrument. Was your new song different from your remixed one even though they had the same time signature? What can we learn about music from this?  

Remix with Live Loops  

Try adding live loops in GarageBand to add to your remix. Live loops allows you to take sections of songs and loop them as you play along or play a new song with CMPSR. Try looping a song in the background as you play your remix. How does this change the feeling of your song? Did it make it harder to play? Why do you think DJs commonly do this during their sets?
