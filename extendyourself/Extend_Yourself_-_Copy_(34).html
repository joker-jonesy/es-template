Extend Yourself

Build More Rides

Your ride was so popular that the principal would like you to build another ride or carnival game. Build the other WeDo ride or invent your own ride.

Go to the RoboCAMP channel on YouTube or search online to find tons of cool rides and carnival game designs.

Create a Lesson 

Create a lesson activity for students who decide to try out your ride. Here are some ideas:

  • Use drawing software to sketch your ride. Leave blank spaces where students could draw lines to show velocity, gravity, and centripetal force. You could create this and other worksheets to go with your ride.
  • Use Scratch to create a simulation that shows how your ride functions and how forces and velocity affect the ride.

Program Your Ride

Try programming your ride to do other fun things. You can program using the Lego WeDo software or try using Scratch software.
