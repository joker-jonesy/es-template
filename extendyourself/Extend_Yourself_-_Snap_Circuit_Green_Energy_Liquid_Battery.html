Extend Yourself - Snap Circuits Green Energy: Liquid Battery Data Collection

 Power Up

  • Which liquid makes the best battery? Do an experiment to find the answer. Compare and contrast different liquids. Record your results and conclusions. 
  • What types of batteries do you use? Collect photographs of different batteries used around school or at home. Create a presentation that compares and contrasts different types of batteries. 
  • How do batteries make electricity? Use the Internet to learn more about batteries. Create a presentation that explains how batteries work.
