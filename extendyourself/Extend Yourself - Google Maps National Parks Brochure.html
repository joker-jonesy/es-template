Extend Yourself - Google Maps: National Parks Brochure

Driving Without Fuel? 

Your family is thinking about buying an electric car. Could your family take this trip without using gas? Are there enough electric charging stations along the route? Go to Google Maps. Type in the same five national park locations in the Search box. Remember to click Add Destination to add another location. After the last national park is entered, and you see the route, type “electric charging stations” in the Search box. If there are electric car charging stations, icons will appear along the route. Could your family take this trip in an electric car? Why or why not?

3D National Parks - Google Earth

With Google Earth, you can visit the national parks in 3D. Click the Voyager Link and explore at least five national parks. Create your own national park tour in Google Earth. Compare Google Earth to Google My Maps. Which do you prefer and why? How else could you use Google My Maps? In what other ways could you use Google Earth?
