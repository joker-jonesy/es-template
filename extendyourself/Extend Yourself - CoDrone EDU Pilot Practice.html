Extend Yourself - CoDrone EDU: Pilot Practice

Sky Writing 

You already wrote a few letters with your drone while completing the Getting Started Course. Now try writing a whole word, name, or message with your flight path. 

Search and Rescue Simulation 

Simulate a search and rescue. Try different flight patterns (like rows, spirals, zigzags, etc.) to see which pattern covers the whole area in the least amount of time. Before you test them out, guess which will be the quickest. To make a fair comparison, try to keep your distances comparable (for example, the distance between rows should be about the same distance as the lines of the spirals).

Trade Simulations 

Switch simulations with another group. Pilot your drone to simulate their flight pattern and they will fly yours.

Fly in Formation 

Pair up with another group and see if you can coordinate a flight pattern with them. For example, both drones could fly in waves at the same time. This can be called flying in formation or synchronized flying. You could even fly coordinated with music!
