Keep It Going!

Public service announcements are an important first step to creating change but in order to really see change it needs to be important to others. Brainstorm ways to keep your PSA relevant within your school. What else can you do? Put up posters, start a no-bullying group (with a teacher supervisor), or invite a speaker to come to class to talk about bullying. What can you think of? Work with your teacher and classmates to put your plan into action!
