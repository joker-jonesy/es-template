Working Backwards With Challenge Cards

Choose a more difficult Challenge Card. After spending time trying to solve the card, look at the solution and see if you can figure out how the circuit works. What makes this circuit special? Is it a series circuit? Is it a parallel circuit? Does it use a switch? To learn about series and parallel, review those sections of the Circuit Maze instruction manual.
