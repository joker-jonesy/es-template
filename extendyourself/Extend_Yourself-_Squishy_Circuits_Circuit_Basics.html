 Learn More about Designers

If you enjoyed designing your own light fixture, go online and research lighting designers to learn more about this area of art and design. Some famous designers include Lindsey Adelman, Deborah Bowness, Ionna Vautrin, George Nelson, Gino Sarfatti, Poul Henningsen, and Achille Castiglioni.

 All About Perimeter

Find the perimeter of the light fixture’s rectangular base. Perimeter is the total distance around the outside (boundary) of a flat shape. Is the perimeter greater or less than the area?

 Find the Area in Real Life

Pick a room in your house and find the area of the room. What tools do you need to measure? Since rooms are large, be sure to measure the length and width of the room using feet.
