Add Ozocodes to Your Maze

Ozocodes are color sequences made of two, three or four colored dots. On the iPad, they appear as colored blinking lights but on paper they appear as dots in a row. Ozobot’s sensors read these color sequences and react based on how it was programmed. A sequence is when one thing follows after another in a specific order.
