Extend Yourself - Sphero: Draw, Design, and Solve

  • Create a longer and more complicated animation. You can find idea starters under step 4 of the activity: Matrix in the Sphero Edu app.  
  • Go through more Activities to learn more skills. Choose any that interests you. You can use the filter icon at the top of the Activities screen to find activities that interest you for Sphero. 
  • Set up obstacles and then program Sphero to navigate around and through the obstacles. 
  • Trade code algorithms with another group and see if you can recreate their design or challenge them to recreate your design. 
  • Experiment with the fraction coding template. Add lights or sounds to your algorithm to make Sphero light up or sound a chime when it finds fractions.
