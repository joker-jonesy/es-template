You Create the Activity 

Brainstorm: What do you want to know/understand about how objects move?

Ideas:

  • If you increase the angle of the ramp, it will affect the speed.
  • If you used real equipment like a wood ramp, wheelbarrow and bricks/books, would it be a lot different than with toys?
  • What moves in the sky that I can use the Go Direct Motion Sensor with?

Interview An Expert

There are many careers that have to consider the weight and speed of objects. These careers include: dump-truck drivers, machine operators, farmers, airplane shuttle, etc.

Find someone who is an expert in working in one of these fields and interview them. 

Some questions you can ask are:

  • How do you handle lifting or moving the heavy object?
  • How is math used everyday in your job?
  • How does the weight of objects you’re moving impact how you move or work with them?
  • What other questions might you ask? What do you want to know more about?
