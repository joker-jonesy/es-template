 Quadrilaterals and Triangles

Think of different triangles. Can you use rotations and reflections with two triangles to make a quadrilateral? Try this first by drawing them, then try to program VEX VR to draw them.

Start with these two triangles.

Then try this with a different triangle. Will two of the same triangles always fit together to make a quadrilateral?

Next, try to make a rectangle. Will any two triangles make a rectangle? What about a square?

Now try this backwards. Draw a quadrilateral and divide it to make two triangles. Start with this one. Then try a quadrilateral that has one more property.

Can you draw a quadrilateral that cannot be divided into two triangles?
