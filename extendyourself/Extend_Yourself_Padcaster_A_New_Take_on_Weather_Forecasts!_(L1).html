Extend Yourself Padcaster: A New Take on Weather Forecasts!

How Did You Come Up With That?

Use the Padcaster equipment to create a short video about how you came up with the idea for your weather report. Consider gathering “behind the scenes” footage of how each part of the forecast was created and the roles that you and your partner had in creating it. Give some encouraging words to others who may be struggling to come up with a fresh new idea for this project or another related project.

Real-Life Number Lines

What other real-life number lines exist or something similar to a number line? Some examples include a protractor, ruler, street house numbers, x or y-axis of a graph, and more. Can you think of one? Create an illustration of one of these or another type of real-life number line that is of interest to you.
