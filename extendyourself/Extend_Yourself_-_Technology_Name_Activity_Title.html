Extend Yourself - VEX GO: A Day in My Ecosystem

Animal Adaptation Challenge 

Try adapting your animal to a different ecosystem. Now that you have created an ecosystem for your animal, pretend your animal was in a different ecosystem.

  • How would that change what they do and how they act? 
  • What adaptations would you give your animal to survive in their new ecosystem? 

Animal Race 

Now that you have programmed your animal to move around its own ecosystem, you will now program it to race another animal through its ecosystem. Find another group that’s finished and have your animals race through the different ecosystems. Time track the race.

  • Who went faster? Why do you think that is?
  • Can you and the other group redesign your robots or your programs to make them move faster?
