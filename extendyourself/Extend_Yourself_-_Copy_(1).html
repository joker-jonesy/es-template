Extend Yourself

  • Create backgrounds for your animations. Watch this tutorial to get started.
  • Combine multiple drawings to create complex animations called compilations. Learn how to create a compilation with this tutorial.
  • Recreate a scene in your favorite movie. In movies, characters show a lot of their emotions through their body language and facial expressions. Make sure to show the characters' emotions in your animation. 
  • Use the internet to learn how artists and computer programmers work together to create animated movies.
