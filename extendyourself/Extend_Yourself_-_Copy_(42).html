Lawn Mower Challenge 

Now that you came up with a solar car solution, see what you can do to help your school’s landscaping team.

The school’s lawn needs to be mowed frequently during the spring and summer months.

Your task is to design and build a prototype solution of a lawn mower, powered by solar energy. Make sure it moves easily and can be used even when it is a cloudy day.
