Extend Yourself - CoDrone EDU: Obstacle Course

Special Blockly Challenge

Complete the special challenge at the end of 1.6: Loops. Challenges are located in the final step before the Lesson Complete step.

Lesson 1.6: Improved Square

Remember the code you wrote to fly in a square (in the Blockly lesson)? Identify the blocks that are repeating so you can write a shorter piece of code with a loop that will do the same thing. As a bonus, use variables!

Reverse Your Path

Program CoDrone EDU to fly the course twice (or more) as it is programmed and then twice (or more) in the reverse direction. Use loops to have the flight patterns repeat.

Trade Courses

Switch obstacle courses with another student group. Each group should try to program their drone to fly the other group’s course.
