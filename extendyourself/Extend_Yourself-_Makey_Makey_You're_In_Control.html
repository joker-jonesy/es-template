Explore the Makey Makey Group Activity Guides to get ideas for your own project.

  • Combine Makey Makey with Scratch to control a game or animation.

  • Add Makey Makey to a play you act out.

The coolest inventions come from students like you. What do you want to do with Makey Makey? Take a look at how others have used it, then create your own projects.

More Ideas

  • Trade projects with another team. Give them feedback on their project.
  • Learn more about the electrical devices around you. Use the Internet and other resources to figure out how these objects work: garage door opener, digital clock, thermostat, or any electronic device.
  • Go on a scavenger hunt in your home. Which objects contain programmable electronic controls?
  • Use Makey Makey to make a controller for a game. Use rules to create patterns of movement for your character in the game.
