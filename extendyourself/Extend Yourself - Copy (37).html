Extend Yourself

Maze Exchange

Mission 1: Challenge another team to create the maze YOU design.

1. Design a track or a maze. Write out the steps Ozobot needs to follow. Include how long it should take Ozobot to get to the finish.

2. Trade your designs with another group. See if you can draw the track from their design instructions. See if they can figure out the one you designed.

KEY: You need to be able to build the track you designed before giving it to another team to figure out.

Game Design

Mission 3: Design your own game board
