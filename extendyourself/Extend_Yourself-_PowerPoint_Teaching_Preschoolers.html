Extend Yourself - PowerPoint: Teaching Preschoolers

Extend Yourself 

  • Put your project to the test. Find a preschool age child and try it out. Does he or she like it? Did he or she learn anything? What could you do to improve your presentation?
  • Explore the animation options in PowerPoint. Click on the animation tab and add motion to your presentation. Do you think animation can help kids learn better?
  • Go online to find out more about how preschoolers learn. Look for ideas to help you make your project even better.
