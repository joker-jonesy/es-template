Extend Yourself - 3Doodler Start+: Create a Puzzle 

Design a 3D Puzzle

Have you ever put together a 3D puzzle? There are different kinds of 3D puzzles. Some 3D puzzles have hundreds of pieces that fit together to create a famous building. Other 3D puzzles have just a few pieces that fit together in a special way. Use the 3Doodler pen to create a 3D puzzle that you can solve.

Give Your Puzzle a Purpose

Your puzzle can more than just show a picture. Think about how you can add to your puzzle to make it do something else. It could keep pencils organized. It could be a cell phone stand. It could even connect to other kits in the SmartLab to make even bigger puzzles! Use the 3Doodler pen to upgrade your puzzle with a specific purpose. laborum.
