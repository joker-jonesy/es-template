 Virtual Pan Balance

Before we go to the virtual pan balance tool, take a look at the images below to preview how we will use the tool.

Step 1:  Drag shapes to the pan balance until the pans are balanced. When you think you know how much the shapes weigh, click Guess Weight.

Step 2: After you click Guess Weights, you will be given the value of one of the shapes as a clue. In the image, we see that the weight of one red square is 1.

Step 3: Now it is time to guess the weight of the blue circle. Since we know that each red square weighs 1 unit and we have 2 red squares, the total weight of the red squares is 2. We only have 1 blue circle on the scale. The blue circle must weigh 2 units. The total weight on the left side is 2, so the only number that will balance that is 2. Here are several correct ways to write the answer:

  • One and one is the same as two
  • 1 + 1 = 2
  • One plus one is the same as 2
  • 1 x 2 = 2 x 1

Once you have thought the problem through, type your answer in the white box next to the shape and click Check.

Step 4:  If you are correct, there will be a green check next to the weight you guessed. Don’t worry if there are Xs next to the shapes that you didn’t use. You are only going to use two shapes at a time.

Important Button

There is a button that says either Count Items or Show Array. If you click the button when it says Count Items it will count how many of each shape there are. If you click it when it says Show Array it shows each shape separately. In the image below Count Items has been clicked.

 Now It's Time to Guess The Weights

  1. Go to the Virtual Pan Balance Website by clicking here.
  2. Click Set 1 at the top.
  3. Drag 4 red squares to the Left Pan.
  4. Drag blue circles to the Right Pan until it balances.
  5. Think about the pattern.
  6. When you are ready click Guess Weights.
  7. Type your answer into the white box next to the blue circle.
  8. Click Check Answer.
  9. If you have a green check, click Close and go to the next example.
  10. If you do not have a green check, click Close and think about it again. When you are ready, click Guess Weights again.
