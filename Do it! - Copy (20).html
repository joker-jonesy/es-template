Do It!

Design and Build Your Geometric Net

Use Fab@School Maker Studio to create a geometric net.

Get Started

  1. Log into Fab@School Maker Studio.
  2. Start a New Project.
  3. Choose Letter paper size and leave "None" in Registration Marks. Click OK.

Build a Geometric Net

Think about the 2D shapes that make up your geometric net.

  1. Select the base shape you will use in your geometric net. A 3D cube is made up of 6- 2D squares. 
  2. Drag the shapes into place. 
  3. A red, solid line will show you wear to cut. A green, dotted line will show you where to fold your geometric net. If you do not see a red or green line, keep dragging your shape into place. 
  4. If you make a mistake you can use the trash can button to undo your work or start over. 

If you get stuck, check out the tutorials on the Fab@School website. 

Print and Construct Your Geometric Net

Print Your Geometric Net

  1. Click on the print button.
  2. Select the options for Print Cut & Fold Lines and Remove Color Fill.
  3. Click Save to PDF.
  4. Open the PDF and print to a printer in your classroom or school.

Construct Your 3D Shape

  1. Use scissors to cut out the geometric net along the red, solid lines.
  2. Fold your geometric net along the green, dotted lines. 
  3. Tape or glue your 3D shape together.
