Do It!

Your challenge is to make a programmable circuit using Makey Makey software and materials that conduct electricity.

  • First, you will need to know which materials conduct electricity and find them in your kit or in the SmartLab. 
  • Then, you will need to think of a project idea. The Makey Makey Gallery has lots of projects by artists, students, and just about anyone. You can even add your project to the online collection.
  • Last, you will set up the Makey Makey software with your computer and start your project.

                                          

Getting Started with Makey Makey

Are you ready to try Makey Makey?

Start by turning your Makey Makey into a mouse and keyboard for your computer. 

  1. Open a word processing document and type, “Hello World.”
  2. Plug the USB cable into your computer and into the Makey Makey board; close the Popup window if it appears on your computer screen.
  3. Attach one alligator clip to the Earth holes; attach another alligator clip to the Space pad. 

Check Your Connections

The circuit must be complete to send electrical signals. Hold the free ends of the alligator clips in your fingers. Tap the tip of the alligator clip that runs to the Space pad and watch what happens to the cursor on the word processing document.
