The Challenge - Vernier Elementary Energy Efficiency: Save Energy at School

The Challenge

Your challenge is to figure out the wattage and lumen level of the bulbs in your classroom using a light sensor. Decide if they are energy efficient and if they give off enough light to help you learn. Then, create a bar graph that represents your data. 

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN determine the wattage and lumen level of the bulbs using a light sensor.
  • I CAN solve one-step “how many more” and “how many less” problems using information from a scaled bar graph.
  • I CAN solve two-step “how many more” and “how many less” problems using information from a scaled bar graph.
  • I CAN subtract within 100.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Vernier Elementary Energy Efficiency Collection
  • Access to Light Bulbs (can be around the SmartLab or school)
  • Logger Lite Software, Logger Pro Software, or Graphical Analysis Software
