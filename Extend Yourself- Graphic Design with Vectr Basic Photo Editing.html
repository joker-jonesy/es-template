Make a scale drawing of a room in your home or show your home’s location relative to a city block. Include the scale factor in a key.

 Design a Collage

Use Vectr to design a collage on a theme or topic that interests you. A collage is a form of visual art that uses images, words, and symbols to create a unified idea.

It can show anything you want to share with others. The pictures can range from images taken on a vacation, to your favorite sport or animal, or to “showing” others who you are.

View this tutorial on using Vectr to create a collage.
