Do It!

Do levers really make work easier? Let’s build some to find out. 

Your challenge is to build models of lever machines, and then explore how they are used to make work easier.

Building Levers with K’NEX

Steps to complete this challenge:

  • Make sure you know how to use the K'NEX parts. 
  • Start with a warm up exercise and build a simple lever. 
  • Choose an advanced building challenge or invent your own simple machine.

  • For all of your projects, label the parts of your lever and explain which lever class your model describes. Go to the What You Should Know section if you need to review lever types.  

Download the lever instruction manual here.

Warm Up Exercise

Build a simple K’NEX lever like the one shown here. Point out the parts of the lever. Where is the load, effort, and fulcrum?

Test Your Lever

Press on the end of the short blue rod and notice how much pressure it takes to lift the weight on the other end. Then replace the blue rod with a longer gray rod and repeat the experiment.

Reflect

  • What did you notice? 
  • Did it take more or less pressure to lift the weight with the longer gray rod? 
  • Which rod did you have to push down farther to reach the table? 
  • What do you think is happening here?
