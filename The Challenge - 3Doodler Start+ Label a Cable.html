The Challenge - 3Doodler Start+: Label a Cable 

The Challenge

Your challenge is to design a system for organizing cables to keep them tidy and free from tangles. Use the 3Doodler pen to create a prototype to organize at least one cable and include a label or symbol to identify each cable.  

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN design a system with a purpose.  
  • I CAN create an icon or symbol that represents another object. 

Stuff I'll Need

  • 3Doodler Start+ Pen  
  • 3Doodler Plastic (ask your facilitator how much you can use)  
  • A cable that needs a label (a charging cable, USB cable, or another cable in the SmartLab works great for this)
