Student Submissions

For a variety of reasons, you may not want students to submit work in LearningHub. Maybe you are using a generic login for all students, or would prefer they submit in your school's LMS, whatever the case - we have a solution!

The Details 

Adjust the assignment submission type to "Observed in Person" for any assignments you do not want students to submit in LearningHub. 


If you want all submissions turned off, please reach out to  support@creativelearningsystems.com and they can turn all submissions off by default.

If you want students to submit work elsewhere, you can create a document version of the deliverables to be printed or uploaded to your school's LMS.

Here is a Google Slides template or a MS PowerPoint template that you can modify and use for your unique needs.
