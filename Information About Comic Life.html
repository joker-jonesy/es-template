What is Comic Life?

Comic Life is an app that can be used across many subjects. It is a creative way you can turn a simple task in science, writing, or even solving math problems into a poster or comic.  Comic Life is a great way to incorporate visual representation of your knowledge.

How Do You Use Comic Life?

Choosing a Blank Template

By choosing a blank template, you will be creating a template completely from scratch. In the first screen that opens when you enter Comic Life, select Blank on the template page. You can choose to start with or without a specific style.

Learn about comics by visiting DC Comics Website.

Changing the Style/Background

This is using the blank template with styles.

  • To see the entire page, select the percentage at the bottom left, and then click Fit Page.
  • You can choose any of the styles provided on the left. This will automatically appear.
  • If you want more options, click Filt, where more designs and colors are provided.

Different Layouts

After selecting a template and a layout for your comic, you can add text and speech bubbles. You can also add different types of images to your comic including:

  • Pictures from your camera -  download and save pictures to your student folder
  • Artwork - scan and save artwork to your student folder
  • Internet images- search and save images to your student folder

Toolbar

The toolbar has lots of different tools to help you make your comic unique. The toolbar is where you can change the text, style, colors, and fonts in your comic.

Save Your Project

To save your project follow the following steps:

  1. Click Save
  2. Select your Folder
  3. Choose a File Name
  4. Click Save

Explore On Your Own

Sometimes the best way to learn something new is to explore and test things out. Explore the Comic Life workspace and the tools available. Some tools help you to change style options, panel layouts, text boxes and bubbles, and lettering. Other tools help you change the text style, font and color, zoom in and out of the screen, change caption styles and colors, and more.
