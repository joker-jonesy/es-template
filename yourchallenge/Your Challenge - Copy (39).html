Vernier Science: Go Motion!

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to use a Vernier motion sensor in experiments that use sound to measure moving objects. 

You are probably used to hearing many sounds - your alarm clock buzzing, the phone ringing, your friends talking, and music playing.

Have you ever thought about using sound to "see" motion? We tend to rely on our eyes for that, but by using sound waves, we can easily measure where something is located.

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about measuring distance with sound. 
  • Students will learn about echolocation.

Stuff You'll Need

  • Vernier Elementary Collection
  • Vernier Elementary Accessory Collection
  • Additional materials based on the activity you choose
