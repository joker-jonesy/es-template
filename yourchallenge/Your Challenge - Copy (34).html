Stellarium: Planet Search

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to use Stellarium to become a planet expert by finding and observing all of the planets and moons in our solar system.

Take your exploration a step further and look at our solar system from one of those other planets!

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about astronomy and the Solar System.
  • Students will learn about the characteristics of the planets in our Solar System.

Stuff You'll Need

  • Stellarium software
    • Mac: In your dock
    • PC: All Applications folder
