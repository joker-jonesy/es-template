Lux Blox: Marble Run

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to build a track that allows a marble or small ball to travel from top of the run to bottom in the shortest amount of time. Your ball must stay on the track. Your marble run must have at least two towers and a bridge that connects the two towers. 

What You'll Learn 

  • You will learn how to define and describe forces.
  • You will learn how gravity and friction affect the motion of objects.
  • You will learn how to apply the steps of the Engineering Design Process.

Stuff You'll Need

  • Lux Blox STEAM Kit
  • Small marble or ball
  • Stopwatch or other time-keeping device
