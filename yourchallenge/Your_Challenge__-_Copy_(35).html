Lego WeDo 1.0: Kicking It!

Your Challenge

The best goal kicker on your school’s soccer team is injured.

Your challenge is to build a robot replacement! Use the Lego WeDo Robotics set to build and program a robot that will sense when there is a ball in front of it and kick the ball. Then, see if you can change the design to make the kicker kick the ball as far as possible.

When you see the suffix “ics” on the end of a word, it means the study or practice of a subject.

What do you think robotics means?

That is right! Robotics is the study of robots!

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about different types of machines, including robots. 
  • Students will learn about programming robots.

Stuff You'll Need

  • LEGO WeDo 1.0 Kit
  • LEGO WeDo software
    • Mac: In your dock
    • PC: All Programs folder
  • Piece of paper
