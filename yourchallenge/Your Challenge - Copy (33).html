Observing With NASA: Astronomy

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to use Observing with NASA telescopes to look at astronomical objects. Then, create a presentation about the objects you found.

What You'll Learn

  • Facts about your astronomical object (a planet, star, nebula, or galaxy)
  • Research and presentation skills

Stuff You'll Need

  • Observing with NASA website
  • Email address (optional Extend Yourself)
