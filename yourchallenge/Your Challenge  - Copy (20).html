Energy Efficiency: Urban Heat Islands

Your Challenge 

Your challenge is to use the infrared thermometer to measure the heat generated by different materials. Then, consider how the materials we use to build our city impact the temperature of our cities. Have you ever walked from grass on to concrete and burned your feet? Why is concrete so much hotter than the grass? Why does concrete feel warm even after the sun has set?

What You'll Need

  • Students will learn about urban heat islands.
  • Students will learn more about the actions humans take to cause temperature increases.

Stuff You'll Need

  • Vernier Elementary Energy Efficiency Collection
