Vernier Science: Digital Data

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to conduct experiments of your choice using two different Vernier sensors and Logger Lite software.

Explore how digital information can be used to learn more about the world around us.

How do we use sensors- and other measuring tools to conduct experiments? Why do scientists collect data? 

What You'll Learn

  • Student will learn about the steps of the scientific method.
  • Students will learn about collecting digital data with specific tools.

Stuff You'll Need

  • Vernier Elementary Collection

You will need one of the following:

  • Logger Lite software
  • Logger Pro software
  • Graphical Analysis software
    • Mac: In your dock
    • PC: All Applications folder
