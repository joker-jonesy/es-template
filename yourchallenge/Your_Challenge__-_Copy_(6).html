K'NEX STEM Simple Machines: Gears

Your Challenge

Gears are found everywhere, including in bicycles, cars, toys, vacuums, and clocks. If you have ever ridden a bicycle up a hill, you know that gears help you to get to the top. But how do gears make the task easier?

Your challenge is to use K'NEX STEM to explore gears and build your own gear machine.
