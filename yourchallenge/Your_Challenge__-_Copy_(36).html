Lego WeDo 1.0 Add-On Set: Amusement Park

Your Challenge 

Your school wants students to study how things move in the real world. The principal thinks this could be done by building an amusement park. Can you build a ride that would help students to learn about how things move? 

Your challenge is to build and program a safe ride with Lego WeDo and explain how it works. If you do, then you will earn the Principal’s Medallion. Are you up to the challenge?

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about force, motion, and velocity.
  • Students will learn about measuring velocity.

Stuff You'll Need

  • LEGO WeDo 1.0 Kit
  • LEGO WeDo software
    • Mac: In your dock
    • PC: All Programs folder

You will need one of the following:

  • Scratch website
  • Scratch software
    • Mac: In your dock
    • PC: All Applications folder
