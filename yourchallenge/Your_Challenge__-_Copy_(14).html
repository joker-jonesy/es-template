Kodu: New World Treasure Hunt

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to create a computer treasure hunt game and explore ways to make it better.

You will create your own world, hide treasure in your world, and then program a character to find the hidden treasure. But do not stop there: What will you do next to make your game experience even better? 

Direct Link: First Game Tutorial

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about writing computer programs.
  • Students will learn about improving their game after testing.

Stuff You'll Need

  • Kodu Game Lab
    • Mac: In your Dock
    • PC: All Applications folder
  • XBOX controller (optional)
