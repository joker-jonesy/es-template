Lego Renewable Energy: Solar Power

Your Challenge

Help solve the world energy crisis by exploring the power of solar renewable energy. Start by building a solar station and then a solar car using the LEGO Renewable Energy kit. Then, see if you can improve your solar car design.

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about the different types of energy. 
  • Students will learn about capturing and using solar energy.

Stuff You'll Need

  • LEGO Renewable Energy Kit
  • Solar Car Instruction Manual
