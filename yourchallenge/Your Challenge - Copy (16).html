Fab@School Maker Studio: Constellation Luminary

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to use Fab@School Maker Studio to explore constellations and create a constellation punch-out.

Fab@School Maker Studio is a digital design and fabrication software that allows you to design, print, and build 2D and 3D projects.

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about constellations.
  • Students will learn about constructing luminaries.

Stuff You'll Need 

  • Fab@School website
  • Fab@School login information (ask your Facilitator if you don't know it) 
  • Paper bag or larger paper to create a luminary

You will need one of the following:

  • Printer, paper, and hole punch or scissors
  • Cricut cutting machine
  • Silhouette cutting machine
