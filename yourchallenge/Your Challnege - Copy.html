Vernier Science: Best Cup Design

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to design, build, and test the best insulated cup. 

Not all cups are alike, and the materials they are made of can determine how well they move heat into or out of your drink. One cup holding hot chocolate could burn your hands and another may barely warm them.

What You’ll Learn

  • How heat moves through different materials
  • How to use the Vernier Go!Temp sensor
  • How to use the Logger Lite software
  • Real-world uses for insulators

Stuff You’ll Need

  • Vernier Elementary Collection
  • Vernier Elementary Accessory Collection
  • Water

You will need one of the following:

  • Logger Lite software
  • Logger Pro software
  • Graphical Analysis software
    • Mac: In your dock
    • PC: All Applications folder
