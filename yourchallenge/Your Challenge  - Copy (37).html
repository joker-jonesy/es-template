Lego WeDo 1.0 Add-On Set: Heavy Lifting

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to build a machine with Lego WeDo bricks. Then program and test your machine to see what it can do.

The president has something incredibly important and top-secret to construct, and she wants YOU to build the lifting machines for his construction project. The president also needs to know the capabilities of your machine. What sizes, shapes, weights, and materials can your machine lift and move? In this Learning Launcher, you will explore different types of machines and then use your knowledge to answer these questions and complete your mission for the president. 

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about simple machines. 
  • Students will learn about the capacity of machines.

Stuff You'll Need

  • LEGO WeDo 1.0 Kit
  • LEGO WeDo Add-on Kit
  • LEGO WeDo software
    • Mac: In your dock
    • PC: All Programs folder
