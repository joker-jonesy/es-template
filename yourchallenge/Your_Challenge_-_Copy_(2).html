Animation-ish: Advanced-ish

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to create an advanced animation with Advanced-ish.

Use Advanced-ish to add a foreground and background to your animation. Also, use the Grabber and Transformer tools to move your drawings around the frame.

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn how images can be combined to create animations.

Stuff You'll Need

  • Animation-ish
    • If you have Animation-ish installed on your computer, find it in your Dock (Mac) or All Applications folder (PC).
    • If you don't have the program installed on your computer, go to the web version of Animation-ish.
