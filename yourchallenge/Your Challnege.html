K'NEX Simple Machines: Inclined Planes

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to build an inclined plane machine so you can show how inclined planes make work easier.

When you think of an inclined plane, you might think of a ramp, but even this jar has a type of inclined plane in its design.

The screw top you find on some jars is designed like a screw, which is a type of inclined plane. Read about Screws in the What You Should Know section to learn more.

What You'll Need

  • Students will learn about the six types of simple machines.
  • Students will learn how inclined planes help you do work.

Stuff You'll Need
