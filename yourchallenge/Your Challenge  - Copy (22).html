Lego Renewable Energy: Wind Power

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to build a motorized fan that runs off of renewable energy. Start with a basic LEGO design and then make your own changes to meet your challenge.

Did you know that the U.S. uses more energy than any other country in the world? 90% of the energy we use comes from non-renewable energy sources.

         

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about the sources of renewable energy. 
  • Students will learn about using wind power.
  • Students will learn about measuring energy.

Stuff You'll Need

  • LEGO Renewable Energy Kit
  • Wind Power Instruction Manual
