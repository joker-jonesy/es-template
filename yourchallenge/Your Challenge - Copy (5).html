Pixie: Introducing Your Family and State

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to use Pixie to teach about your home state. You will create a reference map of your state after learning how to use Pixie through a Family Portrait activity.

Pixie is a graphics program that can be used to make art, stories, presentations, and more.

What You'll Learn

  • Students will create a map of their state. 
  • Students will learn how to use the tools in Pixie to share important information. 

Stuff You'll Need

  • Pixie software program
    • Mac: in your Dock
    • PC: All Applications folder
