Makey Makey: Interactive Board Games with Scratch

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to design a board game that includes a circuit. Then, use Scratch to program sounds or actions that respond to spaces on the board game.

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about the similarities between series and parallel circuits.
  • Students will learn about the components of a circuit and how to complete it.
  • Students will learn to program events using block coding.

Stuff You'll Need

  • Makey Makey Classic or Deluxe Collection
  • Makey Makey Accessory Collection
  • Scratch Programming website
