Sphero: Getting Sphero SPRK+ Under Control

Your Challenge

Robots come in all shapes. In this Liftoff Challenge, you will discover that even a ball can be made into a robot and do many amazing things. 

Your challenge is to learn how to control Sphero and have it do a specific trick. Then, write an algorithm based on the trick you created so that you can make Sphero repeat its actions.

What You'll Need

  • Students will learn about programming robots.
  • Students will learn about the using algorithms to perform tasks.

Stuff You'll Need

  • Sphero SPRK+ Kit
  • iPad or other device with Sphero EDU app
