VEXcode VR: Creating Art with VEX Robotics

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to program your VEX robot to create a tessellation. 

Robots need step-by-step instructions to do a job. They cannot do anything on their own – they can only do what humans program them to do. How could a flying or driving robot be used to create art? When would it be easier or safer to have a robot create artwork? 

In this Learning Launcher, you will explore those questions as you create your own artwork.

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn what a tessellation.
  • Students will learn to program a robot to create a tessellation.
