Sphero BOLT: Getting Started

Your Challenge

Robots come in all shapes and sizes. You will discover that even a ball can be made into a robot and do many amazing things. 

This Liftoff Challenge has three parts. Your first challenge is to learn how to control Sphero BOLT using the Draw Canvas. Second, you will design a path for Sphero BOLT to follow, and colors for Sphero BOLT to flash. When you are happy with your design, you will write your own unique algorithm so that you can make Sphero BOLT repeat its actions at another time. 

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about programming robots.
  • Students will learn about the using algorithms to perform tasks.

Stuff You'll Need

  • Sphero BOLT Kit
  • iPad or other device with Sphero EDU app
