Pixie: 3 R's Comic

Your Challenge

Do you know about the 3 “R’s”: Reduce, Reuse, Recycle?

Your challenge is to explore “Recycle City” online. Then use Pixie to create a comic book about a 3 R topic of your choice.

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about ways to preserve the environment. 
  • Students will learn how to follow a series of steps to create and publish comics using Pixie.

Stuff You'll Need 

  • Pixie software program
    • Mac: in your Dock
    • PC: All Applications folder
  • Recycle City website
