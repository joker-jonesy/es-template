Scratch: Coding - It's a Snap!

Your Challenge 

Your challenge is to create your own animation with Scratch. If you are new to Scratch, you will complete the Getting Started Tutorial. Then you can program your animation!

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about writing computer programs.
  • Students will learn about using coding blocks to program animations.

Stuff You'll Need

You will need one of the following:

  • Scratch website
  • Scratch software
    • Mac: In your dock
    • PC: All Applications folder
