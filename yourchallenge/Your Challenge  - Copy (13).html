Kodu: Programming Games

Your Challenge

In this Liftoff Challenge, you will learn how to create a computer game that programs a fish to shoot missiles. Then it is up to you to make it even better.

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about writing computer programs.
  • Students will learn about debugging and improving computer programs.

Stuff You'll Need

  • Kodu Game Lab
    • Mac: In your Dock
    • PC: All Applications folder
  • XBOX controller (optional)
