PowerPoint: Teaching Preschoolers

Your Challenge

How do young children learn? Your challenge is to create a PowerPoint presentation to teach preschoolers (age 3-4) about numbers, shapes, colors, or letters.

                

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about planning a project.
  • Students will learn about the basic functions of PowerPoint.

Stuff You'll Need 

  • PowerPoint (or Google Slides)
