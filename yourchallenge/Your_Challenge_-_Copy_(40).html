Lego Pneumatics: Make a Scissor Lift

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to build a scissor lift with LEGO Pneumatics and then explain how it works.

Have you ever been to the mechanic and watched how they change the oil on a vehicle? It is always tricky to line up the wheels on the track of the lift. The lift that a mechanic uses is a machine that applies pressurized air to make work easier. It would be very hard to lift a vehicle without the help of a machine. But how does it work? Your challenge is to find out.

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about pneumatics and how it helps you do work.
