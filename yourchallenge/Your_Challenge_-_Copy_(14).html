Makey Makey: You're In Control

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to make a programmable circuit using Makey Makey software and objects that conduct electricity.

This is your opportunity to play the piano on a banana or make the computer ring a cowbell. There is a project idea for everyone. You just have to use your creativity to find materials and program your software to understand your commands. 

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about the properties and examples of conductors. 
  • Students will learn about electrical circuits in electrical devices.

Stuff You'll Need

  • Makey Makey Classic or Deluxe Collection
  • Makey Makey Accessory Collection
