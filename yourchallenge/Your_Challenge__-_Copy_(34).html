Lego WeDo 1.0: Programs and Sensors

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to program Lego WeDo sensors to control the behavior of Scratch Sprites.

With your programmed sensors, you can move Scratch Sprites through a maze, up and down on a see-saw, and more.

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about the function of sensors.
  • Students will learn about two different types of sensors.

Stuff You'll Need

  • LEGO WeDo 1.0 Kit
  • LEGO WeDo software
    • Mac: In your dock
    • PC: All Programs folder
  • Scratch software
    • Mac: In your dock
    • PC: All Applications folder
