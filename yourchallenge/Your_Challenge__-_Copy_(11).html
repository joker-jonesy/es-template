Zometool: Exploring 2D and 3D Shapes

Your Challenge

Your challenge is build as many basic 2D and 3D shapes as you can with Zometool. Then, you will build a 3D model of a structure or object you can find in your environment. In the process, you will explore the properties of 2D and 3D shapes. 

  

What You'll Learn

  • What makes a 2D shape
  • What makes a polygon
  • How to make 3D shapes from 2D shapes
  • How to measure the size of shapes

Stuff You'll Need

You will need one of the following:

  • Zometool Elementary Kit
  • Zometool Classroom Creativity Kit
  • Zometool STEAM Kit
