Vernier Science: Go Temp! Probe

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to measure the temperature in real-life situations using Vernier probes and software. 

When you think of temperature, what do you picture? The weather? You might even imagine a hot chocolate in the winter or a cold lemonade in the summer.

Many of life's decisions are based on temperature. It affects how you dress, how your food is made, the places you decide to visit and when, and even how the Earth supports living creatures.

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about the tools used to measure temperature. 
  • Students will learn about insulators and other variables that affect temperature.

Stuff You'll Need

  • Vernier Elementary Collection
  • Vernier Elementary Accessory Collection
  • Additional materials based on the activity you choose

You will need one of the following:

  • Logger Lite software
  • Logger Pro software
  • Graphical Analysis software
