Snap Circuit Green Energy: Wind Power

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to produce and store energy using wind power. Then, test to see if the energy you produce can power a motor, lights and more. Can you even power these resources when your machine is not collecting wind power? You will use Snap Circuits to design, build, and test your ideas. 

You may know that our main energy sources are fossil fuels, which are non-renewable sources that will one day run out. To make sure we do not run out of energy, we need to find and use alternative energy sources. One alternative energy source is wind power.

How do wind turbines capture the power of the wind and use it to create electricity?

Think about whether the fan represents a renewable or a non-renewable source of energy. Why is it important to create, move, and store energy? Where would you find this concept in the real world? Why does it matter to us?

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about using mechanical energy to produce electrical energy. 
  • Students will learn about renewable energy.
