Circuit Maze: Introduction to Circuits

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to build circuits that light up colored beacons. You will choose challenge cards that tell you which colored beacons to light and which pieces (game tokens) to use. See how many challenges you can solve!

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about the three parts of a circuit. 
  • Students will learn about the differences between closed and open circuits. 
  • Students will learn how to use poles to describe the direction of current flow.
  • Students will learn how to use resistors to avoid short circuits. 

Stuff You'll Need

  • Circuit Maze Kit
  • Circuit Maze Instructional Manual
  • Paper and markers
