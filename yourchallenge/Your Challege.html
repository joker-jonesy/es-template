Laser Maze: Introduction to Lasers

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to direct a laser beam using mirrors and beam splitters to light up a target. You will choose challenge cards that tell you which targets to light and the pieces you need. See how many challenges you can solve!

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about the function of lasers in relation to waves, wavelengths, and color of light. 

Stuff You'll Need
