Energy Efficiency: Is the Bulb Too Bright?

Your Challenge 

Your challenge is to determine the wattage and lumen level of the bulbs in your classroom using a light sensor and decide if the bulbs maximize energy efficiency and support student learning. Then, create a presentation to share what you learned. 

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about the types of bulbs.
  • Students will learn about measuring the amount of power, pressure, and brightness emitted by light bulbs.

Stuff You'll Need

  • Vernier Energy Efficiency Elementary Collection
  • Access to different types of light bulbs (can be around the SmartLab or school)

You will need one of the following:

  • Logger Lite software
  • Logger Pro software
  • Graphical Analysis software
    • Mac: In your dock
    • PC: All Applications Folder
