Do Ink: Creating Compilations

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to use Animation and Drawing by Do Ink's compilation tool to create an advanced animation.

You can use compilations to add a background to your animation and combine multiple drawings.

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about using Do Ink tools such as timeline and pathing to create an animated compilation.
  • Students will learn about the elements of storytelling.

Stuff You'll Need

  • SmartLab iPad
  • Animation and Drawing by Do Ink App
