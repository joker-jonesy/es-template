Architecture: Building Community Spaces

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to learn about architecture using the ArcKit GO Colors 2.0 building set. What are the basic elements of a building? How do architects use scale models? What do architects have to consider when they're designing a new space or building? In this Learning Launcher, you will explore these questions by building a community space with the ArcKit GO Colors 2.0 building kit. 

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about the Engineering Design Process. 
  • Students will learn about applying the design cycle to a project.

Stuff You'll Need

  • ArcKit GO Colors 2.0 Building Set
