IQ Key: Engineering Design Challenges

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to design, build, test, and evaluate machines that move. Then compare your own design to the performance of a basic car or crane. How will you improve your machine?

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about machines and how force and energy are used by machines. 
  • Students will learn about applying the design cycle to a project.

Stuff You'll Need

  • IQ Key Education Collection
  • IQ Key® EDU 5.7 Kit and Manual (For Kits with Yellow Buoys)
  • IQ Key EDU 5.7 Kit and Manual (For Kits with Red Buoys)
  • Measuring tape
  • Materials to build a small ramp
  • Vernier sensors and software program (optional)
