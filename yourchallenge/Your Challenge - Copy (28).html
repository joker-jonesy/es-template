Ozobot: Maze Exchange

Your Challenge

Challenge a team to create YOUR maze.

Design a maze that Ozobot can follow. Then, write clear, detailed instructions so another team can recreate your design. This activity uses markers and paper or an iPad.

What You'll Learn 

  • Students will learn about the functions of Ozobot. 
  • Students will learn about design criteria when creating a project.

Stuff You'll Need

  • Ozobot Starter Kit

You will need one of the following:

  • Paper and colored markers (red, green, blue, and black)
  • iPad or other device with Ozodraw app
