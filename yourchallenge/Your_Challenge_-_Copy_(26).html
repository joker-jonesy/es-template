Zometool: Spinning Tops

Your Challenge 

Your challenge is to build a spinning top that will spin for the longest time possible.

Fidget spinners are one of the newer spinning tops. If you have one, you know how fun they can be.

Have you ever thought about how a spinner works? What keeps spinning tops from falling over?

Discover the world of Zometool and explore this area of cool science. Then take the spinning top challenge. How long will your spinning top stay up?  

What you'll Learn

  • Students will learn about friction.
  • Students will learn about the conservation of angular momentum.

Stuff You'll Need 

You will need one of the following: 

  • Zometool Elementary Kit
  • Classroom Creativity Kit
  • STEAM Kit
