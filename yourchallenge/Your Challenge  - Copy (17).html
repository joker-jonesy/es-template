Climate Kids: Climate Change

Your Challenge 

Your challenge is to analyze facts and data on climate change and use your findings to make a presentation to suggest things we can do to help.

NASA has chosen YOU to view data and determine how global climate change  affects Earth. 

The oceans show the affects of global climate change. The trees show global climate change too. Learn how you can help combat global climate change. 

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about the effects of global climate change.
  • Students will learn about the steps for effective communication and presentations.

Stuff You'll Need

  • NASA's Climate Kids website
