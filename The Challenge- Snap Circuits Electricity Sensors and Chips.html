The Challenge - Snap Circuits Electricity: Sensors and Fraction Models

The Challenge

Your challenge is to design your own circuit that uses sensors and integrated circuits (chips). To prepare, you will build at least two circuits from the Snap Circuits Instruction Manual. Then, determine the grid area using fraction models. 

Have you ever had the chance to play a song with your feet? These large piano mats allow you to do just that. They are so large that you can even play a duet with your friend. But how do they work? Explore more about how sensors and circuits work together to make toys like this piano mat work.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN create an integrated circuit using sensors and chips.
  • I CAN describe what fraction of the circuit board is used.
  • I CAN use a visual fraction model to find the product of two fractions.
  • I CAN use a visual fraction model to solve a real-world problem.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Snap Circuits Elementary Collection
  • Snap Circuits Electricity Instruction Manual
