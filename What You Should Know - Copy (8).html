What You Should Know

Elements of a Story

Setting: Where the story takes place.

Characters: Protagonist is the hero and the antagonist is the villain.

Sequence: Order of the events in the story or the plot.

Exposition: Details that provide the reader additional information about the characters and events.

Conflict: A challenge or problem faced by the characters.

Climax: The most exciting part of the story when the conflict is resolved.

Resolution: The solution to a challenge or problem.
