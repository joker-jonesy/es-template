What is Wixie?

Wixie is an online graphics program that can be used to make art, stories, presentations, videos and more. Use Wixie to:

  • Draw pictures
  • Tell a story
  • Create a video

How Do You Use Wixie?

Start a New Wixie

To start a new project in Wixie:

  1. Go to Wixie and login. If you don't know your login, ask your SmartLab Facilitator.
  2. Click on the + New button.
  3. Select Landscape or Portrait.

Useful Tools

Use the toolbar on the top of the page to add text, images, or videos.

  • Record a Sound
  • Paint
  • Add a Shape
  • Choose a Background Color or Image
  • Add a Measurement Tool

Add Text

Add words to your page:

  1. Click Text.
  2. Double Click inside the text box to type.

NOTE:  There will be a new menu of options and tools when your text box is selected.

Add Images

Add pictures or images to your page:

  1. Click Image.
  2. From here you can:
  • Add clipart (Stickers)
  • Add pictures (Pics4Learning)
  • Take a Picture (Camera)
  • Use a picture from your computer (My Files)
  • You can even search for something specific

Add Videos

Add a Video to your page:

  1. Click Video.
  2. From here you can:
  • Record a Video you the Camera
  • Find a use a video from YouTube
  • Use a Video from your computer

Record Sound

Learn how to record a sound with this step-by-step guide.

Save Your Project

When you are done creating all of your project, you will want to download your project so that it can be shared outside of the Wixie program.  To download your project:

  1. Click File.
  2. Select Download.
  3. Your project can be saved as a:
  • Image
  • PDF
  • Video
  • eBook
  • Wixie
