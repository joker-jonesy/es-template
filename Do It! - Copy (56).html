Wind Power

Your challenge is to design, build, and test electrical circuits that are powered by a fan (turbine). Can you even power lights, motors, and other resources when your machine is not collecting wind power?

To succeed in your challenge, you probably want to complete some introductory projects first. These experiences will give you the skills and experience needed to build and teach others about your final challenge projects.

Document Your Work in your ePortfolio. For all of your projects, remember to:

  • Record your work
  • Take photographs
  • Label images
  • Write about what you did and what happened

Introductory Projects

These introductory projects will give you the experience and knowledge needed to succeed in your challenge. You will experience stored versus direct energy, how different circuits generate different amounts of voltage, and what this means for the operation of devices.
