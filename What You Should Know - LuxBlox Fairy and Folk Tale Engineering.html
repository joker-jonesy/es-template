Important Vocabulary

  • Problem - a problem in a text is an issue, task, or situation that can be difficult to solve or overcome
  • Solution - an answer or way to deal with a specific situation or problem
  • Structure - a structure is something that can be constructed
  • Device - something made for a particular purpose or reason

Fairy Tales and Folk Tales

Fairy Tales are fiction, or make-believe, stories that include different elements. Often times, they include fantasy aspects, characters that are good or evil, as well as a problem and solution. Typically, fairy tales end happily ever after. Examples of Fairy Tales include Goldilocks and the Three Bears, The Three Billy Goats Gruff, and The Three Little Pigs.

Folk Tales are stories that are past down over time from one generation to another. Often times, these are not written down but shared verbally. These tales include a moral or lesson. Examples of folk tales include Paul Bunyan, The Tortoise and the Hare, and Johnny Appleseed.

Problem

In a text, a problem often exists. It is an issue, task, or situation that can be difficult to solve or overcome. Characters often have a problem that they need to solve by the end of the story or tale. This contributes to the plot of the story and helps the story or tale progress by having a purpose or reason.

Solution

In a text, the solution is often found by characters by the end of the story or tale. Often times, a solution will require more than one attempt to find success.

Career Connection and Real-World Application

Author

An author is the person with writes a book, story, poem, or other text. An author has a story or information they want to share with others. Authors can write fiction or nonfiction text, depending on their particular purpose or reason for writing. Authors will often edit or revise their original piece to improve upon it.

Engineer

An engineer is a person who designs, builds, and tests different things to solve problems. There are many different specialties that engineers can focus on like electrical, computer, mechanical, and civil. They try to find solutions to a variety of problems.

Architect

An architect is a person who designs buildings or structures. Architects have to make sure their design or structure is safe. They also want to make sure it functions well for its purpose. For example, architects check to see if a building meets the needs of the people that will be using it or if a park meets the needs of those that will use it. Architects use math, science, and art as they work on their designs.
