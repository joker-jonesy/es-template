Build Your Polygon

Start by building a regular polygon or 2D shape using the Lux Squares.

Measure the Perimeter of Your Polygon

To measure the perimeter of your regular polygon or 2D shape, you will need to add up all the sides (L + H + L + H = Perimeter) of your shape.

  

*Don't forget to label your units. We are not using a measuring tape or ruler for this activity to figure out inches or centimeters, so our units will be the object we are measuring. We are measuring Lux Squares so our units will be Lux Squares.

Next, calculate the perimeter of the regular polygon you built. Label your units.

Measure the Area of Your 2D Shape

To measure the area of your regular polygon or 2D shape, you first have to calculate how long (length) and how tall (height) the shape is, then you can use the rules of multiplication to multiply the length and the height together to figure out the area or the inside of the shape.

   

Last, calculate the area of the regular polygon you built. Label your units.
