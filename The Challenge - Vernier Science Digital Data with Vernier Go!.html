The Challenge - Vernier Science: Collect Digital Data

The Challenge

Your challenge is to complete experiments of your choice using two different Vernier sensors and Logger Lite software. Use the standard addition and subtraction algorithms to solve problems related to your data. At the end, you will create a presentation with your findings and math problems.  How do we use sensors and other measuring tools to do experiments? Why do scientists collect data? 

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN conduct experiments using different Vernier sensors and Logger Lite software.
  • I CAN use the scientific method to guide my experiments.
  • I CAN use the standard addition and subtraction algorithms to solve problems related to my data.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Vernier Elementary Collection
  • Logger Lite Software, Logger Pro Software, or Graphical Analysis Software
    • Mac: in your dock
    • PC: All Applications folder
  • Google Slides or PowerPoint Presentation Software
