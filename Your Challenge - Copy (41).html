Lego Pneumatics: Mechanical Hand

Your Challenge

Your friend broke her foot playing volleyball and has to use a wheelchair for the next two months. She is struggling to reach things like a book on a high shelf at the library or the cereal box stored up high in the cabinet at home. How can you help her? Lucky for her, you are creative and ambitious enough to solve this problem. 

Your challenge is to create a mechanical hand that will extend her reach in order to offer her a little more independence. It should be light weight and easy to use. Make sure you are able to explain to your friend how it works so she can use it properly. 

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about pneumatics and how it helps you do work. 
  • Students will learn about how mechanical body parts help people. 

Stuff You'll Need 

  • LEGO Simple and Powered Machines with Pneumatics Add-on
  • Downloads for Mechanical Hand:
    • Mechanical Hand Instruction Manual
    • Mechanical Hand Student Worksheet
