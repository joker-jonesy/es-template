Do It!

Your challenge is to conduct experiments of your choice using two different Vernier sensors and Logger Lite software. You will use the Scientific Method as your guide. Read The Scientific Method in the What You Should Know section for a review. 

Digital Data Challenge

Use Vernier sensors and Logger Lite software to collect digital data. You can choose from any of these sensors:

    

The Go!Temp and Go!Motion sensors will plug directly into your computer. If you use the Light Probe or Force Sensor, use the Go!Link attachment to connect them to your computer. Watch the video under Using Logger Lite Software in the What You Should Know section if you want guidance on using the software. 

  • Choose one of the sensors.
  • Download the activity sheet:
    • Go! Temp Activity Sheet
    • Go! Motion Activity Sheet
    • Light Probe Activity Sheet
    • Force Sensor Activity Sheet
  • Follow the steps on the activity sheet.
  • Create a slide presentation with your answers to the activity sheet questions and experimental results.  Take screenshots and photographs of your work as you go to include in your presentation.
  • Choose another sensor and follow the steps on its activity worksheet. Remember to record your answers and show your work in your slide presentation.

Reflect

  • What did you learn about digital sensors?
  • How are electrical sensors more helpful than non-electrical sensors?
  • What challenges did you face while using the sensors or Logger Lite?
  • What experiments could you try in the future using these sensors?
