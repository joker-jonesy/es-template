Extend Yourself - Choose Your Presentation Tool: My Hero

Write a Letter

If your hero is still alive, write him/her a letter sharing why you think they are a hero, and to thank him/her for being a role model to you.

Discuss Your Choice

Discuss your hero with friends or family. What do they think about your choice of hero? Do they have knowledge about your hero they can share?

Ask your parent or guardian who their hero was when they were young. Did their heroes change as they grew up?

Heroes Have Flaws

All people have both good and bad qualities, even heroes. Is there anything about your hero that you do not admire?
