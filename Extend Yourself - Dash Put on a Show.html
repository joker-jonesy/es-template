Extend Yourself - Dash: Put on a Show

Prequel or Sequel

Create a prequel or sequel to your number story. What happened just before your story took place, or what happens next? In the example story, Dash is a crossing guard that helps children cross the street. Perhaps your prequel could be about Dash getting ready to go to work. Your sequel could be a story about Dash stopping traffic for a dog that ran into the road. Use Blockly to program your characters.
