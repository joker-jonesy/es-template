Open Hand Project

People who lost their hands can get prosthetic ones, but they are very expensive (as much as $100,000). There are organizations that are trying to make prosthetic hands more affordable by using 3D printer technology. They are also trying to make them exciting for kids who need them. Research “Open Hand Project” or “Open Bionics” to learn more.
