The Power of Video 

Video is a powerful way to communicate ideas. Video combines pictures, sound, and movement. When we read something, we use our sight. When someone tells us something, we use our hearing. By using both senses, videos can help us learn better

Public Service Announcements  

Have you ever heard of public service announcements (PSA’s)? You have probably seen them on television or heard them on the radio.

Public service announcements are similar to advertisements, but instead of advertising a product, they inform or educate people about an important topic like health or safety.

Which Version of Pixie Do You Have? 

Does your SmartLab have Pixie 3 or Pixie 4?

On a PC, go to Help > About Pixie to find out if your SmartLab has Pixie 3 or Pixie 4. Pixie 3 and 4 work the same way, and you will be able to do all the activities in this Liftoff Challenge. 

On a MAC, go to Pixie > About Pixie.

Graphics in Pixie 3 Graphics in Pixie 4

Click the Stickers tab in Pixie 3 to find lots of great graphics you can use in your project. 

Click the Library tab in Pixie 4 to find the stickers folder. You will also find folders with backgrounds and photographs you can use in your project. 

Select each tab to learn more.

Export in Pixie 3 Export in Pixie 4

Pixie 3, click on Project > Create Images to format and save images. Click on Project >Export to export video and web files. 

In Pixie 4 the options to export video and web files are found under Export.

Do not forget you can always use the Help Menu or browse Recipes4Success if you need help using your version.
