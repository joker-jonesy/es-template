The Challenge - iMovie: PSA - No Bullies Here!

The Challenge

Your challenge is to create an anti-bullying public service announcement (PSA). In this project, you will use information about bullying to create a video. You will edit the video using iMovie. You will use multiplication and fractions to support your PSA.  

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN create a public service announcement to make people aware of the impacts of bullying.
  • I CAN use multiplication to resize (scale) populations of students impacted by bullying.
  • I CAN relate equivalent fractions to multiplying a fraction by 1 to support my PSA. 
  • I CAN apply different effects to my PSA to make it more engaging using iMovie.

Stuff I'll Need

  • iPad with the iMovie App
