The Challenge - Snap Circuits Green Energy: Liquid Battery Data Collection

The Challenge

Your challenge is to design and build an electrical circuit that is powered by a liquid battery. Then, test to see which liquids produce energy. You will use Snap Circuits to design, build, and test your ideas. Record how long each liquid battery works (in seconds) by testing each liquid three times, and round each to the nearest hundredth of a second.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN design and build an electrical circuit that is powered by a liquid battery.
  • I CAN identify alternate energy sources.
  • I CAN round decimals to any place using place value understanding.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Snap Circuits Green Energy Collection
  • Snap Circuits Green Instruction Manual
  • Water, Salt, Juice, or Soda (depending on your project choice)
