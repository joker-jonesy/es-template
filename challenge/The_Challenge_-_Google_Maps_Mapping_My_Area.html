The Challenge - Google Maps: Mapping My Area

The Challenge

Your challenge is to use Google My Maps to find your house, school, and other places that you visit often. You will find the area and perimeter of each place and create a presentation to share with your class. 

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN use Google Maps to find the length and width of a location.
  • I CAN find the area and perimeter of a real world location using the length and width.
  • I CAN create a presentation showing the location, measurements, area, and perimeter of 5 locations I visit often.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Google My Maps
  • Google Account
