The Challenge - Snap Circuits Electricity: Build Series and Parallel Circuits

The Challenge

What are different ways to create circuits with more than one light or power source? Your challenge is to learn about parallel and series circuits as you build and test different types of circuit designs. Then, build circuits to fit specific measures of perimeter and area, and learn how to convert unit measurements for the circuits you build. 

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN build and test series and parallel circuits.
  • I CAN describe how a light bulb works.
  • I CAN design a model to show how voltage changes between series and parallel circuits.
  • I CAN determine the perimeter and area of different circuits built on the snap grid.
  • I CAN convert measures from a larger unit to a smaller unit when finding the perimeter and area of circuits built on the snap grid.
