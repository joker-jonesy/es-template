The Challenge -  Choose Your Presentation Tool: My Hero

The Challenge

What is a hero? Who is your hero? Your challenge is to create a presentation about someone you think is a true hero.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN describe what a hero is. 
  • I CAN create a presentation to share information about a hero. 

Stuff I'll Need

  • Presentation Software (PowerPoint, Google Slides, Prezi, or Canva)
