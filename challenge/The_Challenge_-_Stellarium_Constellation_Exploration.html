The Challenge - Stellarium: Stars in the Distance

The Challenge

Your challenge is to explore constellations and their stars using Stellarium.  Then, use place value to describe and compare distances between stars and Earth. Create a presentation to share your findings.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN explore constellations using the Stellarium software.
  • I CAN research a constellation and create a presentation to share my information.
  • I CAN recognize that a digit in one place represents ten times what it means in the place to its right in multi-digit whole numbers.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Stellarium Software
    • Mac: in your dock
    • PC: All Applications folder
