The Challenge - Squishy Circuits: Design and Build a Light Fixture

The Challenge

Your challenge is to build working circuits using an LED and the Squishy Circuits kit. Then, build a light fixture with a rectangular base in order to house your circuit. Finally, calculate the area of the rectangular base.  

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN make a complete circuit with and without an insulator.
  • I CAN calculate the area of a rectangle.
  • I CAN measure the length of the sides of a rectangle.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Squishy Circuits Kit
