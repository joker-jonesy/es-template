The Challenge - Padcaster: Translation, Please

The Challenge

Your challenge is to use a green screen and video recording equipment to travel all over the world and speak in other languages. Create a scale drawing of one of your favorite monuments as a souvenir from the trip.

What I'll Learn

  • I CAN identify the scale factor used to create a scale drawing of my favorite monument.
  • I CAN reproduce scale drawings at a different scale of my favorite monument.
  • I CAN utilize resources provided to translate a sentence into a different language.
  • I CAN use video production equipment and green screen technology to create a video of myself traveling around the world.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Padcaster Studio Kit
  • Do Ink Green Screen App
  • iPad with Padcaster Producer App
  • Google Translate Website or App
  • Padcaster Quick Start Guide
