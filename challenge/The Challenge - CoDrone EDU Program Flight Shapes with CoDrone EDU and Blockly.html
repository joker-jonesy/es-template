The Challenge - CoDrone EDU: Program Flight Shapes with CoDrone EDU and Blockly

The Challenge

Your challenge is to program CoDrone EDU to fly in quadrilateral-shaped flight patterns. Then use variables in your block program to change the size of your flight patterns.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN identify the attributes of quadrilaterals.
  • I CAN name several subcategories of quadrilaterals (such as rhombus, rectangle, trapezoid, parallelogram, square) and describe what makes them different from one another.
  • I CAN use Blockly, a block-based visual programming editor, to program CoDrone EDU to fly in specific patterns.
  • I CAN use variables in Blockly to increase the size of my flight pattern.

Stuff I'll Need

  • CoDrone EDU Drone
  • CoDrone EDU Controller
  • Getting Started Course 
  • Blockly with CoDrone EDU Course 
  • Blockly for Robolink 
  • Protractor
  • Ruler (optional)
