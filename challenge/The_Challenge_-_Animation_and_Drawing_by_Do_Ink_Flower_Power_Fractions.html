The Challenge - Animation and Drawing by Do Ink: Flower Power Fractions

The Challenge

Your challenge is to create an animation that will visually display the addition and subtraction of fractions from a word problem. 

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN explain the relationship between the numerator and denominator of a fraction.
  • I CAN add and subtract fractions with like denominators.
  • I CAN add and subtract fractions with unlike denominators.
  • I CAN visually display the answer to a word problem involving fractions.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Computer or iPad with Animation and Drawing by Do Ink App
