The Challenge - Goobi: Tower Math

The Challenge

Your challenge is to research a famous tower and recreate it using Goobi building materials. As you plan your tower, you will use division to make sure you have enough pieces. You will also use division to figure out how to build a smaller model of your tower. Finally, you will write word problems to describe your tower. See if your friends can solve them! 

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN design and build a tower.
  • I CAN identify the shapes that make up a tower design.
  • I CAN write and model division sentences.
  • I CAN write word problems to describe my tower.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Goobi Kit
  • Grid Paper
    • If you do have grid paper, there are many on the internet that can be printed: Graph Paper Resource 1, Graph Paper Resource 2
  • Goobi Instruction Manual
