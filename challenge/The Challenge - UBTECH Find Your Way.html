The Challenge - UKIT Beginner: Find Your Way

The Challenge

Your challenge is to build and code a robotic car using the UKIT Beginner kit. Then, program your robotic car to navigate to selected coordinates on a grid created on your classroom floor.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN plot points on a coordinate plane.
  • I CAN explain how criteria and constraints help a project achieve success.
  • I CAN build a robotic car that meets a set of criteria and constraints.

Stuff I'll Need

  • UKIT Beginner Kit 
  • Computer or Tablet  
  • UKIT EDU App (downloaded)
  • Masking Tape or Painters Tape
  • Meter/Yard Stick
