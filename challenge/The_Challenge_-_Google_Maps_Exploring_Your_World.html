The Challenge - Google Maps: Maps and Coordinates

The Challenge

Your challenge is to create a custom map of any location in the world using Google Maps. Then, use a coordinate plane and coordinates to navigate your custom map. 

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN use Google Maps to create a custom map.
  • I CAN show how to graph ordered pairs in a coordinate plane using a map I created.
  • I CAN explain what the numbers in an ordered pair represent and how I can use them to navigate my map.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Google Maps Website
  • Google Account 
  • Google Slides
