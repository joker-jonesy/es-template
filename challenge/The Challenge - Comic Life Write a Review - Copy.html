The Challenge - Comic Life: Write a Review  

The Challenge

Your challenge is to choose an experience that you want to write an overall honest review about. In the review, cover all aspects of the experience you had. Conduct a survey of other people who experienced the same product, restaurant, or event as you. Using Comic Life, turn the review of that experience into a comic.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN conduct and synthesize a survey about how others have experienced an event.
  • I CAN use information from a survey and convert it into fractions and ratios.
  • I CAN use survey data to create a diagram or graph showing the information visually.
  • I CAN link my opinion of an experience with facts.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Comic Life Software 
  • Mac: In your dock 
  • PC: All Applications folder
  • Online Storage (or storage on your computer to save images) 
  • Comic Life Survey Form
