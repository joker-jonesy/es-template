The Challenge - Stellarium: Tracking Stars

The Challenge

We do not usually see much action when we watch the sky for a short time, but the stars and planets are actually always on the move! 

Your challenge is to use Stellarium to speed up time in order to see how different objects move across the sky. Use what you learn to draw your own model of our solar system and its motions. Then, collect data on the hours of visibility for the Sun, Moon, and planets, and use the data to create a line plot.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN use Stellarium to view and learn about the solar system.
  • I CAN speed up the passage of time in Stellarium to view the motions of planets and stars.
  • I CAN draw the orbits of planets and moons.
  • I CAN calculate the hours of visibility for the Sun, Moon, and planets from given rise and set information.
  • I CAN create a line plot using data with fractional values.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Stellarium Software
    • Mac: in your dock
    • PC: All Applications folder
