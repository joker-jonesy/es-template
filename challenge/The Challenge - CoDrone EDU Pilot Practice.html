The Challenge - CoDrone EDU: Pilot Practice

The Challenge

Your challenge is to learn how to pilot a drone safely. Then, practice simulating flight patterns for a drone pilot career of your choice. 

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN pilot a drone using a controller.
  • I CAN follow the safety instructions for piloting CoDrone EDU in an indoor environment.
  • I CAN fly a drone to follow unique and pre-designed patterns. 
  • I CAN identify a use for drones and simulate a flight pattern for that purpose.

Stuff I'll Need

  • CoDrone EDU Drone 
  • CoDrone EDU Controller  
  • Getting Started Course
