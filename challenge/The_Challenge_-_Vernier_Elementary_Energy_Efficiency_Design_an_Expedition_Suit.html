The Challenge - Vernier Elementary Energy Efficiency: Design an Expedition Suit

The Challenge

Your challenge is to choose a remote area in the world that you would like to visit on a science expedition. First, you will learn a little bit about the climate. Then, you will use the Vernier Light and Color Sensor to collect data on how much light is reflected by different colored fabrics. You will design an expedition suit that uses the sun’s energy to keep you warm or cool.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN collect data to show that energy can travel from place to place by sound, light, heat, and electric currents.
  • I CAN make conclusions based on the data I collect.
  • I CAN explain my design choices based on the data I collect.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Vernier Elementary Energy Efficiency Collection
  • Graphical Analysis App (use this link if you have a chromebook)
  • Vernier Elementary Accessory Collection
  • 3 Pieces of Cloth/Paper (different colors)
  • Reflectivity of Light Data Sheet
