The Challenge - Geometric Shapes: Time Your Spinning Top

The Challenge

Your challenge is to build a spinning top using Geometric Shapes Building Set pieces that will spin for the longest time possible. You will record your data, redesign your spinning top and retest, and then create a graph to share and analyze your data. 

Have you ever thought about how a spinner works? What keeps spinning tops from falling over? How long will your spinning top stay up?

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN build a spinner that spins for the longest possible time.
  • I CAN use a stopwatch to record the length of time that each spinner spins.
  • I CAN create a bar graph to compare the times of each spinner.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Geometric Shapes Building Kit
  • Stopwatch
  • The Spinner Time Bar Graph Template
