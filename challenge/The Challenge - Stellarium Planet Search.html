The Challenge - Stellarium: Travel Through Space

The Challenge

Your challenge is to discover the many planets in our solar system as seen from Earth. Use a protractor to determine the angles between interesting objects in our solar system, and then use that information to write directions your partner can follow to locate your solar system objects. 

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN locate celestial objects using Stellarium.
  • I CAN measure the angles between objects using a protractor.
  • I CAN sketch angles of a specified measure.
  • I CAN estimate angle measures by using benchmark angles.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Stellarium Software
    • Mac: In your dock
    • PC: All Applications folder
