The Challenge - Comic Life: Introduction to Comic Life

The Challenge

There are many ways to express your stories and ideas. Your challenge is to use the Comic Life application to tell a story in the form of a comic strip. 

Comic Life is a software application that lets you create your own comic strip. It includes a variety of photos, shapes, and text for you to use as you tell a story, explain an idea, or express yourself. What will you make your comic strip about?

What I'll Learn

  • I CAN tell a story with a comic.
  • I CAN order the sequence of events for my story.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Comic Life Software
    • Mac: In your dock
    • PC: All Applications folder
