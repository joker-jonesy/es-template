The Challenge - iMovie: Let's Make a Change

The Challenge

Your challenge is to encourage positive change within your school by editing video clips using iMovie. Conduct a survey to strengthen your argument. You will create a video using different camera angles and use iMovie to add text and sound effects to create an engaging video to motivate others to support your opinion.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN share my opinion about something at school using examples and data in a video. 
  • I CAN conduct a survey to strengthen my argument.
  • I CAN create and solve multi-step word problems to draw conclusions about the survey. 
  • I CAN film using different camera angles.
  • I CAN add text to the video using iMovie.
  • I CAN input sound effects to match the theme of the video using iMovie.

Stuff I'll Need

  • iPad with the iMovie App
  • Survey Template (optional)
