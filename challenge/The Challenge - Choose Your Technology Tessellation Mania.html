The Challenge - Choose Your Technology: Tessellations and Mathematical Challenges

The Challenge

Your challenge is to make tessellations with K’NEX, Pixie software, or another building technology of your choice. You will create your tessellations to meet four mathematical challenges.

What amazing tessellations can you create? 

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN show how colors and shapes can create unique tessellations.
  • I CAN identify where tessellations can be found in art and in nature.
  • I CAN use tessellations to model an expression for a division problem.
  • I CAN interpret a fraction as a division problem to create tessellations.
  • I CAN multiply fractions and whole numbers.

Stuff I'll Need

You will need at least one of the following:

  • K’NEX, Pixie Software, Zometool, or another building/design technology
