The Challenge - Ozobot: Measure a Maze

The Challenge

Your challenge is to challenge a team to create YOUR maze. 

Use shapes to design a maze that Ozobot can follow. Use a ruler to measure the lines of the maze. Collect data about the lengths of the lines and organize the data on a line plot. Then write clear, detailed instructions so another team can recreate and guess your design. This activity uses markers and paper or an iPad.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN write clear, detailed instructions to help others complete a task.
  • I CAN use a ruler to measure length.
  • I CAN collect data and use it to create a line plot.
  • I CAN categorize shapes by their attributes.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Ozobot Starter Kit

You will need one of the following:

  • Paper and Colored Markers (red, green, blue, and black) 
  • iPad with Evo App (or another device with app)
