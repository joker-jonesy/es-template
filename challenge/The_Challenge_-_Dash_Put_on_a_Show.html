The Challenge - Dash: Put on a Show

The Challenge

Your challenge is to create your own math number story or play and program Dash to act out and solve the math problem.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN write a math number story and program Dash to act it out.
  • I CAN write a script for the math number story in order to turn it into a performance.
  • I CAN solve multiplicative comparison problems.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Dash Robot
  • Storyboard or Planning Sheet
  • Props and Scenery
  • Optional: fabric, construction paper, or outfits from other toys to transform Dash into your character
  • Optional: figurines, stuffed animals, or other objects that can serve as additional characters, if needed

One of the following devices:

  • SmartLab iPad, Tablet, or Smartphone with a Bluetooth Connection with Blockly App
  • Chromebook with Blockly Coding Website
