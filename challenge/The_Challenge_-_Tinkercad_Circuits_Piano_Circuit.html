The Challenge - Tinkercad Circuits: Piano Circuit

The Challenge

Your challenge is to use Tinkercad Circuits to design and test circuits. Explore how different circuit parts affect how a circuit works. Then, create a piano circuit that audibly simulates and incorporate specific components including breadboard, pushbuttons, resistors, wires, piezo, timer and capacitor.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN design circuits using Tinkercad.
  • I CAN design a piano using different components.
