The Challenge - Geometric Shapes: Symmetry in Our World

The Challenge

Your challenge is to identify real world objects that have at least one line of symmetry. Then, you will construct these real-world objects and identify the line(s) of symmetry in your models.   

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN build two-dimensional objects using the Geometric Shapes kit.
  • I CAN identify and describe lines of symmetry.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Geometric Shapes Building Kit
