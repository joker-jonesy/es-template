The Challenge - Tello Drone: Programming with DroneBlocks

The Challenge

With all of the exciting new technology being released each year, it’s easy to think that our computers and robots are getting smarter. In reality, however, computers are only as smart as their programmers. Unlike humans, drones only recognize commands in a specific format and do not have the ability to understand spoken instructions as we do.

In order to tell a drone what to do, we have to speak in their language. It is the job of the programmer to convert instructions into a language that the drone will understand. This is the challenge of the programmer, and the challenge you are about to take on.

Your challenge is to program your drone to navigate through any obstacles to deliver the item efficiently. Map out your route and calculate the distance between stops using a coordinate plane.

What I'll Learn

  • I CAN create a visual representation of a planned route for my drone by graphing points in all four quadrants of the coordinate plane.
  • I CAN find the distance between two planned drone stops using coordinates and absolute value.
  • I CAN apply the use of nested loops and compound conditionals.
  • I CAN apply the DroneBlocks App to apply coding concepts to make a drone navigate through obstacles.
  • I CAN test and refine my program under different conditions.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Tello Drone and Charger
  • Computer, Tablet, or Smartphone (with WiFi access)
  • DroneBlocks App
  • Graph Paper
