The Challenge - Comic Life: Illustrating Problem and Solution

The Challenge

Your challenge is to create a comic strip with Comic Life where you illustrate the problem and solution for a made-up multi-step word problem. 

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN solve multistep word problems created with whole numbers.
  • I CAN create a comic strip.
  • I CAN create a comic strip to solve a multi-step word problem.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Comic Life Software
    • Mac Location: In your dock
    • PC Location: All Applications folder
  • Download Comic Life 3 for Schools App from Google Play Store Here.
