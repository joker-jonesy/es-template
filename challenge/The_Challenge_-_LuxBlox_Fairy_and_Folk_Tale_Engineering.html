The Challenge - Lux Blox: Fairy and Folk Tale Engineering

The Challenge

Your challenge is to choose a fairy tale or folk tale and identify the problem in the text. Is there a structure or device that could be created to help solve the problem? Then, use Lux Blox to design, create, and test your structure or device that solves the problem.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN identify the problem in a fairy or folk tale.
  • I CAN brainstorm and design an engineering design structure or device to solve the problem in the tale.
  • I CAN build a structure or device using Lux Blox.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Lux Blox STEAM Kit
  • Fairy Tales Online (Optional)
