The Challenge - Goobi: Earthquake Engineering

The Challenge

Your challenge is to design an earthquake resistant structure using Goobi. Collect data on your design including how many rods, spheres, and tripods are used and calculate the perimeter and area of your design. Then, simulate an earthquake to test your structure and make changes to improve your design.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN design and build an earthquake resistant structure.
  • I CAN identify the number of rods, spheres, and tripods used in my design.
  • I CAN calculate the perimeter and area of my structure.
  • I CAN simulate an earthquake and see if my structure can withstand an earthquake.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Goobi Kit
  • Tub ( or other surface to place build in to simulate an earthquake)
