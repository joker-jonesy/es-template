The Challenge - Google Slides: Show and Tell Fractions

The Challenge

Your challenge is to use Google Slides to show what you know about adding or subtracting fractions. Choose a word problem to solve and illustrate. You will share how to write out and solve the mathematical equation. You will also create a visual fraction model or find pictures online to illustrate the word problem.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN create a Google Slides presentation including; making new slides; inserting and formatting text; inserting and formatting images; inserting mathematical equations; inserting and modifying shapes; and copying slides.
  • I CAN create a good visual presentation.
  • I CAN translate a word problem into a mathematical equation.
  • I CAN illustrate an equation as a visual fraction model.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Google Slides
