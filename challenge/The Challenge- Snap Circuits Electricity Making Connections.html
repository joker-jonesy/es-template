The Challenge - Snap Circuits Electricity: Conductivity and Predictions

The Challenge

Your challenge is to test different materials to see if they conduct enough electricity to make a light glow. Not all materials are the same when it comes to conducting electricity. Do you think you could send electricity from one end of your pencil to the other? What about across a penny? With a simple circuit setup, you will be able to find out for yourself. Then, write and test multiplication or addition expressions using odd and even numbers. This will be done to make predictions about whether the sums or products will be odd or even. 

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN test materials to see if they conduct electricity.
  • I CAN explain the difference between conductors and insulators.
  • I CAN look for patterns with numbers and use them to make predictions.
  • I CAN use operations to explain how I made predictions.
  • I CAN find a way to test my mathematical predictions.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Snap Circuits Elementary Collection
  • Conductivity Test Table 
  • Snap Circuits Electricity Instruction Manual
  • Materials (to test conductivity)
