The Challenge - Vernier Elementary Energy Efficiency: Energy Saver or Energy Consumer

The Challenge

Your challenge is to figure out ways to save energy, and to convince yourself and others to do it! First, figure out if there are ways that you can conserve energy. Are you an energy saver or can you improve your habits? Compare your energy usage with your partner and together, come up with a plan for how each of you could save more energy on a daily basis. Use this information to help you build an argument for conserving energy. You will also calculate the average use of energy in your state—daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly—compare this usage to other states, and use this data to support your argument. You will include all of this important information on a poster, brochure, flyer, or in a presentation.  

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN identify things I use every day that need energy to work.
  • I CAN identify ways to reduce my energy usage and explain why we should reduce the energy we use.
  • I CAN convert watts to kilowatts and use these values to solve real-world problems.
  • I CAN round decimals to the thousandths place.
  • I CAN compare decimals rounded to the nearest thousandth using comparison symbols >, =, and <.
  • I CAN multiply and divide decimals to find the amount of energy used daily, weekly, and yearly in my state.
