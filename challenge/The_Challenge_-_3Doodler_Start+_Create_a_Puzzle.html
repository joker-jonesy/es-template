The Challenge - 3Doodler Start+: Create a Puzzle

The Challenge

Your challenge is to create a puzzle that has at least four interlocking pieces. Use the 3Doodler pen to create and decorate each puzzle piece so that they show a picture when they are put together.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN identify lines of symmetry in a shape or design.
  • I CAN create a puzzle with pieces that connect.
  • I CAN use the 3Doodler Start+ pen to create a unique design.

Stuff I’ll Need

  • 3Doodler Start+ Pen
  • 3Doodler DoodleBlock Puzzle
  • 3Doodler Plastic (ask your facilitator how much you can use)
  • Optional: 3D printed puzzle pieces (ask your facilitator to help print at least four puzzle pieces instead of using the DoodleBlock Puzzle Template)
