The Challenge - 3Doodler Start+: Design a Uniform

The Challenge

Your challenge is to design a uniform for a career that you are interested in and create a model of that uniform using the 3Doodler Start+ pen.

What I'll Learn

  • I CAN explain why certain jobs have uniforms.
  • I CAN create a uniform that meets the needs of a career I am interested in.
  • I CAN use the 3Doodler Start+ pen to create a model.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Research Template
  • 3Doodler Start+ Pen
  • 3Doodler Plastic (ask your facilitator how much you can use)
  • 3D Printed Mannequin (ask your facilitator to help print one of these for each group member)
