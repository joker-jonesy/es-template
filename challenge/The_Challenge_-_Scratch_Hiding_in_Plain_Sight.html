The Challenge - Scratch: Hiding in Plain Sight

The Challenge

Your challenge is to create your own hidden picture animation in Scratch using what you know about geometry.

We can find geometrical shapes hidden in the natural world around us. A bird’s beak is an isosceles triangle. The horizon over the water forms a line. There is a line of symmetry in a peacock feather. Just look around and you will see geometry hidden everywhere. Design a scene using different angles, lines, shapes, and figures as Scratch Sprites. The script you create will help you learn how to identify and differentiate different angles, lines and two-dimensional shapes.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN create an animation using block coding.
  • I CAN identify and draw points, lines, line segments, rays, angles and perpendicular and parallel lines.
