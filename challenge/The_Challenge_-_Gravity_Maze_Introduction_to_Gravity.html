The Challenge - Gravity Maze: Gravity Word Problems

The Challenge

Your challenge is to solve as many Gravity Maze puzzles as you can by using the force of gravity and tower playing pieces to get a ball to fall from the Start Position to the Target Tower. With each puzzle you attempt, you will collect measurements, which you will use to create diagrams and solve related word problems. See how many Gravity Maze challenges you can solve!

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN describe and apply how gravity helps the ball move down the maze.
  • I CAN apply my understanding of gravity and force to master maze challenges.
  • I CAN figure out how to move and turn blocks to get the marble to reach the target.
  • I CAN use all four operations to solve word problems about distance, time, and mass.
  • I CAN represent the operations in a word problem with a figure, diagram, or number line.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Gravity Maze Kit
  • Gravity Maze Instruction Manual
  • Stopwatch
  • Ruler or Measuring Tape
