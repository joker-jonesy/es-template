The Challenge - Climate Kids: Sharing Climate and Weather Data

The Challenge

Your challenge is to view data and determine how global climate change affects Earth.

You will look for patterns in numbers. Then, you will learn how weather and climate are different. You will research recent and annual weather to make scaled graphs for your local area. Then, analyze your collected facts and data and use your findings to make a presentation to suggest ways we can help. 

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN find arithmetic patterns in numbers.
  • I CAN create scaled graphs to show patterns in the weather.
  • I CAN write a message about climate change and use data to support my message.

Stuff I'll Need

  • NASA’s Climate Kids Website
  • Grid or Graph Paper
