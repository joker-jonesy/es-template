The Challenge - Lux Blox: Marble Race

The Challenge

Your challenge is to build a track with a stable base that allows a marble or small ball to travel from the top of the run to the bottom in the most creative way, while remaining on the track. Keep in mind there is no right way to design the marble run. 

Use a stopwatch to measure how long it takes the marble to reach the bottom of each design. Then make a scaled bar graph and picture graph to show which design allowed the marble to move the fastest.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN measure time with a stopwatch.
  • I CAN define and describe forces.
  • I CAN describe how gravity and friction affect the motion of objects.
  • I CAN apply the steps of the Engineering Design Process.
  • I CAN make a graph to compare the time it takes the marble to reach the bottom.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Lux Blox STEAM Kit
  • Grid Paper 
  • Small Marble or Ball
  • Stopwatch (or other time-keeping device)
