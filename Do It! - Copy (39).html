Building Wheels and Axles with K’NEX

Your challenge is to build a wheel-and-axle machine to learn more about how they make work easier.

Steps to complete this challenge:

  1. Explore your K'NEX kit.
  2. Start with a warm-up exercise and build one of the simple wheel-and-axle machines. 
  3. Choose an advanced building challenge or invent your own simple machine.

  4. For all of your projects, make sure you label the parts of your machine. Explain how your machine helps you do work. Go to the What You Should Know section if you need to review these concepts.

Advanced Machines

Now try to build a more advanced machine from the K'NEX STEM Instruction Manual. Some of these machines may not seem like they have wheels and axles at first, but pay attention to what is rotating and what it rotates around. (Hint: The wheel and axle can rotate together, or they can move separately.). Take a picture of the completed model and label its parts.

  • How might these machines make work easier? 
  • What is the wheel's job?
  • What is the axle's job?
