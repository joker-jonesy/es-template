The Challenge - Laser Maze: Explore Lasers and Distance

The Challenge

Your challenge is to direct a laser beam using mirrors and beam splitters to light up a target. You will choose challenge cards that tell you which targets to light and the pieces you need. Then you will measure the distances between the laser maze pieces using an inch ruler, and create a line plot of the data.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN use mirrors to change the direction of light.
  • I CAN measure distances to the nearest whole, quarter (¼), and half (½) inch.
  • I CAN create a line plot to show distances to the nearest whole, quarter (¼), and half (½) inch.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Laser Maze Kit
  • Laser Maze Instruction Manual 
  • Inch Ruler
