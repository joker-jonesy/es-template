Do It!

Your challenges is to build and program a robot that can kick a paper ball. Once you do that, change the design to make it kick the ball as far as possible. Ready to build the Goal Kicker?


Program and Test the Goal Kicker

Once you are done building your robot, connect the Goal Kicker to the computer.

Drag buttons from the bottom of the screen to the middle of the screen to make your program.

When you are done making your program, press the play button to test your program. 

Record Your Work

When designing and testing a robot, it is important to record how it behaves.

  • Complete the Goal Kicker Prediction and Data Table.
  • Take a photo or video of your kicker.
  • Take a screen capture of your program for your presentation.
