Do It! KidsHealth.org: How the Body Works

 The Challenge

Your challenge is to visit the Kids Health website and choose a part of the body to investigate. Then, you will create a presentation to show what you learned.

 Project Steps

  1. Visit the Kids Health Website

  2. Create a Presentation

Visit the Kids Health Website

Most of the time we do not think about all of the different parts of our body and what they do, but each part has an important job. Now is your chance to find out more about how the body works.

  1. Go to the Kids Health website.
  2. Click on a part of the body that you would like to investigate. Example: eyes.
  3. Choose the body part you think is most interesting. If you want to know more about eyes, click on the eye image.
  4. Each body part will have many fun ways to learn: Movies, games, articles, quizzes, and more. Explore them all.

Create a Presentation

Create a presentation showing what you learned. Include the following pieces of information in your presentation:

  • The name of the body part you selected
  • Why you chose your body part
  • Where the part is located in your body
  • The most important job of your body part
  • At least 5 things you found interesting about the part you chose
  • 5 new vocabulary words you learned while researching your body part
  • Lots of pictures- you can use screenshots from the Kids Health website, clip art, or other pictures on the web

How can you make your presentation unique? Try something creative with your presentation:

  • Go online to find videos or sound files to include in your presentation.
  • Write a poem or rap about your body part.
  • Find good ways to keep your body part healthy.
  • Draw a cartoon character or superhero based on your body part.

You will upload a copy, image, or screenshot of your presentation for your project submission.
