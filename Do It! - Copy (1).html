Start Drawing

STEPS:

  1. Watch the How to Create a Frame-by-Frame Animation Tutorial by Do Ink below. 
  2. Open the Animation and Drawing by Do Ink app on the iPad.
  3. Go back to the Animation and Drawing application and click on the plus (+) sign in the top-right corner of the screen. Select "New Drawing." A blank workspace will appear.
  4. Draw an object. Add a frame and draw the object again, but slightly different. Repeat this one more time.

Create Your Animation 

Did you make your picture move?

Learning to draw similar pictures is just part of learning to animate. Next, you will add more frames.  Drawing lots of frames allows you to tell a longer story.

With Animation and Drawing, you can add many frames to tell a long story or explain an idea.

Start Storytelling

Create an animation of an event or story with many frames. Decide what event or story you would like to animate. Think about these questions as you get started: 

  • What objects will you include in your drawing?
  • Which parts will stay still and which parts will move?
  • From one frame to the next, does it look better to make big changes or small changes?

Click Here for Animation Ideas

  • Draw someone riding a bike or skateboard.
  • Show a person walking.
  • Demonstrate a math problem.
  • Show a plant growing.
  • Draw a face smiling.
  • Animate part of a sport like bouncing a basketball or swinging a bat.
  • Draw the sunset or the moonrise.

Share Your Animation

When your animation is complete, you can put it in your ePortfolio and share it with your teacher, friends, and family. 

  1. Go back to the Gallery on the Do Ink app.
  2. Locate your finished animation.
  3. Click on the white square with an arrow coming out of the 
  4. top. Select "Share."
  5. Click on "Video" and select the size you want. When you choose the size, think about where you will post your video and how much storage space you have. The 720x720 size will take up the most space, but it will look better if you plan to show your video full screen.
  6. You can find your saved video in your Photos app.
