The Challenge - Padcaster: If I Could Make A Video For A Younger Me

The Challenge

Your challenge is to create a stop motion short film to teach your younger-self an important lesson. Graph and compare movie speed and frames per second to narrow down how you would like to combine and edit your video footage using Stop Motion Studio Pro.

What I’ll Learn

  • I CAN create a message for young children to learn about an important topic.
  • I CAN graph and interpret the unit rate of the movie speed and frames per second as a slope of the graph of a proportional relationship to decide which option to use for my video.
  • I CAN compare the movie speed and frames per second represented in different ways.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Padcaster Studio Kit
  • iPad with Padcaster Producer App
  • Stop Motion Studio Pro App
  • Padcaster Quick Start Guide
