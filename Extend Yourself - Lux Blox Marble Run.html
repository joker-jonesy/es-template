Extend Yourself - Lux Blox: Marble Race

 Add Some Friction

There are many ways that you can make your marble run more fun and challenging.

  • Change the surface of the bridge by adding a piece of felt or sandpaper. Then measure the amount of time it takes the marble to be transported from the top to the bottom of the marble run.
  • Describe how you changed the surface of the ramp on your marble run and record your results. Explain how friction causes the marble to slow down as it moves through the marble run.

 Time Challenge

Design a marble run that takes exactly one minute. Think about what you must change so the marble goes faster or slower than your original design. Add the new design to your scaled bar graph or scaled picture graph.
