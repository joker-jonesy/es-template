Do It!

Your challenge is to create a mechanical hand that will extend your friend's reach in order to offer her a little more independence.

Build a Mechanical Hand

Use the student worksheet as a guide, and include the answers to the worksheet questions in your ePortfolio. Make sure you can tell your friend how much pressure is needed to pick things up and put them down without dropping or crushing them.

Mechanical Hand Reflections

While you are building and testing, answer these questions:

  • Does a heavier object make it easier or harder for the hand to grip?
  • Does the object’s surface make a difference for the hand?
  • Is there a surface that is easier or harder to grip?
  • What could you change to make this mechanical hand design better?
  • What could you change to make this hand robotic and not just mechanical?
