What You Should Know

What is Advanced-ish?

Advanced-ish is the most advanced level of Animation-ish. 

Advanced-ish allows you to add details to your drawings. For example, you can add background images and move your characters around inside the frame.  When you add details to your drawings, you make the story more interesting. 

Advanced-ish Tutorials

Advanced-ish has more tools and resources than Wiggledoodle-ish and FlipBook-ish.

Advanced-ish Tools and Techniques

The grabber and transformer (also called mover) tools allow you to pick up objects and move them across the page. Remember: A page is called a frame.

                                                      

The two timelines allow you to separate the foreground and the background. In a play, the actor at the front of the stage is in the foreground. The objects on the stage are in the background.

Parts of a Story

Before you use Advanced-ish to tell a story, consider the elements of all great stories.

  • Characters: The people, animals, or imaginary creatures in the story
  • Setting: The place where the story occurs such as a forest, city, distant planet
  • Plot: Events that happen to the characters of the story
