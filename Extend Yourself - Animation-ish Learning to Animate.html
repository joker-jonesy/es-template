 More Multiplication Property Fun

The identity property of multiplication states that the product of any number, when multiplied by 1, is the same number. This example shows that 7 groups of 1 equals 7, or 7×1 = 7. Likewise, 1 group of 7 equals 7, or 1×7 = 7.

Make an animation to show the identity property of multiplication.  The Distributive Property says that when you multiply a factor by two addends, you can first multiply the factor with each addend, and then add the sum. The first model shows 2×(2 + 3). The next model shows (2×2) + (2×3). Both have the same number of hearts: 10! Make an animation to show the distributive property.
