Do It! Animation-ish: Express Yourself

 The Challenge

Your challenge is for you and your partner to use Flipbook-ish to show a change in feelings.

 Project Steps

  1. Animate Your Emotions

  2. Plan Your Animation

  3. Steps to Making Your Animation

  4. Create Your Animation

Animate Your Emotions

You and your partner will use Flipbook-ish to show a shift in feelings. Read the What You Should Know section to review different emotions and what they look like. Take the bonus challenge to see if you can recognize different emotions just from looking at facial features and body language.

Plan Your Animation 

  1. Think about different emotions.
  2. Pick an emotion to animate.
  3. Pick an event that causes that feeling.
  4. Decide how your character feels before and after the event. What would that look like?
  5. Plan out your project using a storyboard like the sample one below. You will upload your storyboard in your project submission.

Need Ideas? 

How would you feel if...

  • Your friends made you a birthday cake
  • Your bike had a flat tire
  • You won the spelling bee
  • You were chased by a bully
  • The Sun appeared after a long, rainy day
  • Your best friend moved away
  • You scored the winning point for your team
  • You tripped and scraped your knee 

Steps to Making Your Animation

  1. Start the Animation-ish software. If you don't have the program installed on your computer, go to the web version and use your username and class code provided by your facilitator to sign into Animation-ish.
  2. Go to: FableVision Learning to watch the lessons.

  3. Watch Lesson 2 to learn how to make frames in FlipBook-ish.
  4. Go back to the Animation-ish application and select FlipBook-ish and the workspace will open.
  5. Try out what you learned from the Lesson 2 video.

Create Your Animation

Using the tools you learned, create a flipbook showing a change in emotions during an event. Share your project with a friend and see their reaction. You will upload your project in your project submission.
