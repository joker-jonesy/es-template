Do It! Choose Your Presentation Tool: My Hero

 The Challenge

What is a hero? Who is your hero? Your challenge is to create a presentation about someone you think is a true hero.

 Project Steps

  1. Choose Your Hero

  2. Learn More About Your Hero

  3. Create a Presentation 

Choose Your Hero

Your challenge is to create a presentation about someone you think is a true hero.

You may have lots of heroes. Think carefully about who you will pick for your presentation. 

If you are working with a partner, decide together on a hero for your presentation.

Learn More About Your Hero

How much do you know about your hero?

Begin your project by learning more about your hero. 
Go online to find more information for your presentation.  

If your hero is someone you know, you can do an interview instead.

Here are some questions you could explore:

  • Where did your hero grow up?
  • What was his/her childhood like?
  • Where did your hero go to school?
  • How did your hero choose his/her job?
  • Did he/she have any special life challenges?
  • Did your hero have a hero or someone s/he looked up to?

Online Search Tip: Add the words "for kids" after your search phrase to find websites created for young learners.

Create Your Presentation 

Your presentation should include the following information:

  • How did you choose the person as your hero?
  • What makes him/her a hero?
  • Describe something about his/her life that you learned in your online research or interview.
  • What can you learn from your hero that might make you a hero to someone someday?

Be sure to include lots of pictures in your presentation.

You can even include sound and/or video files.
