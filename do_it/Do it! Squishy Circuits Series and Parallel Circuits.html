Do It! Squishy Circuits: Series and Parallel Circuits

 The Challenge

Your challenge is to explore series and parallel circuits using the Squishy Circuits kit.

 Project Steps

  1. Build a Series Circuit

  2. Build a Parallel Circuit

  3. Compare Series and Parallel Circuits

Build a Series Circuit

Your first challenge is to build a series circuit and draw the circuit diagram for your series circuit.

  1. Start with a basic closed circuit.
  2. Add additional LEDs in a series. *If you do not know how to create a complete circuit already go back to the Liftoff Launcher Squish Circuit: Basic Circuits.
  3. Use the circuit symbols to draw your circuit diagram showing the closed series circuit.
  4. Share your circuit diagram with a classmate and see if they can recreate it.
  5. Take a picture of your circuit.
  6. You will upload your picture and your drawing into your project submission.

Build a Parallel Circuit

Your second challenge is to build a parallel circuit and draw the circuit diagram for your parallel circuit.

  1. Start with a basic closed circuit. *If you do not know how to create a complete circuit already go back to the Liftoff Launcher Squish Circuit: Basic Circuits.
  2. Add additional LEDs. 
  3. Use the circuit symbols to draw your circuit diagram showing the closed parallel circuit.
  4. Share your circuit diagram with a classmate and see if they can recreate it.
  5. Take a picture of your circuit.
  6. You will upload your drawing and your picture in your project submission.
