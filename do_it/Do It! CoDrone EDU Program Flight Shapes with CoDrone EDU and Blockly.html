Do It! CoDrone EDU: Program Flight Shapes with CoDrone EDU and Blockly

The Challenge

Your challenge is to program CoDrone EDU to fly in quadrilateral shaped flight patterns. Then use variables in your block program to change the size of your flight patterns.

 Project Steps

  1. Review the Getting Started Courses as Needed

  2. Complete Blockly Lessons 1.1-1.3

  3. Choose and Program Your Quadrilaterals

  4. Complete the Variable Lesson

  5. Apply What You Learned to Your Programs

NOTE: Do not try to fly CoDrone until you have reviewed the CoDrone Safety Instructions.

Review the Getting Started Courses as Needed

Review the activities in the Information about CoDrone EDU section, which includes the Getting Started Course.

It is always good to remind yourself of the safety rules for using CoDrone, which are found in Lesson 0.2: Before You Fly. You may also need to refresh your memory on powering and pairing, and charging your drone.

Complete Blockly Lessons 1.1-1.3

Once your drone is set up and you reviewed the safety rules, complete lessons 1.1-1.3 of Blockly with CoDrone EDU Course. These lessons will teach you the basics of programming your drone to takeoff, land, and fly in all directions.

Choose and Program Your Quadrilaterals

You will program CoDrone to fly in at least two quadrilateral-shaped flight patterns. Here are the steps to complete in preparation:

  1. Choose two different types of quadrilaterals for this challenge. 
  2. Draw them on paper, using a ruler to ensure straight lines. 
  3. Write down the names of the quadrilaterals.
  4. List their properties (angles, sides, etc.). Revisit the What You Should Know section if you need help.
  5. Mark the sides to show any parallel lines, equal sides or angles, and right angles.
  6. Measure the angles and write down the measurements.

Review the What You Should Know section if you need help with any of these tasks.

You will submit a photo of your drawing and notes in the project submission.

Once you have chosen your quadrilaterals, use what you learned in the Blockly with CoDrone EDU Course to program your quadrilateral-shaped flight patterns.

Take a screenshot of your block program for your project submission. Make sure to label the screenshots so it is clear which quadrilateral goes with which program.

Complete the Variable Lesson

Complete Blockly 1.4: Variables Lesson to learn the skills needed to make the flight patterns you programmed bigger.

Steps 7 and 8 give the specific directions needed to change the size of the flight pattern. In step 8 you are instructed to change the “seconds” variable from 1 to 2 (without making changes to other variable values such as % power or degrees). How does this change the size of the flight pattern? Try it and find out!

Note: You do not need to complete the Challenge at the end of the lesson at this time. This can be an Extend Yourself activity you complete at the end of this project starter.

Apply What You Learned to Your Programs

Apply what you learned in lesson 1.4 to your own programs. Decide if you want to double the size of the flight patterns, triple them, or make them even larger or smaller. Modify each program you wrote by a different variable amount.

You will submit screenshots of each modified program. Make sure to label the screenshots so it is clear which quadrilateral goes with which program.
