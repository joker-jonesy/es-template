Do It! Vectr: Character Art Symmetry

 The Challenge

Your challenge is to create your own character using shapes and other tools in Vectr. Then, you will analyze your design in order to determine which parts have line symmetry. You will describe how the symmetry or lack of symmetry adds to the style of your character.

 Getting Started

These tutorials and this user guide will help you learn and practice the skills needed to design your own character.

 Project Steps

  1. Create a Character

  2. Analyze Line Symmetry

Create a Character

  1. Visit Vectr and click on New File.
  2. Above the left toolbar, click on Pages.
  3. At the bottom, click on the arrow to choose the orientation of your page. This allows you to choose the way your design shows up on different devices (in the form of social media, YouTube, Letter or Legal size paper, laptop screen sizes, iPhone sizes, etc.).
  4. The Page Settings automatically default to pixels size. If needed, you can change it by clicking on File Settings in the Editing Panel and change to inches or centimeters.
  5. Let your imagination run wild and create your own character.

Analyze Line Symmetry

Once you have created your character, do an analysis of the line symmetry. Find as many different lines of symmetry in your character as possible. Remember to look for lines of symmetry that are horizontal, vertical, and diagonal. You might even have parts with infinite line symmetry. Keep track of your results in a table like the one shown.
