Do It! 3Doodler Start+: Create a Puzzle

 The Challenge

Your challenge is to create a puzzle that has at least four interlocking pieces. Use the 3Doodler pen to create and decorate each puzzle piece so that they show a picture when they are put together.

 Project Steps

  1. Sketch Your Puzzle

  2. Create the Puzzle Pieces

  3. Add Your Design to Each Piece

Sketch Your Puzzle

Drawing some sketches of your puzzle can help you create your puzzle more easily. Remember, your puzzle should have at least four pieces. Be sure to draw your picture and puzzle pieces together so you know how to create it with the 3D pen later.

As you draw, identify any lines of symmetry in your design. These lines can help you create parts that are the same on each side. Do any of your puzzle pieces have lines of symmetry?

Create the Puzzle Pieces

Check with your facilitator to see if you will create your own pieces or use the 3D printed pieces for your project. If you will use the 3D printed puzzle pieces, work with your facilitator to choose the pieces and print them with the 3D printer.

If you will create your own pieces, use the green DoodleBlock template to create each puzzle piece. Pay attention to how the pieces will connect so you create each piece correctly. Fill in each puzzle piece to create a solid background for your puzzle design.

NOTE

Leave the puzzle piece in the green DoodleBlock template until you have filled in the whole shape. That will help it stay the same shape as you add more plastic.

Add Your Design to Each Piece

Use a different color plastic for the design or image on your puzzle piece. Make sure to leave a small gap between each puzzle piece so they can come apart. Add different layers and colors to complete your puzzle.
