Do It!  Choose Your Presentation Tool: SmartLab Heroes

 The Challenge

Your challenge is to learn about a SmartLab hero and create and share a presentation about that hero.

 Project Steps

  1. Learn About Your Hero

  2. Create a Presentation

Learn About Your Hero

Go online and find information about your SmartLab hero. Collect information about your SmartLab hero that you and your audience will think is interesting. 

Create a Presentation

Create a presentation about your SmartLab hero. 

Ideas for parts of your presentation:

  • Where was your hero born?
  • Where did your hero go to school?
  • What did your hero do to become famous?
  • What you admire most about your hero?

Include pictures of your SmartLab hero.
