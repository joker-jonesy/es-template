Do It! 3Doodler Start+: Design a Uniform

 The Challenge

Your challenge is to research a career and design a uniform for someone with that job. Then use the 3Doodler pen to create the uniform on a 3D printed mannequin.

 Project Steps

  1. Research Careers and Their Uniforms

  2. Sketch a Design

  3. Create a Model of Your Uniform

Research Careers and Their Uniforms

Understanding the needs of a career will help you create your own uniform. Use the Research Template to research 2-3 different careers. Consider searching the Internet using a question like this: What kind of uniform does a [CAREER] wear? Identify the parts of the uniform for each career. Explain why each career has a uniform.

Sketch a Design

Choose your favorite career from the Research Template. Sketch a new uniform for someone with that job. Use crayons, colored pencils, or markers to color your sketch. Identify the parts of the uniform that you think are important and explain how each part of the uniform helps that person.

Create a Model of Your Uniform

Use the 3Doodler pen to create your uniform design directly on the 3D printed mannequin. Use 2-3 different colors to represent the parts of your uniform. Be sure to keep the pen close to the 3D printed mannequin so that the uniform will stick to the plastic human.

NOTE

If you don’t have a 3D printed mannequin, you can also use the 3D pen to create the uniform on the sketch you drew in the previous step.

Once you’ve created your uniform with the 3D pen, take a picture and load it onto your computer. Then create a cool background using software like Pixie to show where your person works.
