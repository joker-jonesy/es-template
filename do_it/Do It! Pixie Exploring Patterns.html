Patterns with Pixie

Now it is your turn to create a pattern with Pixie.

Work with your partner to...

  1. Draw a shape.
  2. Copy the shape.
  3. Rotate or reflect the shape.
  4. Combine copies of your shape to make a pattern.

Pixie Tools

Use these Pixie tools to make patterns:

  • Shape
  • Select
  • Rotate or Flip
  • Copy and Paste
