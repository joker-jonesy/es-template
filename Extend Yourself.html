Extend Yourself

  1. Find a short story or poem that you love, can you re-tell the story using only sounds? 
  2. Use the instruments in Garage Band to help you create sounds for an action story or a love story. 
  3. Instead of using the instruments or Sound Library try and create your own sound effects using items in your classroom to make these different sounds.

          Here are some examples:

Long, Wavy Grass

Run several sheets of paper or a brush across a desk. 

Deep, Cold River

Splash your hands in a bowl of water – keep your device dry!

Swirling Snowstorm

Blow into the microphone of your device.

Creepy Footsteps


Tap your fingers on the table.
