What is Exploratorium?

Exploratorium is a learning lab that is located in San Francisco.  In addition to a physical lab, they have also created a website that allows you to actively explore a collection of virtual exhibits.

How Do You Use Exploratorium?

Getting Started

Start by opening the Exploratorium website. The link provided will take you directly to the collection of Optical Illusions. There are over 70 different examples to explore. Each illusion comes with a brief explanation.

Explore On Your Own

The Exploratorium has many other virtual exhibits to explore. To get started, click on the Explore tab.
