The Human Machine

You do things every day that seem simple. You run around, smell things, see colors, eat food, breath air, and even learn new skills and ideas. These all might seem like very different actions, but none of them would be possible without your many body parts working together.

Everything in your body has its own job. Your muscles and bones help you run and jump, your eyes and brain help you see things around you, your mouth and stomach help you eat, and your lungs and heart help bring oxygen to your body.

The way your different body parts have to work together to do something as simple as seeing a red apple, picking it up, and eating it is amazing!
