What You Should Know

Towers

Have you ever wondered how towers are designed? Architects and engineers work together to design buildings that meet people's needs and look good. When building a tower, architects and engineers must consider the types of building materials, the cost of materials, and labor when building structures. Buildings require a ton of material so it is important that architects and engineers choose materials that are strong and cheap.

Towers are used for many different purposes. Towers have been built to serve as strategic advantages for observing and guarding, they have been build to provided electricity, to enhance communication, and to help support bridges, and even to direct plane, boat, and car traffic. Towers traditionally served one purpose, but today tower design includes residences, shops, and restaurants. 

         

The Tallest Towers

The tallest tower in the world is the Tokyo SkyTree in Tokyo, Japan. The Tokyo SkyTree towers at 2,080 feet and is made of steel. It serves as a broadcast tower, observation deck for tourists and it even has a restaurant. It was built to withstand earthquakes and has a 3 leg, tripod design. 

The Canton Tower in China, the second tallest tower in the world at 1,982 feet, is made out of steel and concrete. It serves as a telecommunications tower, it also has an observation deck for tourists, and has a restaurant. 

The Builders

There are many people that help to design and build a tower. If you wanted to build a tower today, your building team would likely include an architect, building engineers, draftsperson, building designer, surveyors, electricians, plumbers, painters, elevator mechanic, carpenter, laborers, and many others.
