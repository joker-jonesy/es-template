Important Vocabulary

  • Programming - a series of coded software instructions to control the operation of a computer or another machine
  • Block Coding - refers to the technique of adding extra bits to a digital word in order to improve the reliability of transmission
  • Loops - an easy way to tell the computer to repeat an action without writing another line of code
  • Sequence - completing a series of steps in a certain order
  • Variables - like a container that holds information that you can change
  • Event - used to trigger certain behaviors in your program
  • Perimeter - the continuous line forming the boundary of a closed geometric figure
  • Area- the measurement of a surface or piece of land
  • Robot- a machine capable of carrying out a series of actions automatically

Area

To find the area of a rectangle you multiply the length and width A=L x W. To find the area of a square, you square one side A2.
