The Scientific Method

The scientific method is the process used by scientists to test ideas and predictions about the world around them. Whenever you do an experiment, be sure to follow these steps:

  1. Think of a testable question you want to answer.
  2. Form a hypothesis, a prediction of what you think you will find.
  3. Test your hypothesis by doing an experiment or a study. 
  4. Analyze your results to find patterns and trends.
  5. Draw a conclusion about your question and hypothesis based on your experiment, and report your findings.

Scientists answer important questions and discover solutions by using the scientific method.

Record which steps you are completing as you are doing your project.
