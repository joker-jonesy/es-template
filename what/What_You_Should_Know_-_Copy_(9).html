How Do Preschoolers Learn?

Do you remember how you learned when you were 3-4 years old? Do you have some old picture books from when you were that age? Maybe you have a younger brother or sister. Before beginning your PowerPoint project, take the time to think about how young children learn.

  • Do they read?
  • Do they write math problems?
  • Do they write any numbers or letters?
  • Do they use computers?

Take a moment to talk with your partner about your impressions or observations of how preschoolers learn.

          

  • Preschoolers learn with their senses: Seeing, hearing, touching, tasting, and smelling.
  • They learn by experiencing and doing.
  • They are learning about words and how to use words well.
  • They need a balance of quiet and active play.
  • They might ask lots of “how” and “why” questions.
  • They are just beginning to gain skills to put objects into matching groups.
