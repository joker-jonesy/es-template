Pictures Tell a Story

Have you heard the expression, “a picture is worth a thousand words”? It means that pictures often communicate ideas better than words alone. 

Let’s say an ice cream shop wanted to interest you in a delicious ice cream brownie sundae. 

Do you think it would be better to list all the ingredients, explain how it is made, and tell you about the taste?…or just show you a picture?

It is best to communicate in ways that make the most sense for your message and your audience.

What Do Signs Mean?

   

Why do you think signs communicate messages with pictures, instead of words?

Using pictures is a great way to communicate ideas, and using pictures with words can be even better. 

Here are ways to show your work in the SmartLab:

  1. Take pictures or screenshots of your project.
  2. Add labels to help someone understand the picture.
  3. Add text to help explain the picture and labels in more detail.
