What You Should Know 

Scratch Plus WeDo

You might have used Scratch or WeDo software before.

What would you do if you could use Scratch to program your WeDo robots?

Would you program:

  • Your Goal Kicker to kick backward?
  • Your Goal Kicker to know when the ball is close to the foot?
  • Your Spinner and top to use sensors to turn on or off?
  • Your Alligator to open and close its mouth using arrow keys on the keyboard?

Compare Programs

In this challenge, you will combine Scratch and WeDo. You will use Scratch to program the Hungry Alligator.

WeDo blocks and Scratch blocks are similar.

For example, there are:

  • Blocks for letters or keys on the keyboard
  • Blocks to control the direction the motor spins
  • Blocks to add sound

Software Check 

Do you need the Scratch – WeDo plugin?  

  1. Open the Scratch website. Click here.
  2. Select Create.
  3. Select More Blocks.
  4. Select Add an Extension.
  5. Choose LEGO WeDo 1.0.

 

If you see a red light and the error message below, you need a plugin. Follow the Installing the Scratch Device instructions.

Check with your Facilitator before installing the WeDo plugin.

If you see a yellow or green light, continue to the Do It! section.
