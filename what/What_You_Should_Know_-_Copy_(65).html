Uses for Pneumatics

While there are many possible solutions to your challenge, pneumatic tools tend to be the lightest-weight options because they are powered by air. Air is light and easy to store. Air hammers and metal hammers are both used to carve stone, but air hammers are half the weight.

 

What other pneumatic tools have you seen? Are they lighter or faster than similar tools that are not powered by air?

Types of Replacement Hands

There are many types of mechanical, prosthetic, and robotic hands with different uses. 


This mechanical "reacher" hand helps people grab things that are high or far away.		 Robotic hands worn by stroke victims help them regain the use of their hands.

Surgeons use robotic hands to assist with precision during surgery.


Robotic hands help people who cannot move. This robot is controlled by the woman’s mind, and helps her feed herself.
