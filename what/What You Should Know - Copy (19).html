What You Should Know

Constellations

What is a Constellation?

A constellation is a group of stars. When the stars are connected, they form an imaginary outline of a shape. Each constellation is given a name.

The first constellations were named thousands of years ago by Egyptians, and other groups of people around the world.

How are Constellations Used?

People used the constellations to navigate long ago.

The stars have helped many people navigate. Harriet Tubman, and other slaves, used the North Star, Polaris, to travel North, where they could be free.

Sailors use the stars to navigate their boats in the ocean.

Even some animals, like Mallard ducks, use the stars when they migrate.

Why Do the Constellations Appear to Change? 

Polaris is a circumpolar star, which means it is near one of the poles. Circumpolar stars near the North Pole can be seen all year round by people living in the United States.

Not all stars can be seen all year. If you went outside and spent the whole night watching the night sky, you would notice that the constellations look like they are moving from East to West. This happens because the Earth rotates, or spins. 

The constellations in the sky also change with the seasons as the Earth orbits, or moves, around the Sun.

Luminaries

What is a Luminary?

A luminary is an object that gives off light.

Paper luminaries are usually made with paper bags. A small candle, like a tealight candle, is set inside.

Sometimes patterns are cut out of the bag or drawn on the bag. Luminaries are used to light pathways or outdoor spaces, especially on special occasions.

Fab@School Maker Studio

Fab@School Maker Studio is an online software program used to design and print 3D models.

You can print your design on a regular printer, a paper cutting machine, or a 3D printer. 

Creating a New Project

  1. Go to Fab@School Maker Studio and login.
  2. Start a New Project.
  3. Choose your project settings and click OK.

Adding and Editing Shape Cut-outs

  1. Click on the shape tool and select the shape you want to add.
  2. Use the squares at the corner of the shape to make it bigger or smaller.
  3. Click in the center of the shape to move it.

Drawing Lines

  1. Click on the drawing tool.
  2. Choose line tool, curve tool, or brush tool.
  3. Use the tool to add lines to your creation.
  4. Click on the drawing tool again to turn off the drawing tool.
