What is a Hero?

The word hero comes from an ancient Greek word meaning “protector” or “defender”. 

Today, we think of heroes as much more. There are many types of heroes.

What does the does the word “hero” mean to you? 

As you think of a hero to share about think about the following questions:

  • Can you think of some examples of heroes? What makes them heroes in your eyes? 
  • Were they brave? Did they help you? Did they help others? Are they sports or music stars? Did they have a great achievement in their life?
