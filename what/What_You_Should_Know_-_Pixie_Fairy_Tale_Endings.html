Characteristics of a Fairy Tale

There are many, many fairy tales from all around the world. All fairy tales share common qualities. Can you name some of them?

Here are a few:

  • Old: They took place long ago.
  • Magic: The characters have special abilities.
  • Fantasy: There are make-believe events or characters.
  • Good vs. Bad: The good and bad characters are known.

 

What Makes a Great Story? 

Great stories share these qualities:

  • Setting: The place where the story happens
  • Characters: The people, animals, or creatures in the story
  • Plot: The events that happen in the story
