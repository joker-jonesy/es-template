What You Should Know

Machines Make Work Easier

The job of a machine is to make work easier. It does this by changing how force is used to move an object, or load.

For example, you do work when you push a grocery cart at the store.

Pushing is the force you apply to your cart (your machine) to easily move groceries (your load). You also do work when you pull something in a wagon. A force can be a push or a pull.

Machines Need Energy to Work

A machine needs a source of energy in order to do work.

With some machines, you are the source of energy, like the man and boy in the example.

An energy source supplies energy to the machine so that it can do the work.

Six Types of Simple Machines

There are six types of simple machines. Simple machines are tools that make it easier to do work. They can help us lift or move something heavy, move things apart, hold them together, or change the direction of a force.

What Is an Inclined Plane?

A plane is a wide flat surface on an angle. An inclined plane is also called a ramp. Ramps allow us to walk up and down with less effort. 

How Do Inclined Planes Help Us? 

Is it easier to jump from the ground to the back of the truck or is it easier to walk up the ramp?

Jumping up onto the back of the truck takes less time but requires more effort. Walking up the ramp takes more time but requires less effort. 

Wedges

A wedge is made of two back-to-back inclined planes. They are used to change the direction of a force to make work easier. A wedge can be used to separate objects or to hold them in place.

Imagine you wanted to split a piece of wood. When you apply force down on the wide end of the wedge, the two sides of the wedge produce forces that point out and away from each inclined surface.

Now imagine you have a door that will not stay open. What do you do? 

You most likely use a doorstop.

When a doorstop is placed under a door, there are two forces acting on the wedge:

  1. The force of the door pushing against the top of the doorstop. 
  2. The friction force between the bottom of the doorstop and the ground.

These two forces point in opposite directions. If the friction force is stronger than the door's closing force, the door will not move.

Screws

A screw is a special type of inclined plane, usually used to hold things together like a shelf and a wall.

How can a screw be an inclined plane?
