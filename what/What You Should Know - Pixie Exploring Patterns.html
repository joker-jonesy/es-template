Characteristics of Patterns 

What is a pattern?

  • Patterns repeat shapes and colors.
  • Patterns have balance.
  • Patterns also have symmetry. Symmetry is when one side looks like the mirror-image of the other side.

In music, sounds also repeat to make patterns. These could be called musical themes.

Rotation 

Making a pattern is easy. You can spin a shape around a center point to create a pattern.

Reflection 

Another way to create a pattern is with reflection. Imagine using a mirror to copy an object.
