What is Tinkercad?

Tinkercad is a 3D modeling program. You can use Tinkercad to create 3D models or drawings of objects that have three dimensions. The tools in Tinkercad allow you to shrink or enlarge your model and view it from the side, top, or bottom.

How Do You Use Tinkercad?

Login/Create an Account

  1. Go to Tinkercad and click Sign Up.
  2. Enter requested info and click Next.
  3. If you are under 13 years old, fill out the form and click Create Account. Then, ask your facilitator for the invite code, enter it, and click Get Approved.
  4. If you are over 13  years old, use your school email, create a password, click Create Account, and you’re all set!

Helpful Tools

Review Your Work

Make sure to check your sizes, Tinkercad measures in millimeters. Use the navigation tool to pan, zoom, and spin your objects. Make sure your objects are where you want them to be.

                       

Name and Save Your Work

Tinkercad automatically saves your work but you might want to rename your design so that it’s easy to find. You can rename your design by clicking on the title at the top left.

Share Your Stamp With Others

Tinkercad has a Gallery where you can share your stamp with others. If you do not have access to a 3D printer, you may want to share your stamp in the Gallery so others can download and print it.

To make your stamp public in the Tinkercad Gallery you will need to be in My recent designs part of Tinkercad. When you Sign in to Tinkercad it will automatically take you to this site.

  1. Your object will be labeled Private. You want to change Private to Public. To change this option, click on Private.
  2. On the next screen, click on the settings icon.
  3. On the next screen, click on Private and change to Public. Then click Save Changes.
  4. To get back to My recent designs from your design screen, click on the Tinkercad logo. Note: Click on the name to change it.
  5. Your 3D stamp is now in the Tinkercad Galley and visible to everyone!
