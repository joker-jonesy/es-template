What is iMovie?

iMovie is video editing software that is available on iPadOS, iOS devices and macOS. You can use iMovie to combine video clips, add special effects, add text, and more. Once you’ve finished editing a video, you can save it and share with others. Video editing software is commonly used to make a film more engaging and entertaining for its viewers. 

How Do You Use iMovie?

Add Video Clips

To create a new project:

  1. Open the iMovie app. 
  2. Click the + sign to add your videos.

Add a Transition

To add a transition to a video clip: 

  1. Insert video clips into the timeline.
  2. Click on the transition icon between the clips.
  3. Locate the menu at the bottom of the screen. 
  4. Choose any transition you would like.
  5. Play the clip to preview the transition. 
  6. To change the length of time the transition lasts, click on duration then adjust to the length desired.

Add Text

To add a title or text to a video clip: 

  1. Click on the part where you want to add text or a title.
  2. Locate the menu at the bottom of the screen. 
  3. Click the “Titles” icon.
  4. Choose the text you want to use.
  5. Click out of the menu bar.
  6. Click on “Title Text Here” on the video itself.
  7. Type what you want to include in text

Add Sound Effects

To add sound effects to a video clip: 

  1. Insert video clips into the timeline.
  2. Click where you want to add sound effects.
  3. A menu will show up at the bottom of the screen 
  4. Click the + symbol in the top right corner.
  5. Click “Audio.”
  6. Click “Sound Effects.”
  7. Choose the sound you want to use.
  8. Click the + next to the sound to add.
  9. To shorten or expand the sound, click on the sound bar after it is added. Move with your hand to adjust to the length you want.
  10. To remove a sound, click on the sound bar. Click on the trashcan icon in the bottom right corner

Add a Ken Burns Effect

The Ken Burns effect is a way to make photos enter, stop, or exit at a certain spot in the photo. The focus is up to the video maker. 

To add a Ken Burns effect to a photo:

  1. Insert a photo into the timeline.
  2. Click on the photo on the timeline.
  3. Look for the menu at the bottom of the screen.
  4. Ensure the “Ken Burns” effect is enabled.
  5. Pinch to position the start of the photo.

Save Your Video

To save your video:

  1. Click on save video (it will save on the iPad). 
  2. Save it to your device: 
  • If you have a Mac, airdrop the video to your computer. 
  • If you have a PC, connect the cable, locate the iPad in File Explorer, and drag the iMovie where you would like to save it.

Explore On Your Own

To learn how to use iMovie, start by recording two short video clips introducing yourself to an audience. Refer to the previous section for assistance on how to complete each step. 

  1. Open iMovie.
  2. Add your two video clips.
  3. Change the transition to “Fade.”
  4. Insert the words “Good Morning!” at the beginning of your video in the lower corner.
  5. Add applause at the end of the video.
