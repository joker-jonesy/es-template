What is K'NEX STEM?

Use your K’NEX kit to build shapes, models, machines, vehicles, rides and more.  Create original designs using your imagination or follow step-by-step directions in the instructional manuals provided.

How Do You Use K'NEX STEM?

K’NEX Parts

K'NEX kits are made up of parts called connectors and rods. There are many shapes and sizes. Every rod can fit into connectors three different ways. 

NOTE: Be sure to push parts together and pull them apart without bending them. This will help you avoid breaking any pieces.
