What is Arckit?

Explore architecture and design with the Arckit model building set.  With Arckit you can build, to-scale, a house or any other structure.

How Do You Use Arckit?

How to Use Arckit GO Colors 2.0 Building Materials

The Arckit GO Colors 2.0 building set has some plastic pieces that allow you to construct the major components of a building including floors, walls, roofing, tiles, stairs, and textures (wallpaper, flooring, etc.).

Floor Pieces
 Wall Pieces
 Roof, Door, and Window Pieces
 Tile Pieces

Each kit comes with plastic pieces that are used to connect pieces.

Plastic Pieces Connectors

The smallest white connectors have four pins on them that fit into the floor pieces. You can use these connectors to keep the floor pieces together.

The straight and L-shaped connectors have holes in them. You can fit walls, stairs, and roofing pieces into these connectors to put the different parts of your building together.
