What is Animation and Drawing by Do Ink?

Animation and Drawing by Do Ink is a software application that allows you to make animations on your computer. Use the drawing editor to create vector artwork and frame-by-frame (flip book) animations. Use the composition editor to combine your artwork to tell a longer story.

How Do You Use Animation and Drawing by Do Ink?

Start New Project

  1. Open the Animation and Drawing by Do Ink app on the iPad.

  2. Go back to the Animation and Drawing application and click on the plus (+) sign in the top-right corner of the screen. Select New Drawing. A blank workspace will appear.

Tools

There are four primary areas in the Drawing Editor:

Select each tab to learn more.

Upper Toolbar
 Canvas
 Lower Toolbar
 Frame Sequencer

The Upper Toolbar contains an assortment of controls for managing editor settings, drawing properties, and layers in your drawing.

The Canvas is where you'll create your drawings. It shows the currently selected frame in your drawing sequence.

The Lower Toolbar contains all the tools you'll need to create and edit your drawings.

The Frame Sequencer to select a drawing to work on. The Frame Sequencer also holds tools for adding, deleting, and copying frames.

Save, Share, and Export

Your animation will be automatically saved in the Gallery.

To share and export your animation:

  1. Go back to the Gallery on the Do Ink app.
  2. Locate your finished animation.
  3. Click on the white square with an arrow coming out of the top. Select Share.
  4. Click on Video and select the size you want. When you choose the size, think about where you will post your video and how much storage space you have. The 720x720 size will take up the most space, but it will look better if you plan to show your video full screen.
  5. You can find your saved video in your Photos app.
