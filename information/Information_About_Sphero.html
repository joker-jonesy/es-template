What is Sphero BOLT?

Sphero BOLT is a programmable robot with sensors and mechanical parts. Sphero BOLT can be controlled by drawing or be programmed using blocks or text code to roll, show animations on its LED matrix, do tricks, respond to sensor data, and even play games. 

How Do You Use Sphero BOLT?

Create a Sphero Edu App Account

You will use the Sphero Edu app to control your robot. If you do not already have one, create an account on Sphero Edu. 

Getting Started

  1. Make sure your Sphero BOLT is charged. You will see a solid white light on the charger when it is fully charged.

  2. Log into the Sphero Edu app on your device. 

  3. Tap the "Connect Robot" button and select your robot to connect to your Sphero BOLT. (Your device ID is written just below the little white Sphero logo on the internal hardware of the Sphero BOLT.)

  4. Select the Activities icon on the bottom of your device screen.   

  5. Select the Sphero tab at the top of the screen to see all available activities developed by Sphero.

Block Programming

One way to program Sphero BOLT is with code blocks. Each block represents a line of code. These blocks of code snap together like a line of puzzle pieces. By connecting blocks of code, you create a computer program and tell your robot what to do.

Create and Save Your Program

To create a new program:

  1. Click the Programs tab.
  2. Select + Create. 
  3. Name your program. Then, choose your program type and your robot.
  4. Click Create.  

Your program will save automatically as you are working on it. To access your program later click the Programs tab, then select My Programs.
