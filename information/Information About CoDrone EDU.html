What is CoDrone EDU?

CoDrone EDU is a quadcopter drone, which means it has four arms and each arm has its own rotor. In the diagrams pictured below, you can see the four propellers surrounded by a protective frame. You can fly the CoDrone manually or you can program it using Blockly—a block-based visual programming editor—or Python, a programming language.

Getting Started With CoDrone EDU

It is highly recommended that you complete the Getting Started Course or read the Quickstart Guide booklet even if you are an experienced drone piloting and/or programmer. In addition to covering safety, the specific functions of this drone, and programming and piloting basics, you will learn how to care for, maintain, and safely complete a software update your drone.  

NOTE: Be sure to read all sections marked with an exclamation mark carefully since these are tips that will help you keep your drone and batteries working properly 

Learning the safety tips is especially important, both to protect the people in the classroom, and for the drone itself.
