What is Gravity Maze?

Gravity Maze is a gravity powered puzzle game. You use the force of gravity and tower playing pieces to get a ball to fall from a specified Start Position to the Target Tower.

How Do You Use Gravity Maze?

Gravity in Gravity Maze

Gravity Maze uses gravity and towers with ramps to guide a ball down from the Start Position to the Target Tower. If there is an obstacle (like a ramp) in the way, a ball cannot fall straight down. However, if the obstacle is on an angle, the ball will roll down toward the lower side since gravity is still pulling the ball down. By moving past the towers so that the ramps connect, you can guide the ball down to the Target Tower.

Kit Contents

The kit comes with a game grid, different sized building towers, marbles, and challenge cards.

How to Play

  1. Choose a challenge card. You will want to start with beginner, but eventually, you can also try intermediate, advanced, or expert.
  2. On the card, you will see the Start Position (represented by a colored tower icon with a white dot inside) and the red Target Tower. You may also see more towers represented on the card. Add the towers you see to your grid. Make sure they are in the right spots and facing the right directions. Dot markings help you figure out which way the towers should face.
  3. Look under Add to Grid. This shows you which additional towers you will need to add to your grid in order to find the solution. Colored squares represent vertical towers. Colored rectangles represent horizontal towers.
  4. The Solution to the card is on the back. But try not to look! Challenge yourself to solve the card on your own.
  5. If you cannot figure out the solution, try solving a simpler card. The cards are numbered.
