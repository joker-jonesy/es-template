What is Lux Blox?

Your Lux Blox kit is a construction set that allows you to build different shapes and solids.  You can even create designs that bend, flex, and move.

How Do You Use Lux Blox?

Lux Squares and Trigons

Lux Blox have two main pieces: a square-shaped piece called Lux Squares and a triangle-shaped piece called a Trigon. These pieces can be connected to create many different shapes and designs.

Dimple and Nest

The dimple is the ball-shaped part of the Lux Squares and Trigons. They click into the empty space or "nest" on the other pieces.

Taking Pieces Apart

Lux Blox snap together easily and hold strong so taking them apart can be hard. If your Lux Blox is too hard to pull apart, try placing a pencil into the center hole to help the piece pop off. Try not to twist the pieces to avoid breaking off the dimples.
