Information About iMovie

What is iMovie?

iMovie is a video editing app. In the app, you can add photos and videos to create a movie or longer video. The app has tools that let you edit the video clips and photos in your movie. The editing tools allow you to:

  • Crop, or cut off parts of, videos and pictures
  • Rotate video clips
  • Stabilize (make less shaky) videos
  • Add video transitions
  • Change the speed of clips.

iMovie also has options to change the audio, or sound. You can get rid of background noise and increase the sound of quiet clips.

How Do You Use iMovie?

The iMovie App

iMovie is an app that can be used on iPads and iMac computers. You can record video, take pictures, edit the videos or pictures, and more.

Parts of iMovie

When you first click the iMovie icon, it takes you to the projects page. This is where you can see iMovie projects that have been started or completed.

When you click the plus button and then the word Movie, iMovie displays the moments and media page. Under Moments, you can view media that was taken or recorded with iMovie on this iPad. 

Under Media, you can see all images or videos on the iPad. When you click Create Movie under Moments, iMovie will show, or display, the project interface. On the top is the media viewer section, where you can view the video clip or image. 
The bottom section is the timeline where you can add a video or a picture. The timeline is also where you will edit the video.

Editing Videos in iMovie

There are several ways to edit a video clip in iMovie. In this project, we will focus on three editing tools.

Select each item to learn more.

Split Clips

To split a video clip:

  1. Add the video clip to the timeline. 
  2. Click the clip. iMovie will show a menu at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Choose a part of the clip to split or cut. 
  4. Put that part on the white line in the middle of the timeline. 
  5. Click Split. 
  6. You will now have 2 smaller clips. 
  7. Delete the one you do not want (trashcan in the right corner).

Filters

To add a filter:

  1. Add the video clip to the timeline.
  2. Click the clip. 
  3. iMovie displays at the bottom of the screen. 
  4. Click the word Filters. 
  5. Choose a filter. 
  6. Play the clip to see if that filter is what you want. 

Titles

To add a title:

  1. With your project open, tap a video clip or photo in the timeline to reveal the inspector at the bottom of the screen.
  2. Tap the Titles button.
  3. Tap to select a title.
    To turn off titles, tap None.
  4. To change where the title appears on screen, tap the sample title in the viewer, then drag it where you want it to appear.
  5. To edit the title, tap the sample title, tap Edit, then type a new title.
  6. Tap Done on the keyboard.

Note: iMovie displays a photo or video clip with a title in the timeline, with a T icon in the upper-left corner.

Saving a Project

Save your project to your device so you can add it to your portfolio. Click the Share button to save the video to your iPad.

To transfer the video to your computer:

  • If you have a Mac, airdrop the video to your computer.
  • If you have a PC, connect the cable, locate the iPad in File Explorer, and drag the iMovie where you would like to save it.

Explore On Your Own

To start recording a video clip with iMovie, follow these steps:

  1. Click the iMovie icon on the iPad (purple).
  2. Click the plus button Create a project. If there is not a plus button, click Done and Project until you see a plus button.
  3. Click the word Movie.
  4. Click the blue words Create Movie.
  5. Click the camera icon.
  6. Start recording your first video clip.
