The Challenge - K'NEX: Design and Test a Spinning Top

The Challenge

Your challenge is to build a spinning top that will spin for the longest time possible (15 seconds or more). Collect and analyze your data using place value to determine which design allowed your top to spin the longest.

Have you ever thought about how a spinner works? What keeps spinning tops from falling over?

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN build a K’NEX spinning top that spins for 15 seconds or longer.
  • I CAN demonstrate conservation of angular momentum. 
  • I CAN compare the data from spinning my top using >, =, and < symbols.
  • I CAN read and write multi-digit numbers using base-ten numerals, number names, and expanded form.

Stuff I'll Need

  • K'NEX Simple Machines Deluxe Kit
  • K'NEX Instruction Manual
  • Stopwatch
  • Data Table
