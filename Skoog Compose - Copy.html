Skoog: Compose

Your Challenge 

Your challenge is to use the Skoog to play musical notes then compose a song using your Skoog. The Skoog is a customizable electronic musical instrument and programmable control device.

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about using music notation and scales
  • Students will learn how to combine music notes to compose a song.

Stuff You'll Need

  • Skoog
  • SmartLab iPad (located in the Digital Communications folder)
