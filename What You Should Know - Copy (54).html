Get to Know Your Robot: Ozobot

Ozobot moves and changes color. It also responds to codes that control these and other functions.

Its controller has been programmed to read color codes, but you can also write your own programs for Ozobot.

How Ozobot Uses Line and Color

Ozobot has five sensors underneath it, which it uses to sense color. Once you turn on Ozobot, it will begin to search for color. If it senses color, it will begin to move in search of lines (paths) to follow. 

Line-Drawing Practice

If you choose to use Ozobot with markers, keep in mind that it likes to follow thick lines (1/4 inch). Warm up by drawing lines for Ozobot to follow.

How Engineers Solve Problems

Engineers solve problems. 

First, they write technical requirements to define what a solution must be able to do. Then, they write design specifications to identify how their solution should solve the problem. Next, they do research, design a solution, build it, and document the instructions so they could build it again later. This is all part of the Engineering Design Process (EDP).

The Engineering Design Process is a method engineers use to develop solutions to problems.
