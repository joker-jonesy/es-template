Budget Friendly Comic! 

Now that you learned to create a comic regarding a disagreement between two opinions and what it could cost to print and ship them, your next challenge is to work backwards. A newspaper company gives you a payment of $220. They want you to create another comic about the other side of the disagreement being the better option, print it, and mail it back to them with half of the amount they gave you. The other half of the amount is your payment. How many copies will you be able to print and send with the amount of money given?

Don’t Say It! 

Did you know that you don’t always have to say something for people to understand what you are trying to say? Make a comic about anything you want. Instead of telling the comic in words, use the least amount of words you can! That’s right! Use almost no words; instead, try to tell your story in pictures and images. By adding more details in your images, people can look at a comic strip of just images and understand what is going on in the story.
