What is Comic Life?

Comic Life is an app that can be used across many subjects. It is a creative way you can turn a simple task in science, writing, or even solving math problems into a poster or comic.  Comic Life is a great way to incorporate visual representation of your knowledge.

How Do You Use Comic Life?

Choosing a Blank Template

By choosing a blank template, you will be creating a template completely from scratch. In the first screen that opens when you enter Comic Life, select Blank on the template page. You can choose to start with or without a specific style.

Learn about comics by visiting DC Comics Website.

Changing the Style/Background

This is using the blank template with styles.

  • To see the entire page, select the percentage at the bottom left, and then click Fit Page.
  • You can choose any of the styles provided on the left. This will automatically appear.
  • If you want more options, click Filt, where more designs and colors are provided.

Different Layouts

Comic Life contains a variety of options when it comes to layouts. When you click the Layouts tab, you can see different ways that comic boxes can be laid out.  Every page can have a different layout. If you do choose one of these layouts, you can still add or remove boxes.

Select each item to learn more.

To Add a Premade Layout to Your Screen:

  • Select the layout you want.
  • Click and drag the layout image to the main screen where you are working.

For Additional Pages:

  • Select the New Page drop-down menu in the top bar.
  • Choose either a blank new page or a similar page.
  • You can change the layout and boxes on each comic page.

Explore On Your Own

Use a blank template in Comic Life to create a short comic about two characters telling each other what they ate for breakfast.
Steps:

  1. Choose a style or background for your template.
  2. Choose a layout for your comic.
  3. Add or remove boxes you may need.
  4. Find/create images, then save and upload them into Comic Life.
  5. Add speech bubbles and other details.
