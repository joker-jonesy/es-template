The Challenge - K'NEX STEM Simple Machines: Build Machines with Gears

The Challenge

Gears are found everywhere, including in bicycles, cars, toys, vacuums, and clocks. If you have ever ridden a bicycle up a hill, you know that gears help you get to the top. But how do gears make the task easier? 

Your challenge is to use K'NEX STEM to explore gears and build your own gear machine. First, you will review attributes of shapes as you sort K’NEX pieces. Then, you will apply this concept to identify similar and congruent gears as you explore how the gears work—or won’t work—together.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN build a machine with gears that work similarly to a real machine.
  • I CAN explain how different types of gears fit together to do work.
  • I CAN describe the attributes of triangles and quadrilaterals.
  • I CAN describe the difference between similarity and congruence.

Stuff I'll Need

  • K'NEX STEM Introduction to Simple Machines: Gears Kit
  • K'NEX STEM Introduction to Simple Machines: Gears Instruction Manual
