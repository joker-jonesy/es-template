Using Pixie

Have you used Pixie before?

Do you know how to:

  • Create and save new projects?
  • Use Pixie paint tools like the brush, shape, fill and eraser?
  • Use stickers and graphics from the internet?
  • Create and edit text?

If so, you are ready for this Liftoff Challenge! If you answered no, or are not sure, ask your Facilitator how to proceed. Remember, you can always find great resources for using Pixie by going to Recipes4Success."Recipes" are projects to help you learn Pixie. "Snacks" are tips and tricks for using Pixie.

Recycle City

Explore Recycle City.  Visit the different sections of the city and click on resources that teach about the 3 R's. Choose one of the topics that interests you most. Plan a comic that will teach others about the topic. You will create this comic in Pixie.

   

3 R's Comic Challenge

Are you ready to create your own comic about the 3 R’s? Hopefully, you came up with some great ideas while exploring Recycle City.

Discuss possible story ideas with your partner. Go online to research more ways to reduce, reuse, and recycle if you still need an idea for your comic. 

Before launching Pixie, complete a storyboard for your project similar to the one shared below.  Include the goal of the comic.

Save Your Project

When you are done creating all of your panels in Pixie , save your project as images for your project submission slide presentation. To learn how to do this, go to Publishing Your Comic in the What You Should Know section.

Be sure to explain how you chose the idea for your comic. You can print your comic at home or in the SmartLab with your Facilitator's permission. Or, REDUCE your use of paper by keeping it as a digital comic. You will upload your comic in your project submission.
