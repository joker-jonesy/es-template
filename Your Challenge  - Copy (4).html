K'NEX Simple Machines: Levers

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to learn how simple machines work by building levers with K’NEX. 

What do seesaws, wheelbarrows, and scissors have in common? They are all simple machines that use levers to make work easier. Having a friend lift you off of the ground on a see-saw is much easier (and more fun) than trying to jump that high by yourself.

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about the types of simple machines.
  • Students will learn more about the classes of levers and how they help you do work..

Stuff You'll Need

  • K'NEX Simple Machines Deluxe Collection
  • K'NEX Levers Instruction Manual
