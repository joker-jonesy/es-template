Do It!

Add a Buzzer or Motor

  1. Use two pieces of conductive dough.
  2. Build a basic closed circuit.
  3. Instead of an LED, use a motor or buzzer for your resistor.
  4. Switch the circuit on using the battery pack.
  5. Switch the circuit off using the battery pack when finished. 

Add a Switch 

Your challenge is to add a switch to your basic closed circuit so you can control the flow of electricity to your motor or buzzer. 

The official name for the switch included in the Squishy Circuits kit is called a Single Pole Single Throw Switch (SPST). It is named after its job which is to switch a single circuit on and off.

  1. Use the basic circuit you built with a motor or buzzer. 
  2. Add another piece of conductive dough.
  3. Connect the single pole single throw switch to the conductive dough and matching wires. Make sure it is in the off position (0)      
  4. Make sure you turn the battery pack to the on position. 
  5. Flip the switch to the on position (1) and watch your motor run or listen to you buzzer beep.
