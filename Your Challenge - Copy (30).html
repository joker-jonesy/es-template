Ozobot: Program a Lawn Mower

Your Challenge

Robots Inc. needs YOU!

The Robots Inc. team of mechanical and electrical engineers has designed and built lots of robots capable of many things.

They need YOU to be their software engineers and write the software programs that tell the robots how to move and communicate with their users.

Your challenge is to program a robot lawn mower to go over a whole lawn and to use light effects to show when it is mowing. 

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about the characteristics of robots.
  • Students will learn about writing code and programming robots for specific tasks.

Stuff You'll Need

  • Ozobot Starter Kit
  • Colored paper
  • Ozoblockly
