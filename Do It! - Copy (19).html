Create a Luminary Using Fab@School Maker Studio

Pick a Constellation

For more choices, research more constellations and choose one that you like.

Create the Design

Use Fab@School Maker Studio to create the constellation you chose.

  1. Log into Fab@School Maker Studio.
  2. Start a New Project.
  3. Choose Letter paper size and leave "None" in Registration Marks. Click OK.
  4. Use the circle or star shape to place the stars in the constellation.
  5. Use the drawing tool connect the stars and create an outline of the constellation.

Print the Design

  1. Click on the print button.
  2. Select the options for Print Cut & Fold Lines and Remove Color Fill.
  3. Click Save to PDF.
  4. Open the PDF and print to a printer in your classroom or school.

Construct Your Luminary

  1. You can use scissors or a hole punch to cut out the stars/holes in your constellation. 
  2. Tape or glue your design to a paper bag, or tape the sides together to form a cylinder.
  3. Place a flashlight, candle, or battery-powered tealight inside your luminary to project the constellation.
