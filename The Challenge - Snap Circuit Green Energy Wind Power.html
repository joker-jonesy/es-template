The Challenge - Snap Circuits Green Energy: Wind Power Data Collection

The Challenge

Your challenge is to learn about alternative energy systems and wind power. Then build your own wind powered circuits! You will keep track of the voltage your windmill generates in different positions and record the data on a line plot. Then you will use the plot to analyze your results.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN create and record data on a line plot.
  • I CAN use a line plot to interpret data and find interesting outcomes.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Snap Circuits Green Energy Collection
  • Snap Circuits Green Alternative Energy Instruction Manual
