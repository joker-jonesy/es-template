The Challenge - Padcaster: A New Take on Weather Forecasts! (L1)

The Challenge

Your challenge is to create a new take on a local weather report. Share with your classmates how they can prepare and respond to upcoming weather changes. Include positive and negative numbers on a thermometer to provide further evidence of upcoming temperatures. 

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN use Padcaster equipment and the Padcaster Producer app to create an original weather forecast for my classmates.
  • I CAN identify and use integers and its opposites to represent quantities of a real-life weather forecast.
  • I CAN display integers on a vertical number line (thermometer) and explain where zero fits into weather reports.
  • I CAN write, interpret, and explain the order of rational numbers within a real-life weather forecast scenario.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Padcaster Studio Kit
  • iPad with Padcaster Producer App
  • Padcaster Quick Start Guide
