The Challenge - Google Maps: National Parks Brochure

The Challenge

You are planning a summer road trip for your family to visit your favorite national parks.  

Your challenge is to create a brochure for a family trip to five national parks in the United States. Your brochure will include distance and time between locations, cost of gas, and any additional information you would like to include.

What I'll Learn

  • I CAN identify Earth’s features at national parks.
  • I CAN draw conclusions about Earth’s features.
  • I CAN solve problems involving multiplication using number lines to calculate the cost of gas.
  • I CAN communicate relevant travel information in a brochure.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Google My Maps
  • Google Account
  • Paper (3 pieces)
