Lego WeDo 1.0: Hungry Robots!

Your Challenge 

Your challenge is to build the Hungry Alligator robot and program it to open and close its mouth. Once you do that, change the program so the alligator learns a new trick.

While you build and program you will explore how gears and pulleys are used in machines.

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about combining simple machines to design complex machines.
  • Students will learn about applying the engineering design while designing a model.

Stuff You'll Need

  • LEGO WeDo 1.0 Kit
  • LEGO WeDo software
    • Mac: In your dock
    • PC: All Programs folder
