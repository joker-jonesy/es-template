Information About Geometric Shapes

What is Geometric Shapes?

The Geometric Shapes kit is a building kit that has sticks and balls that connect to create shapes. You can use the Geometric Shapes kit to build and explore 2D and 3D shapes.

How Do You Use Geometric Shapes?

Combine straight sticks in 3 sizes- short, medium, and long with 2 different sized ball connectors to build squares, rectangles and 3D shapes like prisms and cubes. Use the curves to make circles and 3D shapes like cylinders.

The holes around the circumferences of each connector are spaced differently. The red connectors have holes 60 degrees apart, and the orange connectors have holes 45 degrees apart.
