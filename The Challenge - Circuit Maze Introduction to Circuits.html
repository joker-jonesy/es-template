The Challenge - Circuit Maze: Explore Circuits with Decimals and Fractions

The Challenge

Your challenge is to build circuits that light up colored beacons. You will choose challenge cards that tell you which colored beacons to light and which pieces (game tokens) to use. See how many challenges you can solve! Before you get started, you will write decimals as fractions to explore voltages.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN build circuits to light up colored beacons using Circuit Maze.
  • I CAN write decimals as fractions to represent the voltages needed to power small electronics.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Circuit Maze Kit
  • Circuit Maze Instruction Manual
  • Paper and Markers
