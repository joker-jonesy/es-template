Do It!

Choose a Tower

Use the Internet to locate a famous tower. Collect some information on the tower you choose.

  1. What is the name of your tower?
  2. When was the tower built?
  3. How long did it take to complete?
  4. What material is it made out of?
  5. Do humans occupy the tower? What is the job of the tower?
  6. What shapes do you notice in the architectural design?

Build a Tower

After gathering information about the tower of your choice. Make a plan to construct your tower out of Goobi building materials. 

  1. Pick base shape. Think about what the footprint of the building might look like and start with that shape. You can find helpful suggestions on starting your structure in the Goobi Instruction Manual (PDF). 
  2. Decide how you will incorporate details of the tower you choose. Can you see shapes when you look at a picture of the tower?
  3. Construct the tower. Can you make a miniature version of the famous tower you found?
