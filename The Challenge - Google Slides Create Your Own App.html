The Challenge - Google Slides: Create Your Own App

The Challenge

Your challenge is to create your own app using Google Slides. You will create a homepage that features icons that, when clicked, will lead you to other slides. After visiting the other slides, you will create a homepage button for easy return.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN use Google Slides to create an interactive app with links between slides.
  • I CAN use the steps of the engineering design process to design an app.
  • I CAN design an app that features at least three different slides, or screens.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Google Slides 
  • Design Process Sheet
