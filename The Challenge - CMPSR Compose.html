The Challenge - CMPSR: Compose

The Challenge

Your challenge is to use CMPSR to play musical notes, then compose a song using whole numbers and fractional beats. You will add the whole number and fractional beats, and then decompose each line of music into a sum of fractions. CMPSR is a customizable electronic musical instrument and programmable control device.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN use CMPSR to compose my own song.
  • I CAN count beats represented as whole numbers and fractions in musical notes.
  • I CAN compare two fractions.
  • I CAN add fractions with like and unlike denominators.
  • I CAN decompose a fraction as a sum of fractions with the same denominator.

Stuff I'll Need

  • CMPSR
  • SmartLab iPad with the Garageband app
  • CMPSR First Songs
