The Challenge - Snap Circuits Green Energy: Solar Energy and Solar Panels

The Challenge

Your challenge is to design, build, and test electrical circuits that use a solar cell. 

You will collect and store energy using solar power. Then, test to see if the energy you collect can power a motor, lights, and more. After testing your solar model, you will apply the concept of solar cells to solar panels. You will design a roof and draw a plan for covering the roof with solar panels.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN build circuits that are powered by solar energy.
  • I CAN determine the dimensions of a rectangular roof with a given area.
  • I CAN determine the area of a rectangular solar panel.
  • I CAN determine the perimeter of a rectangular solar panel.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Snap Circuits Green Energy Collection
  • Snap Circuits Green Energy Instruction Manual 
  • Graph Paper or Geogebra
