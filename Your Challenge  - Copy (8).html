K'NEX STEM Simple Machines: Levers

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to learn how simple machines work by building levers with K’NEX STEM Building Solution kit.

What do the scissors, the wheelbarrow, and the see-saw have in common?

They are all simple machines that use levers to make work easier. Having a friend lift you off of the ground on a see-saw is much easier - and more fun - than trying to jump that high by yourself.

What You'll Learn 

  • How simple machines work
  • How a lever makes work easier
  • Parts of a lever
  • Different classes of a lever

Stuff You'll Need

  • K'NEX STEM Building Solutions Simple Machines: Levers & Pulleys kit
  • Instruction Manual (PDF)
