Snap Circuit Green Energy: Liquid Battery

Your Challenge  

To make sure we do not run out of energy, we need to find and use renewable or alternative energy sources. 

The energy produced by alternative energy sources (like wind or solar) is most often converted directly into electricity. If this energy is not immediately used while it is being produced, it needs to be stored so we can use it later.

Batteries help store electricity, allowing us to collect renewable energy and use it at more convenient times. Here you will explore batteries and how they release their stored energy.

Your challenge is to design and build an electrical circuit that is powered by a liquid battery. Then, test to see which liquids produce energy. You will use Snap Circuits to design, build, and test your ideas.

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn how batteries store energy.
  • Students will learn how to design, build and test an electrical circuit that is powered by a liquid battery.

Stuff You'll Need

  • Snap Circuits Green Alternative Energy kit
  • Snap Circuits Green Energy Instruction Manual
  • Water, salt, juice, or soda (depends on your project choice)
