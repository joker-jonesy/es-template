The Challenge - VEX GO: Clean Up Your Community

The Challenge

Your challenge is to build a robot and program it to collect as much magnetic metal as possible. There is a shortage of magnetic metal in your city and the mayor has asked you to be on the lookout. The metal you collect will be given to the city to be reused in building a community project.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN design and program a robot to collect magnetic metal.
  • I CAN distinguish between items that are metal and items that are not.
  • I CAN identify ways to help my community and keep the earth healthy.
  • I CAN decompose the programming task to help me create my program.

Stuff I'll Need

  • VEX GO Kit
  • Web-based Programming: VEXcode GO
  • To Update Firmware: VEX Classroom (requires iPad)
  • Metal Items (coins, screws, paperclips, etc.)
  • A Video Recorder (iPad, computer camera, GoPro)
