Important Vocabulary

  • Length - the distance from one location or point to another
  • Width - the distance of the side of an object from one point to another
  • Area - the amount of space a shape takes up
  • Perimeter - the distance around the outside of a shape
  • Square Units - a unit of measurement used to represent the area of a 2-dimensional shape

Perimeter

Perimeter is the distance around the outside of a shape. Using the length and width of a shape, the perimeter can be found. Length is the distance from one location or point to another. Width is the distance of the side of an object from one point to another.

Area

Area is the amount of space a shape takes up. The length and width can also be used to find the area. However, the formula is different because area is a different measurement.

To find the area, the length and width have to be multiplied together. The area is measured in squared units because it represents how many squares can be laid down to equal the entire area.

All of this information can be used in a number of ways. Length and width can be used to find the perimeter and area. Perimeter tells an individual what the distance walked around the outside of the building is. Area tells an individual the amount of walking that would take place inside the building if it were empty. This information can help a family plan which places to go to based on size. If Tanner’s family is spending the day shopping, knowing the distance around the store or from location to location can help them better plan their day. Tanner’s family can calculate the length, width, perimeter, and area to cut back on the amount of time they spend traveling and shopping inside each store. This is just one example of why this information is helpful.

Career Connection and Real-World Application

Land Surveyor

A land surveyor helps determine the size of land plots. Being able to identify the size of a land plot allows for the planning of where to build roads and buildings. The size also impacts the price the land is sold for when it comes time to build. Land surveyors use the same skill known as topography to survey the land.

Architect

Architects design buildings and houses. Architects have to plan based on the weather conditions in the area, type of soil, and several other factors to make sure that a building or house will remain standing. Architects use a skill known as topography to design. Topography is the study of land features and formations (natural and man-made).
