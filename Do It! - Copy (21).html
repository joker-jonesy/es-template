  • Ideally, see how different shapes and colors affect the style of a character.

Create a Character

  • Visit vectr.com and click on New File
  • Above the left toolbar, click on Pages
  • At the bottom, click on the arrow to choose the orientation of your page.  This allows you to choose the way your design shows up on different devices (in the form of social media, YouTube, Letter or Legal size paper, laptop screen sizes, iPhone sizes, etc.). 
    • The Page Settings automatically default to pixels size, if needed you can change it by clicking on File Settings in the Editing Panel and change to inches or centimeters. 

Let your imagination run wild and create your own character.
