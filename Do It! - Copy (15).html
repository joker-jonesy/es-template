Do It!

Build a Series Circuit 

Your first challenge is to build a series circuit and draw the circuit diagram for your series circuit.

  1. Start with a basic closed circuit. 
  2. Add additional LEDs in a series. *If you do not know how to create a complete circuit already go back to the Liftoff Launcher Squish Circuit: Basic Circuits. 
  3. Use the circuit symbols to draw your circuit diagram showing the closed series circuit. 
  4. Share your circuit diagram with a classmate and see if they can recreate it.
