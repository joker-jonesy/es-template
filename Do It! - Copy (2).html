Creating a Compilation

To add a background to your animated object, you will create a composition. 

STEPS:

  1. Watch the tutorial below on Combining Drawings in the Composition Editor by Do Ink.
  2. Open the Animation and Drawing by Do Ink app on the iPad.
  3. Design a background by creating a new drawing. You will only need one frame for the background.
  4. Go back to the gallery.
  5. Click on the plus (+) sign in the top-right corner of the screen. Select "New Composition."
  6. Try what you learned from the Water Cycle video.

Use a drawing tablet if one is available. Watch the videos as many times as you want. You can even pause the videos to practice.

Advanced Animation Ideas

Animate: 

  • Your own original story (keep it simple)
  • A scene from your favorite movie or play
  • A comic strip you found in the newspaper
  • A scene from your favorite book
  • A joke
  • A recipe, such as how to make spaghetti
  • A hobby of yours, such as how to catch a fish
  • A natural cycle, such as changes in the seasons
  • A skill, such as how to choreograph a dance step
  • A dream, like what it might be like to take a rocket to space
