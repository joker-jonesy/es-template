The Challenge – GarageBand: Rhythm of Ratios 

The Challenge

Your challenge is to compose music using Math!  You will compare ratios, identify equivalent ratios, and translate them into a drum beat you play in GarageBand.  

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN represent ratios using fractions and colon notation. 
  • I CAN identify equivalent ratios by comparing the relationship between parts. 
  • I CAN use rhythmic patterns to represent different ratios. 
  • I CAN create and record a musical composition using GarageBand that showcases proportional relationships. 
  • I CAN explain the connection between ratios and the structure of my musical piece. 

Stuff I'll Need

  • GarageBand
