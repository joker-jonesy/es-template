The Challenge - Wixie: Exploring Polygons and Patterns

The Challenge

Your challenge is to use Wixie to draw polygons including rectangles and calculate their perimeter and area. Then make patterns by rotating and reflecting rectangles with polygons. 

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN draw and measure the lengths of the sides of rectangles.
  • I CAN find the perimeter and area of rectangles.
  • I CAN draw rectangles with the same perimeters, but different areas.
  • I CAN draw rectangles with the same areas, but different perimeters.
  • I CAN use rotations and reflections to make patterns.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Wixie Software Program (subscription required)
