Lego WeDo 1.0: Scratch the Alligator

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to combine Scratch and WeDo. You will use Scratch to program the Hungry Alligator. What will you program your alligator to do?

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about how to control a robot through programming.

Stuff You'll Need

  • LEGO WeDo 1.0 Kit
  • LEGO WeDo software
    • Mac: In your dock
    • PC: All Programs folder
  • Scratch software
      • Mac: In your dock
      • PC: All Applications folder
