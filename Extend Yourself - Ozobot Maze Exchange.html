 Add Ozocodes to Your Maze

Ozocodes are color sequences made of two, three, or four colored dots. They appear as colored blinking lights on the iPad, but they appear as dots in a row on paper. Ozobot’s sensors read these color sequences and react based on how it was programmed.

 How Many Quadrilaterals Can You Make?

Create a maze using as many quadrilaterals as you can. Create instructions to guide a partner through re-creating your maze. Focus on the shapes’ attributes and how they can be categorized in your instructions.

 Interpreting Data

Create a large line plot by combining the data from multiple graphs. Work with two other groups and combine the data from all three line plots. What do you notice about the data? Can the data be organized in this way?

 More Maze Activity Ideas

  • Add dead ends and obstacles to your original maze design.
  • What other creative mazes could you make for Ozobot?
  • Write design specifications for a new maze. Your team and at least one other should design mazes to meet the specifications. Compare designs.
