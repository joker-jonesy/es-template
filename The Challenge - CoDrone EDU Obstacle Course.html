The Challenge - CoDrone EDU: Obstacle Course

The Challenge

Your challenge is to design an obstacle course using a coordinate plane. Then, build the course and program CoDrone EDU to fly the path while avoiding added obstacles.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN draw and label coordinates on a coordinate plane.
  • I CAN figure out the distance between two coordinates that share an x or y coordinate. [e.g. (5,2) and (5,6) are 4 units apart.]
  • I CAN use Blockly, a block-based visual programming editor, to program CoDrone EDU to fly in specific patterns, including up and down to avoid obstacles.
  • I CAN use Blockly conditionals (IF/THEN logic) to program CoDrone EDU to fly in certain patterns based on conditions.

Stuff I'll Need

  • CoDrone EDU Drone
  • CoDrone EDU Controller
  • Getting Started Course
  • Blockly with CoDrone EDU Course
  • Blockly for Robolink
  • Masking Tape or Painter’s Tape
  • Graph Paper
