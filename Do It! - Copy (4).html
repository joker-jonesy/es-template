Do It!

Create an advanced animation with Advanced-ish.

Get Advanced-ish!

  1. Start the Animation-ish software.
  2. To log in, ask your Facilitator for the login and use the password: create123!
  3. Go to Animation-ish lessons here. 
  4. Watch Lessons 3, 4, and 5.
  5. Go back to the Animation-ish application and select Advanced-ish. The workspace will open.
  6. Use what you learned in the lessons to make your own animation with Advanced-ish.

Use a drawing tablet if one is available. Watch the videos as many times as you want. You can even pause the videos to practice. 

Advanced-ish Ideas

Animate: 

  • Your own original story (keep it simple)
  • A scene from your favorite movie or play
  • A comic strip you found in the newspaper
  • A scene from your favorite book
  • A joke
  • A photograph (pick a photograph you like, import it into Animation-ish, trace it, and add movement)
  • A recipe, such as how to make spaghetti
  • A hobby of yours, such as how to catch a fish
  • A natural cycle, such as changes in the seasons
  • A skill, such as how to choreograph a dance step
  • A dream, like what it might be like to take a rocket to space
