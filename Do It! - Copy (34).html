Do It!

Do levers really make work easier? Build some to find out. 

Your challenge is to build models of lever machines and then explore how they are used to make work easier.

Building Levers with K’NEX

Steps to complete this challenge:

  1. Make sure you know how to use the K'NEX parts. 
  2. Warm up by building and testing a simple lever. 
  3. If you want to warm up some more, build another basic machine.
  4. Choose an advanced machine to build and/or invent your own machine that has a lever as part of the design.

  5. For all of your projects, label the parts of your lever and explain which lever class your model shows. Go to the Three Classes of Levers in the What You Should Know section to review lever types.  

Download the K'NEX Levers Instruction Manual here.

Warm Up Exercise

Build a simple K’NEX lever like the one shown here. Point out the parts of the lever.

Test Your Lever

Press on the end of the short blue rod and notice how much pressure it takes to lift the weight on the other end. Then replace the blue rod with a longer gray rod and repeat the experiment.

 

Reflect

  • What did you notice? 
  • Did it take more or less pressure to lift the weight with the longer gray rod? 
  • Which rod did you have to push down farther to reach the table? 
  • What do you think is happening here?
