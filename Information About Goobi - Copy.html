What is Goobi?

Goobi is a magnetic construction set.  Using an assortment of balls, bars, and tripods you can design a variety of shapes, solids, towers, and more.

How Do You Use Goobi?

The Goobi building set has three parts- balls, bars, and tripods.

The bars have magnets on both ends, one positive and one negative. The magnets can be attached to each other or to the nickel plated steel balls. You can connect up to 12 bars to one ball. The tripods hold in place up to three bars, they are used to add strength when building 3D shapes.
