Do It!

Your challenge is to use a Vernier motion sensor in experiments that use sound to measure moving objects. 

Using the Go!Motion Sensor

The experiments in this Liftoff Challenge use the Vernier Go!Motion sensor.

If you have not used this sensor before, start by practicing with the Go!Motion Sensor Practice Activity.

Go!Motion Experiments

Use the Vernier Go!Motion sensor to do some experiments with echolocation. Start with the experiment that interests you the most. Answer each of the questions on the Vernier activity sheets.

Presentation

Use a presentation tool like PowerPoint or Google Slides to create a presentation demonstrating what you learned about motion and echolocation measurements.

Be sure to include:

  • Screenshots of your work in Logger Lite
  • Your data, graphs, and other experimental results
  • Photos or movies of your experiment in action
  • Your observations during the experiment(s)
  • What you learned from the experiment(s)

What new questions do you have about motion or echolocation? 

Think about how you could change one of the above experiments to answer some of your questions, or how you might create your own experiment from scratch.
