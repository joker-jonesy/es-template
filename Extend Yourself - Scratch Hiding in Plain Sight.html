Gamify Your Animation

Turn your animation into a game! Your friends can learn and have fun at the same time.

  • Program the animation so that players score points for clicking on hidden geometrical figures.
  • Add a timer so players have to find concealed objects in a specific amount of time.
  • Level up your animation by adding additional levels for players to complete.
