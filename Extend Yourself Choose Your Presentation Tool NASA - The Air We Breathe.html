Extend Yourself - Choose Your Presentation Tool: Adding and Subtracting Air Molecules

 Learn More About Air

Still curious about air?

  • Use the internet to find answers to specific questions.
  • Use the internet to find images about air and atmosphere.
  • Make a list of ways you use air each day. For example, you may use air in a bike tire to inflate it.
  • Find out how scientists study the air around us. Did you know that scientists use balloons to send weather instruments into the atmosphere?

 Make Air!

On the last page of The Air We Breathe, there is a recipe for creating CO2. Make the recipe at home, recording what happens to the balloon after adding the vinegar. How much vinegar would you need to make the recipe for both you and a friend? For you and two other friends? Support your answer by showing your math work.
