What Is Frames Software?

Frames is a software application that lets you tell your story with clay animation, stop-motion, and cartoons.
Explore Frames and learn to:

  • Use drawing tools to create your own illustrations.
  • Clone shapes and frames to build cartoons.
  • Add images from a camera.
  • Add backgrounds to your frames.
  • Add narration and background sounds.
  • Add text, titles, and transitions.

What is Stop-Motion Animation?

Stop-motion animation is a great way to tell a story. Animation means to be full of life. Stop-motion animation is a process that brings your character to life one frame at a time.

What Is a Frame?

A frame is one picture in a series of pictures that make up a movie. Take a look at the example on the left. There are 10 frames or pictures of Mary dancing. We measure the frame rate in frames per second (fps). This means that, every second, a certain number of pictures are shown. If we showed all 10 frames or pictures of Mary dancing in one second, we would have 10 fps.

How Does it Work?

Objects are moved between frames and when the frames are played back in order, the character appears to be moving and is brought to life. We said earlier that there are 10 pictures of Mary. Each picture shows Mary in a different position. If we show each picture of Mary one after the next, we can make Mary look like she is actually moving. More frames per second make the movements look smoother and harder to distinguish. Fewer frames can make the animation look choppier.
