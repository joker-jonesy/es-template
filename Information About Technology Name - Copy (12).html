What is Padcaster?

The Padcaster Studio Kit is a set of audio-visual recording equipment. The equipment includes an iPad, tripod, monopod, microphone kit, teleprompter, angle lens, green screen, and more. This equipment is used to create a variety of videos from vlogs to films to instructional content.

How Do You Use Padcaster?

Set up the Padcaster Equipment

Please note that instructions for assembly should be included with your Padcaster equipment. Be careful when you use the equipment because parts of it are fragile.

  1. Find the tripod and open up the legs. There are three settings for the height and latches to lock them into place. The center column also adjusts up and down. The handle can be attached to the side.
  2. Insert iPad into the Padcaster iPad case
  3. Be sure that the lens of the iPad is matched up to the opening for the lens on the Padcaster iPad case.
  4. Attach the Padcaster iPad case to the tripod/monopod
  5. Choose the lens you would like to use and attach it to the Padcaster iPad case (if desired).
  6. Attach the microphone to the top of the Padcaster iPad case. 
  7. Attach the light (if desired)
  8. If you did not attach a lens, you can attach the teleprompter (if desired).

Padcaster Producer App Guide

The following Padcaster Quick Start Guide gives details about the effects, tools, and how to use the Padcaster Producer app.

Export Your Video in Padcaster Producer App

To export your video when it is finished:

  1. Click the icon in the bottom left corner.
  1. On the next screen, click the video you want to export.
  2. Click the button in the top left with a rectangle and arrow combined.
  1. Choose email or file sharing. Ask your teacher to know which option to choose.

Explore On Your Own

Padcaster Producer App

Using just the Padcaster Producer app on the iPad, complete the following tasks. This activity does not require all of the equipment to be set up at this time. Refer to the Padcaster Producer App Guide for guidance with these steps.

Record a tour of your classroom from your seat.

  1. Explore the Green Screen menu. Get the green screen out, if necessary.
  2. Add a title and change the color to red.
  3. Adjust the Zoom, Pitch, and Tracking Speed.
  4. Adjust the Camera settings.
  5. Reset everything.
