GarageBand: Spooky Sounds

GarageBand is a digital audio workstation for audio recording and editing. 

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to create a spooky story from sounds in the GarageBand app on your iPad. 

What You'll Learn

  • Students will explore sound by experimenting with the instruments in the GarageBand app.
  • Students will put together their own spooky sounds- to create a story that’s told through sound only - no speaking.
  • Students will learn about the basic elements of music – beat, tempo, rhythm, volume, and timbre.

Stuff You'll Need

  • iPad or Mac Computer
  • GarageBand App
  • Microphone (optional)
  • Headphones (optional)
