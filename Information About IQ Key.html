What is IQ Key?

IQ Key is a construction set that allows you to build and explore mechanical machines, which are moving parts that perform specific functions.

IQ Key is see through so you can see and understand how various parts of a machine move and interact.

You can build many types of machines with just one kit. Here are just some examples of the machines you can build with IQ Key. You can find these and other machines in your manual.

How Do You Use IQ Key?

IQ Key Parts and Function

IQ Key includes lots of parts. You can connect parts together to make many different machines.

The Gear Capsule Functions document gives details on the function of each mechanical capsule.

Removing Connectors

Connectors should go together and come apart easily; however, some connectors fit too tightly or too loosely with some capsules. If you have trouble removing any of the connectors, do NOT twist them off. Your kit comes with a wrench. Use the wrench to PULL them off. If a connector is too loose, wrap a small piece of cellophane tape around the connection point to make it tighter.
