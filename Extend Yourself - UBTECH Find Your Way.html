Extend Yourself - UKIT Beginner: Find Your Way

All in One

Look at the coordinate pairs that your robot navigated to. Can you get the robotic car to navigate to each of these again but this time all in one program?  Have the robot take a brief rest at each of the points. 

Get in Shape

Can you program the robotic car to make a square that is 4 units on each side? How about one that makes a triangle? Take a video of your success and a screenshot of your program.

Come Home

Can you program your robot to navigate to a specific point and then return back to the point of origin in one program?

Your Story

Can you program your robotic car to move to coordinate pairs based on a story you create?
