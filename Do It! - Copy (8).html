Become a Superhero

Are you ready to star in your own comic strip?

In this challenge, you and your partner will each pick a super power and create a comic strip about your heroic adventures.

As superheroes, you will use your talents and powers to help people or to solve a problem.

STEPS:

  1. Choose a problem.
  2. Choose a superpower.
  3. Make up a story about solving the problem with the superpower. 
  4. Make a comic about your story.

What Problem Will You Solve?

Think about a problem in the world or a problem that you face every day. Maybe you wish you could...

  • Do all of your homework with just one blink of an eye
  • Fly so you didn't have to walk to school when it is cold outside.
  • Spread joy to those who are sad.
  • Stop bullying at school
  • Put out a forest fire or stop a tornado

What Super Power Would You Need?

Think about a super power that could help solve your problem.  

Maybe you want:

  • Amazing strength
  • Ability to spread joy 
  • Invisibility
  • Super speed
  • Mind control
  • Ability to control weather
  • Super hearing

Create Your Comic Strip

Make up a story about your problem and how you are going to use your super power to solve it. Then, open your Comic Life software to get started. Go to the What You Should Know section to learn about what makes a great comic. Make sure to include all of the characteristics of a comic listed in the What You Should Know section. 

Choose a Template

Once you click on the Comic Life icon to launch the software, click on a template. There are many different categories so be sure to look through a few.

Add Panels

Click on a panel layout. There are several different layouts. Think about your story and the sequence of the story. If a big, important even happens right away, you may want to choose the layout with the largest panel at the top. Drag and drop the layout onto the workspace.

Add Images and Drawings

After selecting a template and a layout for your comic, you will add images, text, and speech bubbles.

You can:

  • Take photos with a camera and save them to your student folder
  • Scan artwork and save it to your student folder
  • Use the Internet to find and save images to your student folder

Tool Bar

The toolbar is where you will be able to change the text, style, colors, and font.
