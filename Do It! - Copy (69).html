Do It!

Your challenge is to build a machine with Lego WeDo bricks. Then program and test your machine to see what it can do.

STEPS:

  1. Choose either the forklift or tower crane.
  2. Open the WeDo software and choose your building project.
  3. Follow the instructions in the software to build your machine.
  4. Program and test your machine to see how it works.
  5. If you have time, invent your own machine invent your own machine.

Time for Some Heavy Lifting

To find out what your machines can handle, start by building one. Choose either the forklift or tower crane. Follow the building instructions in the WeDo software.

Program Your Machine

Drag buttons from the bottom of the screen to the middle of the screen.

To test your machine connect it to the computer and press the play button.

Test Capacity

Now that you know your machine functions, it’s time to figure out its capabilities.

Do these tests to see what your machine can do:

  1. Try lifting objects of different sizes and shapes. What is the largest object your machine can lift? Tallest? Widest? Thinnest? Thickest? Record your observations.
  2. Once you collect all of this data, can you figure out ways to change your machine’s design so it can lift more? Test your changes one at a time. Can your machine now handle heavier objects? Taller objects? Longer object? Be specific in your records.
