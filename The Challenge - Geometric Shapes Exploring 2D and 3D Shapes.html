The Challenge - Geometric Shapes: Explore 2D Shapes and Area

The Challenge

Your challenge is to build and explore the properties of 2D shapes. Then, build different sized rectangles and find the area of each one.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN make a 2D shape with Geometric Shapes.
  • I CAN make polygons with Geometric Shapes.
  • I CAN measure the lengths of the sides of rectangles to the nearest whole inch and whole centimeter.
  • I CAN find the area of a rectangle by multiplying the side lengths.

Stuff I'll Need

  • Geometric Shapes Building Kit
  • Ruler (with inches and centimeters)
