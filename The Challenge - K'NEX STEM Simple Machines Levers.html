The Challenge - K'NEX STEM Simple Machines: Build and Measure with Levers

The Challenge

Your challenge is to learn how simple machines work by building levers with the K’NEX STEM Building Solution kit. 

What do the scissors, the wheelbarrow, and the see-saw have in common?

They are all simple machines that use levers to make work easier. Having a friend lift you off of the ground on a seesaw is much easier—and more fun—than trying to jump that high by yourself.

Once you learn about levers, you will use K'NEX to build a pan balance model, and use the model to determine the weight of five objects.

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN describe how simple machines work.
  • I CAN describe how a lever makes work easier.
  • I CAN identify the parts of a lever.
  • I CAN define different classes of a lever.
  • I CAN determine the weight of objects using only a ten-gram weight and a pan balance.

Stuff I'll Need

  • K’NEX STEM Building Solutions Simple Machines: Levers & Pulleys Kit
  • K'NEX STEM Simple Machines Levers Instruction Manual
  • Ruler, 5 small objects (paper clips, erasers, bottle caps, etc.), 1-10 gram weight
  • K'NEX Levers Guess the Weight Table (PPT) or K'NEX Levers Guess the Weight Table (PDF)
  • PhET Balancing Act Webpage
  • Pan Balance - Shapes Website
