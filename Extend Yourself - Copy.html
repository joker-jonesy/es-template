Extend Yourself

  • Compose your own original song. Write the color pattern so that your peers can play your song. If you know how to read music, try converting a song with musical notes to a song with color notes. 
  • Change the settings to see how it changes the sounds. Change the scales and keys in the settings and try to play a familiar song like Old Macdonald. Does it sound different?
  • Download the free Skoog Scratch app. The Skoog Scratch app allows you to record your own sounds for the colors of the Skoog.
