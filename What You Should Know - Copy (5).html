The Power of Video

Video is a powerful way to communicate ideas. Video combines pictures, sound, and movement. When we read something, we use our sight. When someone tells us something, we use our hearing. By using both senses, videos can help us learn better.

Public Service Announcements

Have you ever heard of public service announcement (PSA’s)? You have probably seen them on television or heard them on the radio.

Public service announcements are similar to advertisements, but instead of advertising a product, they inform or educate people about an important topic like health or safety.
