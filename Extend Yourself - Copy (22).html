Working Backwards With Challenge Cards

Choose a more difficult Challenge Card. After spending time trying to solve the card, look at the solution and see if you can figure out how the tower should be built. Which parts of the towers are being utilized? Are there sections of towers involved that you did not even notice before?  Does the ball roll through the same tower more than once? If so, how does that work?
