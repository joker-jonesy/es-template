Lego WeDo 1.0: Gearing Up!

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to build a “Smart Spinner” robot to spin a top, and then turn off the motor once the top is released.

Once you figure that out, try building the “smartest” spinner: see if you can get your top to spin the fastest and longest.

  

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about the components of robots.
  • Students will learn about using simple machines called gears.

Stuff You'll Need

  • LEGO WeDo 1.0 Kit
  • LEGO WeDo software
    • PC: All Programs folder
