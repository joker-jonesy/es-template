The Challenge - iMovie: All About Us

The Challenge

Your challenge is to use iMovie to create two short videos, one about you and one about your partner. Once your videos are complete, plan a movie viewing party with the class. 

What I'll Learn 

  • I CAN use different camera angles and explain why I chose a specific angle.
  • I CAN create a video to clearly communicate information to an audience.
  • I CAN use the editing tools and explain how I used them to make my video better.
  • I CAN use multiplication to solve word problems. I can use equations and drawings to determine the unknown number and solve the problem.

Stuff I'll Need

  • iPad
  • iMovie App
  • Movie Viewing Party Schedule
  • Storyboard Template
