Squishy Circuits: Circuit Basics

Your Challenge

Your challenge is to complete the circuit and light an LED using the Squishy Circuits kit. 

What You'll Learn

  • Students will learn about electricity and electrical current. 
  • Students will learn about the difference between closed and open circuits. 
  • Students will learn about the causes of short circuits.
  • Students will learn about the differences between conductors and insulators.

Stuff You'll Need

  • Squishy Circuits Kit
