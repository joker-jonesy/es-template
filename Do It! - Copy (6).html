Do It!

Use Pixie to create a comic book about a 3 R topic of your choice. 

STEPS:

  1. Explore Recycle City and choose a topic for your comic.
  2. Sketch your ideas and then create your comic. 
  3. Save Your comic as images so you can include it in your ePortfolio.

Using Pixie

Have you used Pixie before?

Do you know how to:

  • Create and save new projects?
  • Use Pixie paint tools like the brush, shape, fill and eraser?
  • Use stickers and graphics from the internet?
  • Create and edit text?

Remember, you can always find great resources for using Pixie by clicking Recipes4Sucess under the help menu. "Recipes" are projects to help you learn Pixie. "Snacks" are tips and tricks for using Pixie. 

Recycle City

Explore Recycle City. Visit the different sections of the city and click on resources that teach about the 3 R's. Choose one of the topics that interests you most. Plan a comic that will teach others about the topic. You will create this comic in Pixie.

3 R’s Comic Challenge

Are you ready to create your own comic about the 3 R’s? Hopefully, you came up with some great ideas while exploring Recycle City.

Discuss possible story ideas with your partner. Go online to research more ways to reduce, reuse, and recycle if you still need an idea for your comic.  

Sketch your ideas on paper, and then launch Pixie to start creating. 

Save Your Project

When you are done creating all of your panels, save your project as images for your ePortfolio slide presentation.

Be sure to explain how you chose the idea for your comic. You can print your comic at home or in the SmartLab with your Facilitator's permission. Or, REDUCE your use of paper by keeping it as a digital comic.
