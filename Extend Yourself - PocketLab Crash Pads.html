Convert Your Results

Centimeters, millimeters, meters, and kilometers are part of the metric system. Most of the world and the science community uses the metric system. In the U.S. we use the customary or imperial system. Some examples of these units for length are inches, feet, and yards. It is important to be able to convert between the two systems. For example, we can convert centimeters to inches, and we can convert meters into feet. To do this we need to know the conversion factor.

Use what you know about conversions and the above images to help you convert the measurements from your challenges.
