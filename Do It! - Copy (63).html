Do It!

Your challenge is to build a “Smart Spinner” robot to spin a top, and then turn off the motor once the top is released.

Once you figure that out, try building the “smartest” spinner: see if you can get your top to spin the fastest and longest.

STEPS:

  1. Learn about gears by completing gear tutorials.
  2. Build and test a spinning top. Complete the data tables.
  3. Make your Smart Spinner "smarter".

Time to Explore Gears

Your first task is to complete the gear tutorials (#2, 3, 4, 5). The motor base is the same for each of these, so do not take it apart until you finish #5.

Steps on getting started with Lego WeDo Software:

  1. Open WeDo under “All Programs”.
  2. Click the activity tab in the top left corner of your screen. 
  3. Click the top left set of gears to start tutorial 2.
  4. To get to the next tutorial, click the gear at the top left of the screen.

Smart Spinner Challenge

Now that you have had a chance to build and test different gears, you are ready for the Smart Spinner Challenge! Your first task is to build the spinner using different combinations of gears, and find out which combinations spin faster and longer, and which spin slower and for a shorter length of time. Be sure to fill out the Activity Data Table as you go.

Make a Smart Spinner "Smarter"

Now that you have built and tested your Smart Spinner, your next objective is to figure out how to make it spin for the longest time possible!

Will you make changes to the program? 

Will you make changes to the  mechanics of the top?

Will you make changes to the mechanics of the spinner?

Write your plan and reasons for your choice in your ePortfolio.

After you have completed your modifications, take a photo or video of your spinner and a screenshot of your program for your presentation.
