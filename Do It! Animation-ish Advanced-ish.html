Do It! Animation-ish: Pattern Animation

 The Challenge

Your challenge is to create an advanced animation with Advanced-ish that follows a pattern.

Use Advanced-ish to add foreground and background to your animation. Also, use the Grabber and Transformer tools to move your drawings around the frame.

 Project Steps

  1. Plan Your Pattern 

  2. Review the Lessons

  3. Create Your Pattern

Plan Your Pattern 

Plan your pattern animation, think about how you can incorporate shapes or numbers to create a pattern. Will you create a repeating shape pattern or a number that follows a growing pattern? Consider the following as you plan your design:

  1. What image will repeat or grow? What can you add to your pattern animation that will stay the same?
  2. Will you add additional shapes, characters, or scenery?
  3. How will you share the pattern rule with your audience? 

Review the Lessons

  1. Start the Animation-ish software. If you don't have the program installed on your computer, go to the web version and use your username and class code provided by your facilitator to sign into Animation-ish.
  2. Go to: FableVision Learning to watch the lessons.
  3. Watch Lessons 3, 4, and 5.
    Lesson 3: Advanced-ish (Part 1)
    Lesson 4: Advanced-ish (Part 2)
    Lesson 5: Advanced-ish (Part 3)
  4. Go back to the Animation-ish application and select Advanced-ish. The workspace will open.
  5. Use what you learned in the lessons to make the animation pattern you planned with Advanced-ish.

Create Your Pattern

Use the tools in Advanced-ish to create your pattern. When you create your animation, think about how you can incorporate a pattern. A growing pattern or repeating pattern will be the easiest to use. What image will repeat or grow?  After you have completed your pattern take a screenshot of your pattern and write your your pattern rule.
